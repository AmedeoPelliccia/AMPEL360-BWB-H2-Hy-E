# ATA 13 - Hardware and General Tools

## Overview
This chapter contains catalogs and specifications for standard hardware, consumables, and general tools used in the maintenance and operation of the AMPEL360 aircraft.

## Structure

### 01-HARDWARE_CATALOGS
Catalogs of standard hardware (fasteners, fittings, etc.)

### 02-CONSUMABLES_CATALOG
Catalog of consumable materials and supplies

### 03-GENERAL_TOOLS_CATALOG
Catalog of general-purpose tools

### 04-INVENTORY_AND_CONTROL
Inventory management and control procedures

### 05-SAFETY_DATA
Safety data sheets and handling procedures

## Governance
All hardware and tools must meet aerospace standards and be approved for use with composite structures and hydrogen systems where applicable.
