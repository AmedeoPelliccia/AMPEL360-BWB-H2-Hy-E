Standards for wind turbine installations at airport facilities.

## System Specifications

### Turbine Selection

- **Size**: 100 kW - 2 MW rated power
- **Hub Height**: 50-100m (subject to aviation clearance)
- **Rotor Diameter**: 20-80m
- **Standards**: [IEC 61400](https://www.iec.ch/)

### Site Requirements

- Minimum average wind speed: 5 m/s at hub height
- Aviation safety clearances per local regulations
- Noise impact assessment: <45 dB(A) at nearest residence
- Environmental impact assessment

- SCADA monitoring and remote control
