# 85-80-02-001 Solar PV Systems

## Purpose

Establishes standards for solar photovoltaic (PV) systems integrated into airport infrastructure.

## System Design

### PV Array Configuration

- **Module Type**: Monocrystalline or polycrystalline silicon
- **Efficiency**: Minimum 18% at STC
- **Power Rating**: 350-550 Wp per module
- **Standards**: [IEC 61215](https://www.iec.ch/), [IEC 61730](https://www.iec.ch/)

### Mounting Systems

- **Rooftop**: Ballasted or mechanically attached
- **Ground-Mounted**: Fixed tilt or single-axis tracking
- **Carport**: Elevated structures over parking areas
- **Standards**: Local structural codes, wind load analysis

### Inverters

- **String Inverters**: 10-100 kW for distributed arrays
- **Central Inverters**: 500 kW - 2 MW for large installations
- **Efficiency**: Minimum 98% European weighted
- **Standards**: [IEC 62109](https://www.iec.ch/), [IEEE 1547](https://standards.ieee.org/)

## Grid Integration

- Interconnection at 11kV or 400V level
- Anti-islanding protection per [IEEE 1547](https://standards.ieee.org/)
- Power quality: THD <5%, PF >0.95
- SCADA integration for monitoring and control

## Standards

- [IEC 61215](https://www.iec.ch/) - PV module qualification
- [IEC 62109](https://www.iec.ch/) - Power converter safety
- [IEEE 1547](https://standards.ieee.org/) - DER interconnection
