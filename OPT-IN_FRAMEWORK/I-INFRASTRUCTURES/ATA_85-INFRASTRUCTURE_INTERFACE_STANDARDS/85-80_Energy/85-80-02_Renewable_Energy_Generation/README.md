# 85-80-02 Renewable Energy Generation

## Purpose

This subsection defines standards and specifications for renewable energy generation systems integrated into airport infrastructure supporting AMPEL360 BWB-H2-Hy-E aircraft operations.

## Scope

Covers renewable energy generation technologies and their integration:

- Solar photovoltaic (PV) systems
- Wind turbine installations
- Green hydrogen (H2) production via electrolysis
- Energy forecasting and optimization
- Grid integration and power quality

## Applicable Standards and Regulations

- [IEC 61215](https://www.iec.ch/) - Terrestrial photovoltaic (PV) modules - Design qualification and type approval
- [IEC 61400](https://www.iec.ch/) - Wind turbine generator systems
- [IEEE 1547](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Standard for Interconnection and Interoperability of Distributed Energy Resources
- [IEC 62282](https://www.iec.ch/) - Fuel cell technologies (for H2 electrolyzers reverse compatibility)
- [IEC 62109](https://www.iec.ch/) - Safety of power converters for use in photovoltaic power systems
- [EU Renewable Energy Directive](https://energy.ec.europa.eu/topics/renewable-energy/renewable-energy-directive-targets-and-rules_en)

## Key Design Considerations

### Solar PV Systems

- Rooftop and ground-mounted arrays
- Optimal tilt and orientation for location
- String inverters vs. central inverters
- Monitoring and performance tracking

### Wind Turbines

- Site assessment and wind resource evaluation
- Turbine selection and foundation design
- Noise and visual impact considerations
- Aviation safety clearances

### Green H2 Production

- PEM or alkaline electrolyzer technology
- Integration with renewable energy sources
- H2 compression and storage
- Safety systems and hazardous area classification

## Environmental and Sustainability

- Carbon footprint reduction targets
- Lifecycle analysis and circularity
- Land use and biodiversity considerations
- Community engagement and social license
