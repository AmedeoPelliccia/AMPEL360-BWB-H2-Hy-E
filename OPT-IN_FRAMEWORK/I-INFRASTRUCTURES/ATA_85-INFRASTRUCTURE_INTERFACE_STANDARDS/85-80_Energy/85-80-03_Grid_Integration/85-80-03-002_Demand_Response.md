## Purpose

Standards for participating in utility demand response programs.

## Program Types

### Peak Load Reduction

- Curtailment of non-essential loads during peak periods
- Advance notice: 2-4 hours
- Duration: 2-6 hours
- Incentive: Capacity payment + energy credit

### Frequency Regulation

- Fast response load adjustment (<1 second)
- Automatic generation control (AGC) signal following
- Communication via IEC 61850 or OpenADR

### Load Shifting

- Time-of-use optimization
- Pre-cooling/pre-heating of thermal masses
- Battery charging during off-peak periods

## Implementation

- Integration with energy management system (see [85-80-06](../85-80-06_Energy_Management_Systems/README.md))
- Automated load control and monitoring
- Performance verification and settlement
