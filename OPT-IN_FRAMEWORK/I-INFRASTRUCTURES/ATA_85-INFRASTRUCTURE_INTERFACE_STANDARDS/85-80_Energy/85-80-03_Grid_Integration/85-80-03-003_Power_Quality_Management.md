## Purpose

Standards for maintaining power quality in airport electrical systems.

## Power Quality Parameters

### Voltage Quality

- Steady-state voltage: ±5% of nominal
- Voltage sags: <10% below nominal for <1 second
- Voltage swells: <10% above nominal for <1 second
- Voltage unbalance: <2%

### Harmonic Distortion

- Total harmonic distortion (THD): <5% voltage, <8% current
- Individual harmonic limits per [IEEE 519](https://standards.ieee.org/)
- Mitigation: Passive/active filters, reactor chokes

### Flicker

- Short-term flicker severity (Pst): <1.0
- Long-term flicker severity (Plt): <0.8
- Standards: [IEC 61000-4-15](https://www.iec.ch/)

## Monitoring

- Continuous power quality monitoring at key nodes
- Event recording and analysis
- Integration with SCADA system

## Standards

- [IEEE 519](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Harmonic control
- [IEC 61000-4-30](https://www.iec.ch/) - Power quality measurement
- [IEEE 1159](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Monitoring electric power quality
