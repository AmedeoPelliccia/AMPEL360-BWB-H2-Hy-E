# 85-80-03 Grid Integration

## Purpose

Defines standards for integrating airport energy systems with the utility grid, ensuring reliable, efficient, and compliant interconnection.

## Scope

- Utility interconnection agreements and technical requirements
- Demand response programs and load management
- Power quality management and harmonic mitigation
- Grid services (frequency regulation, voltage support, reactive power)
- Islanding protection and anti-islanding requirements

## Applicable Standards

- [IEEE 1547](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Interconnection and Interoperability of DER
- [IEC 61727](https://www.iec.ch/) - Photovoltaic (PV) systems - Characteristics of the utility interface
- [IEEE 519](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Harmonic Control in Electrical Power Systems
- [IEC 61000](https://www.iec.ch/) - Electromagnetic compatibility (EMC)
