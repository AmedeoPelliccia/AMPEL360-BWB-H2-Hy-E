# 85-80-04 Energy Storage Systems

## Purpose

Establishes standards for energy storage systems integrated into airport infrastructure for peak shaving, renewable firming, and backup power.

## Scope

- Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS)
- Flywheel energy storage
- Thermal energy storage
- Control strategies and optimization

## Applicable Standards

- [IEC 62933](https://www.iec.ch/) - Electrical energy storage (EES) systems
- [IEEE 1547](https://standards.ieee.org/) - DER interconnection (storage systems)
- [IEC 62619](https://www.iec.ch/) - Secondary cells and batteries - Safety for lithium systems
- [NFPA 855](https://www.nfpa.org/codes-and-standards/all-codes-and-standards/list-of-codes-and-standards/detail?code=855) - Standard for Installation of ESS
