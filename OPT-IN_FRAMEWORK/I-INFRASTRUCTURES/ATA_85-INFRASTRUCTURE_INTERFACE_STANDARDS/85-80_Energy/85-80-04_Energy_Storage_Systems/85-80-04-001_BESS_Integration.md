## Purpose

Standards for integrating battery energy storage systems (BESS) into airport infrastructure.

## System Specifications

### Battery Technology

- **Lithium-ion**: High energy density, fast response
- **Capacity**: 1-10 MWh per installation
- **Power Rating**: 500 kW - 5 MW per inverter
- **Cycle Life**: Minimum 5,000 cycles at 80% DOD
- **Standards**: [IEC 62619](https://www.iec.ch/), [UL 1973](https://www.ul.com/)

### Power Conversion System (PCS)

- Bidirectional inverters for charge/discharge
- Efficiency: >98% at rated power
- Response time: <100 ms
- Grid-forming capability for islanded operation

## Safety Systems

- Fire detection and suppression per [NFPA 855](https://www.nfpa.org/codes-and-standards/all-codes-and-standards/list-of-codes-and-standards/detail?code=855)
- Thermal runaway prevention and mitigation
- Explosion venting and gas detection
- Emergency shutdown systems

## Applications

- Peak shaving and demand charge reduction
- Renewable energy firming and smoothing
- Frequency regulation and grid services
- Backup power for critical loads

## Standards

- [IEC 62933](https://www.iec.ch/) - Electrical energy storage systems
- [NFPA 855](https://www.nfpa.org/codes-and-standards/all-codes-and-standards/list-of-codes-and-standards/detail?code=855) - ESS installation standard
- [IEEE 1547](https://standards.ieee.org/) - DER interconnection
