## Purpose

Standards for 400Hz electrical power supply to aircraft during ground operations.

## Technical Specifications

### Electrical Characteristics

- **Frequency**: 400 Hz ±0.5 Hz
- **Voltage**: 115/200 VAC three-phase (per [MIL-STD-704](https://quicksearch.dla.mil/))
- **Power Rating**: 90 kVA, 180 kVA typical
- **Regulation**: ±2% voltage, ±0.5 Hz frequency

### GPU Types

#### Fixed GPU

- Installed at gate positions
- Underground cable feed from LV distribution
- Solid-state frequency converter
- Remote monitoring and control

#### Mobile GPU

- Trailer-mounted units
- Diesel or electric powered
- Stored in service area when not in use

## Safety Features

- Ground fault protection
- Overcurrent protection
- Emergency stop circuits
- Cable integrity monitoring
