# 85-80-07-002 Backup Generators

## Purpose

Standards for diesel/gas backup generators for extended outages.

## Generator Specifications

- **Prime Power Rating**: Continuous rating for essential loads
- **Standby Rating**: Short-term emergency rating (80% duty cycle)
- **Fuel**: Diesel, natural gas, or bi-fuel capability
- **Start Time**: <10 seconds from signal to rated power
- **Standards**: [ISO 8528](https://www.iso.org/)

## Fuel Storage

- **Diesel**: 24-72 hour capacity at full load
- **Storage Tanks**: Double-wall or secondary containment
- **Fuel Quality**: Regular testing and polishing
- **Refueling**: Contracted service with guaranteed delivery

## Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS)

- **Type**: Open or closed transition
- **Transfer Time**: <10 seconds typical
- **Bypass**: Manual bypass for maintenance
- **Standards**: [IEC 60947-6-1](https://www.iec.ch/)

## Maintenance

- Weekly no-load exercise test (30 minutes)
- Monthly load test (30-50% load)
- Annual full-load test
- Service per manufacturer schedule (250-500 hour intervals)

## Standards

- [ISO 8528](https://www.iso.org/) - Reciprocating internal combustion engine driven generating sets
- [NFPA 110](https://www.nfpa.org/codes-and-standards/all-codes-and-standards/list-of-codes-and-standards/detail?code=110) - Emergency and standby power systems
