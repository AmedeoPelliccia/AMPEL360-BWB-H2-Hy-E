# 85-80-07 Emergency Power

## Purpose

Defines standards for emergency and backup power systems ensuring continuity of critical airport operations.

## Scope

- Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems
- Backup diesel/gas generators
- Critical load management and prioritization
- Automatic transfer switches and load shedding

## Applicable Standards

- [IEC 62040](https://www.iec.ch/) - Uninterruptible power systems (UPS)
- [ISO 8528](https://www.iso.org/) - Reciprocating internal combustion engine driven alternating current generating sets
- [NFPA 110](https://www.nfpa.org/codes-and-standards/all-codes-and-standards/list-of-codes-and-standards/detail?code=110) - Emergency and Standby Power Systems
- [IEEE 446 (Orange Book)](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Emergency and Standby Power Systems
