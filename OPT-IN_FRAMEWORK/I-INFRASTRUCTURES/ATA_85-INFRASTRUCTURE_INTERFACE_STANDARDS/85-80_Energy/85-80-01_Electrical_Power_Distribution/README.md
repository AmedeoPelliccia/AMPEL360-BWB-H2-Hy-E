# 85-80-01 Electrical Power Distribution

## Purpose

This subsection defines the standards and specifications for electrical power distribution infrastructure at airport facilities supporting AMPEL360 BWB-H2-Hy-E aircraft operations.

## Scope

Covers electrical power distribution systems from high-voltage primary distribution (33kV) down to low-voltage utilization (400V), including:

- Primary distribution networks (33kV)
- Secondary distribution networks (11kV)
- Low-voltage distribution systems (400V/230V)
- Substation design and specifications
- Cable routing and installation standards
- Protection and control systems
- Load management and monitoring

## Applicable Standards and Regulations

- [IEC 60364](https://www.iec.ch/) - Low-voltage electrical installations
- [IEEE 141 (Red Book)](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Electrical Power Distribution for Industrial Plants
- [IEC 61936-1](https://www.iec.ch/) - Power installations exceeding 1 kV AC
- [BS 7671](https://electrical.theiet.org/) - Requirements for Electrical Installations (UK Wiring Regulations)
- [NEC (NFPA 70)](https://www.nfpa.org/codes-and-standards/all-codes-and-standards/list-of-codes-and-standards/detail?code=70) - National Electrical Code
- Local airport authority electrical standards
