# 85-80-06 Energy Management Systems

## Purpose

Establishes standards for SCADA, energy management, and optimization systems controlling airport energy infrastructure.

## Scope

- SCADA architecture and communication protocols
- Load forecasting and demand prediction
- Optimization algorithms for energy dispatch
- Real-time pricing and cost optimization

## Applicable Standards

- [IEC 61850](https://www.iec.ch/) - Communication networks for power utility automation
- [IEC 62351](https://www.iec.ch/) - Power systems management and information exchange - Data and communications security
- [ISO 50001](https://www.iso.org/) - Energy management systems
- [IEEE 2030](https://standards.ieee.org/) - Guide for Smart Grid Interoperability
