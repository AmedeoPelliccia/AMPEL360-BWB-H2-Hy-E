## 6. Advantages of H₂ Fuel Cell GSE

### 6.1 Operational Benefits

- **Fast Refueling**: 5-10 minutes vs. 1-2 hours for battery charging
- **Long Range**: 200-400 km per refueling vs. 150-200 km for battery-electric
- **Consistent Performance**: No degradation with SOC or temperature
- **High Power Density**: Suitable for heavy-duty applications
- **Zero Emissions**: Only water vapor and heat at point of use

### 6.2 Strategic Benefits

- **Hydrogen Ecosystem Alignment**: Synergy with aircraft H₂ refueling infrastructure
- **Energy Independence**: Can be produced on-site via electrolysis
- **Grid Independence**: No dependency on electrical grid capacity
- **Renewable Integration**: Green H₂ from renewable electricity
