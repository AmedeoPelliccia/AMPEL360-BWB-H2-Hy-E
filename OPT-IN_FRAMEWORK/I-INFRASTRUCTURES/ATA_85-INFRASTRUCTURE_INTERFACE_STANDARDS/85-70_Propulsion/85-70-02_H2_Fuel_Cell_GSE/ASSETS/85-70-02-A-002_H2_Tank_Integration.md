## 1. Purpose

This document provides detailed specifications for hydrogen storage tank integration on fuel cell-powered Ground Support Equipment.

---

## 2. Tank Types and Specifications

### 2.1 Type IV Composite Cylinders (Preferred)

- **Construction**: Carbon fiber composite overwrap with plastic liner
- **Pressure Rating**: 700 bar (70 MPa)
- **Weight**: 40-60% lighter than Type III
- **Service Life**: 15-20 years or 10,000 refueling cycles
- **Standards**: [ISO 19881](https://www.iso.org/standard/66787.html), [UN ECE R134](https://unece.org/transport/standards/transport/vehicle-regulations-wp29/standards/addenda-1958-agreement-regulations-121)

### 2.2 Tank Configuration

- **Number of Tanks**: 2-6 cylinders (depending on vehicle size)
- **Individual Capacity**: 8-12 kg H₂ per cylinder
- **Total Capacity**: 40-80 kg H₂ (for tugs and heavy equipment)
- **Mounting**: Under-chassis or behind-cab mounting
- **Protection**: Crash-resistant mounting with sacrificial structures

---

## 3. Safety Systems

### 3.1 Pressure Relief

- **PRD (Pressure Relief Device)**: TPRD (Thermally-activated) at 110°C
- **Vent Piping**: Directed away from vehicle and operators
- **Burst Disc**: Secondary overpressure protection

### 3.2 Leak Detection

- **H₂ Sensors**: Multiple sensors in tank compartment
- **Detection Threshold**: 1% H₂ by volume (25% LEL)
- **Response**: Automatic ventilation and system shutdown
