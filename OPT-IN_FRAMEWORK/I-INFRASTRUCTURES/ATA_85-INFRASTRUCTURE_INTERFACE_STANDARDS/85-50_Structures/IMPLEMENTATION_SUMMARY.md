- **Aviation**: ICAO Annex 14, FAA AC 150/5300-13B, CS-ADR-DSN

- **Sustainability**: ISO 14040/14044, ISO 14067, ISO 20887

### 4. BWB-Specific Adaptations

Special consideration for BWB aircraft:
- **Wide fuselage geometry**: Multi-door boarding systems
- **Large wing span**: Hangar and apron clearances
- **Low ground clearance**: GSE access considerations
- **Distributed loads**: Pavement design for BWB landing gear

### 5. H₂ Safety Framework

Comprehensive H₂ safety coverage:
- **Safety distances** per NFPA 2 and ISO 19880-1
- **Blast-resistant design** for worst-case explosion scenarios
- **Fire protection systems** (deluge, passive barriers)
- **Leak detection** and ventilation requirements
- **Cryogenic material selection** (9% Ni steel, austenitic SS, Al alloys)
- **Emergency response infrastructure** (shelters, muster points)

### 6. Sustainability and Circularity

Low-carbon design principles:
- **Embodied carbon targets**: <0.3 tCO₂e/m² (50% reduction vs. baseline)
- **Recycled content**: ≥90% for steel, ≥30% for concrete aggregates
- **Design for disassembly**: Bolted connections, modular components
- **Material passports**: Digital tracking via ATA 95
- **End-of-life recovery**: >90% recyclability target
- **Circular economy**: Integration with ATA 99 tracking
