# 85-00-05-303: CO2 Venting and Safety Interface

## 1. Introduction
This document defines the safety interfaces related to CO2 venting and leak management.

## 2. Venting Interface
*   **Location:** Away from personnel access points and engine intakes.
*   **Discharge:** Diffused to prevent dry ice formation or asphyxiation hazard.

## 3. Leak Detection
*   **Sensors:** Interface to ground monitoring system.
*   **Alarm:** Visual and audible alarm at the service panel.

## 4. Requirements
*   **REQ-C-201:** Venting shall not occur in enclosed hangars without extraction.
*   **REQ-C-202:** Service personnel must wear CO2 monitors.
