# 85-00-05-702: Ground Power Unit Standards Interface

## 1. Introduction
This document specifies the electrical standards for Ground Power Units (GPUs) servicing the BWB.

## 2. Electrical Specification
*   **Output 1:** 115/200V AC, 400Hz, 3-Phase.
*   **Output 2:** 270V DC (for high voltage loads).
*   **Power Quality:** Per [ISO 6858](https://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#iso:std:iso:6858:ed-2:v1:en) and [MIL-STD-704](https://quicksearch.dla.mil/qsDocDetails.aspx?ident_number=36041).

## 3. Requirements
*   **REQ-E-101:** GPU voltage regulation shall be within +/- [Value]%.
*   **REQ-E-102:** Protection against over/under voltage and frequency is mandatory.
