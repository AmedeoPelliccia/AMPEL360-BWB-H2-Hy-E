# 85-00-05-401: GSE Access and Clearance Interface

## 1. Introduction
This document defines the access routes and clearance zones for Ground Support Equipment (GSE) around the BWB aircraft.

## 2. Access Zones
*   **Under-Wing:** Height restrictions due to BWB geometry.
*   **Fuselage:** Access to cargo doors and service panels.
*   **Nose:** Towbar attachment access.

## 3. Requirements
*   **REQ-G-001:** All GSE operating under the wing shall have collision avoidance sensors.
*   **REQ-G-002:** Service roads shall be marked to ensure separation from H2 zones.
