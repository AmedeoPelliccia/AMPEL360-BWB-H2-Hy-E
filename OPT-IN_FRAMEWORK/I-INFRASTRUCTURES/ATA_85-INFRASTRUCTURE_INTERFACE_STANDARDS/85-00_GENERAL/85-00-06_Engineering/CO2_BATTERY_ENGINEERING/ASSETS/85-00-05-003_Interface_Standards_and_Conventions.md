# 85-00-05-003: Interface Standards and Conventions

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the standards and conventions used for all interface documentation and design within the AMPEL360 project.

## 2. Applicable Standards
The following international standards are referenced:

### 2.1. General Aviation
*   **[IATA AHM](https://www.iata.org/en/publications/manuals/airport-handling-manual/):** Airport Handling Manual
*   **[ICAO Annex 14](https://www.icao.int/safety/airnavigation/aerodromes/pages/annex-14.aspx):** Aerodromes

### 2.2. Hydrogen & Cryogenics
*   **[ISO 19880](https://www.iso.org/search.html?q=ISO+19880):** Gaseous hydrogen fueling stations
*   **[SAE AS6679](https://www.sae.org/standards/content/as6679/):** Liquid Hydrogen Storage and Handling in Aerospace

### 2.3. Electrical
*   **[ISO 6858](https://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#iso:std:iso:6858:ed-2:v1:en):** Aircraft ground support electrical supplies
*   **[IEC 61851](https://www.iec.ch/search?q=IEC%2061851):** Electric vehicle conductive charging system

### 2.4. Digital
*   **[AIDX](https://www.iata.org/en/programs/passenger/aidx/):** Aviation Information Data Exchange
*   **[ACRIS](https://aci.aero/about-aci/priorities/airport-it/acris/):** Airport Community Recommended Information Services

## 3. Naming Conventions
*   **Interface IDs:** `85-00-05-XXX` (where XXX is the unique identifier).
*   **Asset IDs:** `85-00-05-A-XXX` (for drawings, models, diagrams).
*   **Coordinate System:** Aircraft coordinates (STA, BL, WL) per ATA 06.

## 4. Units of Measurement
*   **Primary:** SI Units (Metric)
*   **Secondary:** Imperial Units (where required by specific regional standards)
