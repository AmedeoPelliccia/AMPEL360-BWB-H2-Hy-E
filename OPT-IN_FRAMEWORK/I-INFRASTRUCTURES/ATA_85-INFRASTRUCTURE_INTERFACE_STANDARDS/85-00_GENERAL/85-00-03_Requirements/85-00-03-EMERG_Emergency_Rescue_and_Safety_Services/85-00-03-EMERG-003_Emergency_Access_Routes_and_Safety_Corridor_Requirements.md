# 85-00-03-EMERG-003 — Emergency Access Routes and Safety Corridor Requirements

## 1. Requirement Statement

Emergency access routes and safety corridors **SHALL** be maintained around aircraft parking stands:
- **Access Routes**: Minimum 6m wide all-weather roads to all stand sides
- **Safety Corridors**: 15m clear zone around aircraft during H₂ refuelling
- **Turning Radii**: Emergency vehicle turning radii accommodated (minimum 15m radius)
- **Lighting**: Emergency lighting operational 24/7

## 2. Rationale

BWB configuration and H₂/CO₂ battery systems require enhanced emergency access routes and safety corridors to support rapid emergency response and personnel evacuation.
