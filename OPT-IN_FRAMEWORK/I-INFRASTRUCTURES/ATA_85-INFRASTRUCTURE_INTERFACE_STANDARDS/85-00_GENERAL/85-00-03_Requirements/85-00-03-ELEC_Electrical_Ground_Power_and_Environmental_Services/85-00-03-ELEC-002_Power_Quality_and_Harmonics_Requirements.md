# 85-00-03-ELEC-002 — Power Quality and Harmonics Requirements

## 1. Requirement Statement

Ground power **SHALL** meet the following quality standards:
- **Voltage Regulation**: ±5% of nominal voltage
- **Frequency Stability**: ±5 Hz of nominal frequency (400 Hz)
- **Total Harmonic Distortion (THD)**: < 5%
- **Power Factor**: > 0.85

## 2. Rationale

Power quality ensures safe and reliable operation of sensitive avionics and systems without damage or degradation.
