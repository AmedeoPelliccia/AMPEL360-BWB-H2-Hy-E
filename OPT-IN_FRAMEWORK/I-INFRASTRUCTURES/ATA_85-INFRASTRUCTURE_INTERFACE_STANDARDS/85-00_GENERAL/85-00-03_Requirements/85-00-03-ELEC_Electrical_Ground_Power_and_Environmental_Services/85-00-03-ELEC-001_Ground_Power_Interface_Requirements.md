# 85-00-03-ELEC-001 — Ground Power Interface Requirements

## 1. Requirement Statement

The aircraft **SHALL** accept external ground power via standard 400 Hz AC interface with:
- **Voltage**: 115/200V AC, 3-phase, 400 Hz ±5 Hz
- **Power**: Minimum 90 kVA capacity
- **Connector**: Standard NATO/MS connector per MIL-STD-2050

## 2. Rationale

Standard ground power enables aircraft systems operation during turnaround without draining onboard batteries, supporting maintenance, passenger comfort, and system checks.

## 3. Category

- **Requirement Type**: Interface — Electrical
- **Domain**: Electrical Ground Power

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] 400 Hz AC interface operational
- [ ] Power capacity: 90 kVA minimum
- [ ] Compatible with standard ground power units (GPUs)
