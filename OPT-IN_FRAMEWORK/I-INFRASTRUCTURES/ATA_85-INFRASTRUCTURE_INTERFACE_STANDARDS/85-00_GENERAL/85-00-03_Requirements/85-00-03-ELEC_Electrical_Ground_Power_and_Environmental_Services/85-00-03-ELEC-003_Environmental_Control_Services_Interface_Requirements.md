# 85-00-03-ELEC-003 — Environmental Control Services Interface Requirements

## 1. Requirement Statement

The aircraft **SHALL** accept preconditioned air (PCA) for cabin environmental control via standard interface:
- **Air Flow**: 2.5 kg/s (150 kg/min) minimum
- **Temperature**: Adjustable from 18°C to 30°C
- **Pressure**: Cabin pressure equivalent (sea level to 2,400 m altitude)

## 2. Rationale

Preconditioned air maintains passenger comfort during turnaround without operating aircraft environmental control system (ECS), reducing energy consumption and wear.

## 3. Category

- **Requirement Type**: Interface — Environmental Services
- **Domain**: Environmental Control

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] PCA interface compatible with standard ground units
- [ ] Air flow capacity: 2.5 kg/s minimum
- [ ] Temperature range: 18-30°C adjustable
- [ ] Pressure regulation capability

## 6. Verification Method

- **Method**: Test + Demonstration
- **Evidence**: PCA interface test, flow/temperature/pressure validation, passenger comfort assessment
