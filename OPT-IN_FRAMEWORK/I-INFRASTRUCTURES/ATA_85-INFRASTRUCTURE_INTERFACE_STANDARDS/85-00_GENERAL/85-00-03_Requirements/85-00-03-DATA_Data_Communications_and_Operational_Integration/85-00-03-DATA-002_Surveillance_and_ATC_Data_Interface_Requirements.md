# 85-00-03-DATA-002 — Surveillance and ATC Data Interface Requirements

## 1. Requirement Statement

Aircraft **SHALL** support standard surveillance and ATC data interfaces:
- **ADS-B Out**: Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast per [DO-260B](https://www.rtca.org/content/standards-documents)
- **Mode S**: Extended Squitter (Mode S EHS) per ICAO standards
- **Transponder**: Mode A/C/S transponder operational

## 2. Rationale

Surveillance data interfaces ensure safe integration into controlled airspace and support NextGen/SESAR air traffic management.
