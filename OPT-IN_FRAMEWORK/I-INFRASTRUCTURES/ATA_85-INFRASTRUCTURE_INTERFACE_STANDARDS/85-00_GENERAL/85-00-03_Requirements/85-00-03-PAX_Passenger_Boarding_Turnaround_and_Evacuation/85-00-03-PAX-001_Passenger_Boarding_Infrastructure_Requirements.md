# 85-00-03-PAX-001 — Passenger Boarding Infrastructure Requirements

## 1. Requirement Statement

Passenger boarding bridges **SHALL** be compatible with BWB door configuration:
- **Door Heights**: 2.5m - 4.0m above ground level
- **Door Locations**: Forward and mid-fuselage positions (BWB-specific)
- **Bridge Angle**: Adjustable 0° to 15° elevation, ±10° lateral
- **Capacity**: 2 simultaneous boarding bridges (220 passengers, target boarding time 15 minutes)

## 2. Rationale

BWB configuration requires boarding bridge compatibility at non-standard door locations to enable efficient passenger boarding and deplaning.
