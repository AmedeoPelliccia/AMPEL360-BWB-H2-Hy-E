# 85-00-03-PAX-003 — Evacuation Routes and Ground Rescue Access Requirements

## 1. Requirement Statement

Emergency evacuation infrastructure **SHALL** support:
- **Evacuation Slides**: Deployment clearances for all emergency exits per [CS-25.810](https://www.easa.europa.eu/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27)
- **Ground Rescue Access**: Clear routes for rescue vehicles within 3 minutes of alarm
- **90-Second Evacuation**: Infrastructure compatible with 90-second evacuation requirement
- **Accessibility**: Rescue access to all aircraft sides

## 2. Rationale

BWB configuration introduces unique evacuation challenges requiring airport infrastructure to support rapid emergency egress and rescue access.
