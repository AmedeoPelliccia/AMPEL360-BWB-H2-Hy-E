# ATA 85-00-07: Verification & Validation

## 1. Mission
The 85-00-07 layer consolidates the verification and validation (V&V) strategy, test plans, and evidence for all infrastructure interfaces defined in [ATA Chapter 85](../../README.md), ensuring compliance with airworthiness requirements and operational standards.

## 2. Structure
*   **[General V&V Documents](./)** – Overview documents 001-005
*   **[MASTER](./MASTER/README.md)** – Master V&V matrix, test levels, and regression strategy
*   **[AIRPORT_INTERFACE_VV](./AIRPORT_INTERFACE_VV/README.md)** – Airport clearance, compatibility, and apron operations testing
*   **[H2_INFRASTRUCTURE_VV](./H2_INFRASTRUCTURE_VV/README.md)** – Hydrogen refueling safety, flow, and capacity validation
*   **[CO2_BATTERY_VV](./CO2_BATTERY_VV/README.md)** – CO2 battery pressure, venting, and logistics testing
*   **[GROUND_SERVICES_VV](./GROUND_SERVICES_VV/README.md)** – GSE access, turnaround time, and workflow validation
*   **[PAX_BOARDING_EVAC_VV](./PAX_BOARDING_EVAC_VV/README.md)** – Passenger boarding and evacuation demonstration
*   **[DIGITAL_INFRASTRUCTURE_VV](./DIGITAL_INFRASTRUCTURE_VV/README.md)** – Data connectivity, latency, and cybersecurity testing
*   **[ENERGY_INFRASTRUCTURE_VV](./ENERGY_INFRASTRUCTURE_VV/README.md)** – Ground power, grid integration, and renewable validation

## 4. Governance
V&V activities follow [SAE ARP4754A](https://www.sae.org/standards/content/arp4754a/) development assurance guidance and trace to certification requirements per [CS-25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes), [DO-178C](https://www.rtca.org/content/standards-guidance-materials), and [DO-254](https://www.rtca.org/content/standards-guidance-materials).
