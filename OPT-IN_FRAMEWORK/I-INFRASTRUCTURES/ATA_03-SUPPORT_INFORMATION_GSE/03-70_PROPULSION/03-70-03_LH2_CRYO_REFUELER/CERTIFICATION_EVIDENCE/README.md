## Applicable Regulations

### International Standards
- **ISO 14687** - Hydrogen fuel quality
- **ISO 13984** - Liquid hydrogen - Land vehicle fueling system interface
- **ISO 17268** - Gaseous hydrogen land vehicle refueling connection devices

### European Standards
- **EN 1473** - Installation and equipment for LNG
- **PED 2014/68/EU** - Pressure Equipment Directive
- **ATEX 2014/34/EU** - Equipment in explosive atmospheres

### Aviation Standards
- **EASA Part 145** - Maintenance Organization Requirements
- **ICAO Annex 14** - Aerodromes
- National aviation authority requirements

### Safety Codes
- **NFPA 2** - Hydrogen Technologies Code
- **NFPA 55** - Compressed Gases and Cryogenic Fluids Code

## Certification Process

1. **Design Review**: Regulatory authority review of design
2. **Testing**: Verification testing per applicable standards
3. **Documentation**: Compilation of evidence
4. **Inspection**: On-site inspection by authorities
5. **Approval**: Issuance of type certificate or approval
6. **Ongoing Compliance**: Periodic audits and reporting
