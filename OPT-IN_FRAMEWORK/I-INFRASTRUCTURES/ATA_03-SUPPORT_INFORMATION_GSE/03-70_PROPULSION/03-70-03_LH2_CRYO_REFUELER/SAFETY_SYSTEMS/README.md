This folder should contain:
- Hazard analysis (HAZOP, FMEA)
- Safety requirements
- Emergency shutdown systems (ESD)
- Hydrogen leak detection systems
- Fire and explosion protection
- Personnel protection equipment (PPE) requirements
- Exclusion zone definitions
- Grounding and bonding specifications
- Venting and relief systems
- Safety interlocks
- Emergency response procedures
- Training requirements

---

## Safety Principles

- Multiple layers of protection (inherent, passive, active)
- Fail-safe design philosophy
- Automated emergency response
- Continuous monitoring
- Clear operator procedures
- Regular safety audits

---

## Key Hazards

- Hydrogen leak and ignition
- Cryogenic burns
- Asphyxiation (oxygen displacement)
- Overpressure
- Material embrittlement
- Static electricity
