# 03-20 - SYSTEMS-GENERAL

**ATA Chapter:** 03 - Support Information GSE  
**Origin Block:** 20 - Functional Systems  
**Status:** Active

---

## Purpose

This directory contains the functional systems of the ATA 03 domain for Ground Support Equipment information management and operational support.

---

## Structure

Unlike the GENERAL layer, concrete systems under this origin block are structured according to how they are:
- Conceived
- Designed
- Implemented
- Evolved in time

Each subsystem `03-20-YY_*` has its own internal design-driven structure that best fits the system's lifecycle.
