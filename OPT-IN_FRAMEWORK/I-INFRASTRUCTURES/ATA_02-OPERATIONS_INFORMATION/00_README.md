# ATA 02 - Operations Information

## Overview
This chapter contains all operational information required by flight crews and ground personnel for the safe and efficient operation of the AMPEL360 aircraft. It includes flight manuals, performance data, and operational procedures.

## Structure

### 01-FCOM-FLIGHT_CREW_OPERATING_MANUAL
Flight Crew Operating Manual containing:
- 01-LIMITATIONS: Operating limitations and restrictions
- 02-NORMAL_PROCEDURES: Standard operating procedures
- 03-NON-NORMAL_PROCEDURES: Emergency and abnormal procedures
- 04-SYSTEM_DESCRIPTIONS: Aircraft systems descriptions

### 02-QRH-QUICK_REFERENCE_HANDBOOK
Quick Reference Handbook for in-flight emergency procedures

### 03-FPERF-PERFORMANCE
Aircraft performance data and calculations

### 04-WBM-WEIGHT_AND_BALANCE
Weight and balance manual and procedures

### 05-MEL-MINIMUM_EQUIPMENT_LIST
Minimum Equipment List defining dispatch requirements

### 06-GROUND_OPS-GROUND_OPERATIONS_MANUAL
Ground operations procedures and guidelines

## Governance
All documents in this chapter are subject to regulatory approval and must maintain traceability to certification requirements.
