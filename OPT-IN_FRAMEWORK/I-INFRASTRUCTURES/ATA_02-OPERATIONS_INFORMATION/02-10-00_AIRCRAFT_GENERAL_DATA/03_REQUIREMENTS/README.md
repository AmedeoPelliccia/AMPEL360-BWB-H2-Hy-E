## Requirements Categories

### RQ-GENERAL
General aircraft identification, type certificate data, configuration control, and documentation requirements.

### RQ-DIMENSIONS
Physical dimensions including overall dimensions, ground clearances, airport compatibility, and BWB-specific geometry specifications.

### RQ-WEIGHTS
Weight specifications including MTOW, MLW, MZFW, OEW, and critical center of gravity envelope for BWB configuration.

### RQ-CAPACITIES
Capacity specifications for passengers, cargo, hydrogen fuel storage, and crew complement.

### RQ-PERFORMANCE
Performance requirements including design range, cruise speed, service ceiling, takeoff and landing distances.

### RQ-SYSTEMS
System specifications for propulsion, hydrogen fuel system, CAOS (Computer Aided Operations System) integration, and environmental systems.

### RQ-OPERATIONAL
Operational requirements including crew requirements, certification basis, operational limitations, and dispatch requirements.

## Key Documents

### Requirements_Master_List.csv
Master list of all requirements with ID, category, title, priority, status, source, and verification method.

### Requirements_Traceability_Matrix.csv
Traceability matrix linking requirements to design elements, test cases, and compliance documents.

### Requirements_Verification_Matrix.csv
Verification matrix detailing how each requirement will be verified (analysis, test, inspection, demonstration).

## Compliance Standards

- **CS-25**: EASA Certification Specifications for Large Aeroplanes
- **FAA Part 25**: Federal Aviation Regulations
- **ATA iSpec 2200**: Aviation industry specifications
- **S1000D**: International specification for technical publications
