## 1. Purpose

This document defines the frozen mass properties baseline for the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 aircraft. These values are monitored by the mass properties watchdog CI check.

## 2. Frozen Mass Properties

### 2.1 Design Weights

The following mass values represent the certified design baseline:

- MTOW: **186000.0 kg** (Maximum Takeoff Weight)
- OEW: **95000.0 kg** (Operating Empty Weight)
- MZFW: **140000.0 kg** (Maximum Zero Fuel Weight)
- MLW: **150000.0 kg** (Maximum Landing Weight)

### 2.2 Capacity Limits

- Maximum fuel capacity: **8000.0 kg** (liquid hydrogen)
- Maximum payload capacity: **45000.0 kg** (passengers + cargo)

## 3. Design Philosophy

**Weight Reduction Strategies:**
- Composite materials: 60% by weight
- BWB integrated structure: 40% lower bending moments
- Electric propulsion: Eliminates heavy turbofan engines

**Weight Penalties:**
- H₂ tank system: +4500 kg vs conventional fuel tanks
- CO₂ capture system: +3000 kg (unique to AMPEL360)

## 4. Compliance

These mass properties satisfy:
- [CS-25.25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27) (Weight Limits)
- [CS-25.1581](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27) (General - Weight and Balance)
- Structural design loads per [CS-25.301](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27)

Any change to these frozen values requires ECR approval.
