# TS-02-10-003: H₂ Tank Location Trade Study

**Document ID:** TS-02-10-003  
**Title:** Hydrogen Tank Location Optimization  
**Project:** AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA  
**Date:** 2025-11-05  
**Status:** Complete - Three-Tank Centerline Selected  
**Classification:** Engineering Critical

---

## Executive Summary

This trade study evaluates optimal locations for hydrogen storage tanks in the BWB configuration, balancing CG control, safety, structural efficiency, and operational flexibility. **Selected: Three tanks along centerline below cabin floor** provides optimal performance and safety.

---

## 7. Safety Systems Integration

### 7.1 Leak Detection

**Option C Implementation:**
- 16 H₂ sensors per tank bay (48 total)
- Continuous concentration monitoring
- Automatic ventilation activation
- Isolation valves between tanks

### 7.2 Fire Protection

**Multi-Layer Defense:**
1. Fire barriers between tanks and cabin
2. Inert gas (N₂) blanketing system
3. Temperature monitoring (12 sensors/tank)
4. Automatic fuel shutoff
5. Emergency vent to atmosphere

### 7.3 Redundancy

**Three-Tank Advantage:**
- Continue flight with one tank isolated
- Dispatch with one tank inoperative (MEL)
- Progressive failure containment
- Enhanced operational reliability
