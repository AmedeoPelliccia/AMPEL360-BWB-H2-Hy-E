## Typical Measurements

### BWB-Specific Dimensions
1. **Overall Length:** Nose to tail (datum to Station 1000)
2. **Wingspan:** Tip to tip at maximum chord
3. **Height:** Ground to highest point (tail vertical)
4. **Wing Sweep:** Leading edge angle verification
5. **Dihedral/Anhedral:** Wing angle from horizontal

### Station Verification
- Fuselage station locations (FS 0 to FS 1000)
- Waterline heights (WL 0 to WL 500)
- Buttock lines (BL centerline to ±500)

### Critical Clearances
- Ground clearance (engines, belly)
- Landing gear dimensions
- Door/access panel alignments
- Antenna positions

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Environmental Conditions:**
   - Temperature: 15-25°C preferred
   - Avoid direct sunlight on instruments
   - Minimize air drafts for laser measurements

2. **Operator Qualification:**
   - Training required: Dimensional inspection course
   - Minimum 2 personnel for large measurements
   - Recording: Use provided data sheets

3. **Measurement Sequence:**
   - Start with datum/reference points
   - Work systematically (forward to aft)
   - Cross-check critical dimensions
   - Document any deviations
