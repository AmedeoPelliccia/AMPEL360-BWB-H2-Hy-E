# Systems Overview

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-02-10-00-OVW-006  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Propulsion & Energy (ATA 71, 24)

**Primary Power:** 4 × 2.5 MW PEM fuel cell stacks  
**Total Power:** 10 MW continuous  
**Fuel:** Gaseous hydrogen from cryogenic storage  
**Efficiency:** 55-60% (vs 40% turbofan)

## Fuel System (ATA 28)

**Type:** Cryogenic liquid hydrogen  
**Storage Temperature:** -253°C  
**Pressure:** 2.8-3.2 bar  
**Tanks:** 4 vacuum-insulated composite  
**Boil-off:** <0.3% per hour ground, <0.1% flight

## Environmental Control (ATA 21)

**Pressurization:** 8.5 psi differential  
**H₂ Compartment Ventilation:** 10 ACH normal, 50 ACH emergency  
**Leak Detection:** 8 sensors per compartment, <100ms alert  
**CO₂ Capture:** 100 kg/FH solid-state sorbent battery

## CAOS - AI Operations (ATA 95)

**Digital Twin:** Real-time physics simulation  
**Predictive Maintenance:** 500 FH advance warning  
**Fleet Learning:** Anonymous data sharing  
**Crew Interface:** Advisory with override authority

## Flight Controls (ATA 27)

**Type:** Fly-by-wire, fully digital  
**Surfaces:** Elevons, split rudders, winglets  
**Redundancy:** Quadruple channel  
**BWB Stability Augmentation:** Required for all flight phases

## Hydraulic System (ATA 29)

**Type:** Electric-hydraulic hybrid  
**Pressure:** 5,000 psi  
**Circuits:** 3 independent systems  
**Backup:** Electric backup actuators

## Landing Gear (ATA 32)

**Configuration:** Tricycle retractable  
**Main Gear:** 4-wheel bogies (2 per side)  
**Nose Gear:** Dual-wheel steering  
**Braking:** Carbon-carbon, electrically actuated

## Electrical Power (ATA 24)

**Primary Source:** Fuel cell stacks  
**Secondary Power:** 2 MWh battery bank  
**Distribution:** 270 VDC main bus  
**Backup:** RAT (Ram Air Turbine) for emergency

## Avionics (ATA 31, 34, 42, 45, 46)

**Flight Management:** Dual FMS with CAOS integration  
**Navigation:** GPS/IRS hybrid, GBAS capable  
**Communications:** SATCOM, VHF, HF, ACARS  
**Displays:** 5 large-format LCD (pilot + copilot)  
**Maintenance System:** CAOS predictive analytics

## Ice and Rain Protection (ATA 30)

**Wings:** Electric heating elements  
**Engine Inlets:** Hot air bleed from fuel cells  
**Windshields:** Electric heating, wipers  
**Sensors:** Heated pitot-static probes

## Fire Protection (ATA 26)

**Detection:** Optical smoke and heat sensors  
**Suppression:** Halon-free nitrogen inerting  
**H₂ Compartments:** Enhanced ventilation and monitoring  
**Extinguishers:** Portable units in cabin

## Oxygen System (ATA 35)

**Crew:** Chemical oxygen generators  
**Passengers:** Drop-down masks (15 min)  
**Supplemental:** High-pressure gaseous backup

## Water and Waste (ATA 38)

**Potable Water:** 450 L, pressurized system  
**Waste System:** Vacuum toilets, 500 L capacity  
**Gray Water:** Overboard discharge

## Lights (ATA 33)

**Exterior:** LED navigation, landing, taxi lights  
**Interior:** LED cabin lighting with mood control  
**Emergency:** Battery-powered path lighting

## Communication (ATA 23)

**Voice:** VHF, HF, SATCOM voice  
**Data:** ACARS, SATCOM data, ADS-B Out  
**Intercom:** Digital crew and cabin intercom  
**PA System:** Distributed passenger address

## Navigation (ATA 34)

**Primary:** Dual GPS/SBAS receivers  
**Inertial:** Triple IRU (Inertial Reference Units)  
**Radio Nav:** Dual VOR/ILS/DME receivers  
**Radar:** Weather radar with turbulence detection

## Autoflight (ATA 22)

**Autopilot:** Dual-channel with monitor  
**Autothrottle:** Integrated with fuel cell control  
**FMS:** Dual flight management system  
**CAOS Integration:** AI-optimized flight path
