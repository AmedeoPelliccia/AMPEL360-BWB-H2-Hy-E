# Weight and Balance Limits

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-02-10-00-OVW-003  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Structural Weight Limits

| Limit | Value (kg) | Condition |
|-------|------------|-----------|
| **Maximum Ramp Weight** | 185,500 | Includes taxi fuel |
| **Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW)** | 185,000 | At brake release |
| **Maximum Landing Weight (MLW)** | 150,000 | Structural limit |
| **Maximum Zero Fuel Weight (MZFW)** | 140,000 | Payload + OEW |
| **Operating Empty Weight (OEW)** | 95,000 | As equipped |

## Fuel Capacity

| Tank | Capacity (kg) | Location |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Center Tank 1 | 2,500 | Forward center body |
| Center Tank 2 | 2,000 | Aft center body |
| Left Outboard | 1,750 | Left wing |
| Right Outboard | 1,750 | Right wing |
| **Total Usable** | **8,000** | All tanks (H₂) |

## Payload

**Maximum Structural Payload:** 45,000 kg  
**Passengers (220 @ 95 kg avg):** 20,900 kg  
**Cargo Volume:** 48.5 m³ (containers)  
**Typical Cargo Weight:** 8,000 kg

## Center of Gravity Limits

**Forward Limit:** 15% MAC  
**Aft Limit:** 42% MAC  
**Normal Range:** 25-38% MAC  
**BWB Advantage:** 27% usable CG range vs 20% conventional
