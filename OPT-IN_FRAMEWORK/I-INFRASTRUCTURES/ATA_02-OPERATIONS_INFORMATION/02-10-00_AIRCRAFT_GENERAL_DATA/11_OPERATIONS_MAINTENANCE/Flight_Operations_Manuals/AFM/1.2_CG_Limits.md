# AFM 1.2 - Center of Gravity Limits

**Document:** Aircraft Flight Manual Section 1.2  
**ATA Chapter:** 02-10-00 - Aircraft General Data  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-05

## CG Envelope Limits

### Forward CG Limit
- **15% MAC** (Mean Aerodynamic Chord)
- Reference: BWB aerodynamic datum
- Minimum for control authority and stability

### Aft CG Limit
- **42% MAC** (Mean Aerodynamic Chord)
- Maximum for longitudinal stability
- BWB configuration specific

### Lateral CG Limits
- **±2.5% span** from centerline
- Symmetric loading required
- Maximum lateral offset: ±1.3 m

## Mean Aerodynamic Chord (MAC) Data

### BWB MAC Definition
- **MAC Length:** 12.5 m
- **MAC Location:** Station 15.8 m (BWB datum)
- **Leading Edge MAC (LEMAC):** Station 9.5 m
- **Trailing Edge MAC (TEMAC):** Station 22.0 m

### Reference Datum
- **BWB Datum:** Nose reference point
- **Vertical Datum:** Ground line at maximum takeoff attitude
- **Lateral Datum:** Aircraft centerline

## Hydrogen Fuel CG Effects

### H₂ Tank Configuration
- 6 cryogenic tanks in BWB center body
- Symmetric arrangement about centerline
- Tank sequencing for CG control

### Fuel CG Variation
- Full fuel CG: 21% MAC
- Empty fuel CG: 28% MAC
- Maximum shift during flight: 12% MAC

### Automated CG Management
- CAOS real-time CG calculation
- Active fuel transfer system
- Automatic trim adjustment
- Digital twin CG monitoring

## CG Calculation Methodology

### Standard Weight Method
```
CG = Σ(Weight × Arm) / Total Weight
```

### BWB Specific Considerations
- Wing-body integration effects
- Distributed lift generation
- Non-linear aerodynamic center movement

## Loading Limitations for CG Control

### Passenger Seating Restrictions
- Zone A (Forward): Seats 1-80 (15-22% MAC)
- Zone B (Center): Seats 81-140 (23-32% MAC)
- Zone C (Aft): Seats 141-220 (33-40% MAC)

### Cargo Loading Sequence
1. Forward cargo hold: Load first
2. Aft cargo hold: Balance loading
3. Center cargo: Fine adjustment
4. Fuel loading: Final CG positioning

### Critical Loading Scenarios
- All-forward seating: Requires aft cargo ballast
- All-aft seating: Requires forward cargo loading
- Minimum passenger load: Fuel transfer required

## CG Verification Requirements

### Pre-flight Verification
- Weight and balance calculation mandatory
- CAOS digital twin validation
- Pilot confirmation of loading
- Ground crew sign-off required

### In-flight Monitoring
- Real-time CG display on flight deck
- Fuel transfer automation
- Predictive CG alerts
- Digital twin continuous tracking

## Emergency Procedures

### CG Out of Limits
1. Immediate crew notification
2. Calculate corrective actions
3. Fuel transfer if in-flight
4. Landing at nearest suitable airport

### Fuel Imbalance
- Maximum lateral imbalance: 500 kg
- Automatic balancing system
- Manual override available
