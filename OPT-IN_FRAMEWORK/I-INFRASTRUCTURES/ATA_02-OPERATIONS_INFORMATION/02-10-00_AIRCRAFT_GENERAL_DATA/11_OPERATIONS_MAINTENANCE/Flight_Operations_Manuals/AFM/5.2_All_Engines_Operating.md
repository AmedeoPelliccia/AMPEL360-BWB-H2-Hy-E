### Takeoff Performance Adjustments

#### Altitude Correction
- Add 7% to distances per 1,000 ft elevation
- Reduce V-speeds by 2 KIAS per 1,000 ft

#### Temperature Correction
- Add 3% to distances per 10°C above ISA
- V-speeds remain constant (IAS)

#### Runway Condition
- Wet runway: +15% distance
- Contaminated (3mm water): +25% distance
- Slush/Snow: Not approved for takeoff

#### Wind Components
- Headwind: Reduce distance by 5% per 10 kt
- Tailwind: Increase distance by 10% per 5 kt
- Maximum tailwind: 10 kt

### Landing Performance Adjustments

#### Altitude Correction
- Add 5% to distance per 1,000 ft elevation

#### Temperature Correction
- Add 2% to distance per 10°C above ISA

#### Runway Condition
- Wet runway: +15% distance
- Standing water (3mm): +45% distance
- Ice/Snow: Landing not approved without de-icing

#### Wind Components
- Headwind: Reduce by 5% per 10 kt
- Tailwind: Increase by 15% per 5 kt
- Maximum tailwind: 10 kt

#### Slope Correction
- Upslope: Reduce distance by 10% per 1° up
- Downslope: Add 10% per 1° down
- Maximum approved slope: ±2°
