## 1. Purpose

This Validation Plan defines the approach for validating that the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 aircraft general data meets operational requirements and performs as intended in the operational environment.

---

## 2. Scope

### 2.1 Validation Areas

- **Weight & Balance:** MTOW, OEW, CG location
- **Performance:** Range, cruise speed, ceiling
- **Operational Capability:** Mission performance validation

### 2.2 Applicable Standards

- EASA CS-25
- FAA 14 CFR Part 25
- ICAO Annex 8
- Operational requirements specifications

## 5. Success Criteria

### 5.1 Weight & Balance
- MTOW within ±500 kg of target
- OEW within ±1000 kg of estimated
- CG range covers full operational envelope
- No load limitations in operational range

### 5.2 Performance
- Range ≥ 6,500 km with full payload
- Cruise speed ≥ Mach 0.82
- Service ceiling ≥ 41,000 ft
- All performance margins met

### 5.3 Operational Validation
- All operational procedures validated
- Pilot handling qualities Level 1 or 2
- Ground operations within time limits
- Maintenance accessibility confirmed
