## 3. Test Method

### 3.1 Flight Test Approach
**Option 1: Actual Range Flight**
- Fly actual distance with payload simulation
- Monitor fuel consumption throughout flight
- Land with calculated reserves

**Option 2: Fuel Consumption Method (Preferred)**
- Fly representative mission segment
- Measure fuel consumption rate precisely
- Extrapolate to full range
- Multiple flight conditions tested

### 3.2 Test Configuration
- Aircraft weight: Maximum takeoff weight (185,000 kg)
- Fuel load: 8,000 kg Hâ‚‚ (full tanks)
- Payload simulation: Ballast for 220 passengers + cargo

### 3.3 Test Conditions

- Speed: M 0.85 (design cruise)
- Atmospheric conditions: ISA or corrected to ISA
- Weather: Avoid significant headwinds/tailwinds
