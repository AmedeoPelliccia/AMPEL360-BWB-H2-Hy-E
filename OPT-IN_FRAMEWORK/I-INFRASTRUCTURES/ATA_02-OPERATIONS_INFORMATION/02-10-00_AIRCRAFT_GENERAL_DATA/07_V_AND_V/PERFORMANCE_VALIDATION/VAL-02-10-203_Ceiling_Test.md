## 2. Specification

### 2.1 Design Requirement
- **Requirement ID:** RQ-02-10-PERF-003
- **Parameter:** Service Ceiling
- **Target Value:** 43,000 ft (FL430) minimum
- **Definition:** Altitude at which rate of climb = 100 ft/min
- **Conditions:**
  - Standard atmospheric conditions (ISA)
  - Maximum continuous power
  - Aircraft at mid-cruise weight

### 2.2 Related Performance
- Initial cruise altitude capability: FL410
- Maximum certified altitude: FL450
- Optimum altitude (best fuel economy): FL380-FL410

---

## 3. Test Method

### 3.1 Altitude Build-Up Approach
1. Start at normal cruise altitude (FL410)
2. Incrementally increase altitude
3. Measure climb performance at each level
4. Identify service ceiling (100 ft/min climb)
5. Test at maximum certified altitude if achievable

### 3.2 Test Points
- FL410: Baseline cruise
- FL420: Climb performance measurement
- FL430: Service ceiling validation
- FL440: Performance margin
- FL450: Maximum altitude (if achievable)

### 3.3 Data Collection
- Altitude
- Rate of climb
- True airspeed
- Power setting
- Fuel flow
- Outside air temperature
- Aircraft weight
- Handling characteristics
- Pressurization system performance
