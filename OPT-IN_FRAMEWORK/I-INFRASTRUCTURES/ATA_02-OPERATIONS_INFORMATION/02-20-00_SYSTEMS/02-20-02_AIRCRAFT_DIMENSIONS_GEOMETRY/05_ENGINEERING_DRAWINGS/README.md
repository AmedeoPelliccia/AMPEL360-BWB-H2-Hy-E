## Completed Drawings Summary

### Priority Set (7 drawings) - ✅ COMPLETE

These drawings form the "visual skeleton" of the Q100 BWB:

1. **GE-001** - Three-View General Arrangement
   - Top, side, and front views
   - Principal dimensions clearly annotated
   - ICAO Code E compatible design

2. **RS-001** - Coordinate System
   - ATA 100 / ISO 1151 standard
   - 3D isometric with axis directions
   - Datum point: FS15000, BL0, WL5000

3. **PF-001** - BWB Planform Top View
   - 52m wingspan with station markings
   - 37° leading edge sweep
   - Aspect ratio 3.2, area 845 m²

4. **CB-001** - Maximum Center Section
   - Double-bubble pressure vessel
   - Lower bubble: 36m × 4.3m (cabin)
   - Upper bubble: 20m × 4.2m (H₂ deck)

5. **CAB-001** - Cabin Floor Plan
   - Complete deck layout (220 passengers)
   - Business 2-2-2 (rows 1-5, 30 seats)
   - Economy 2-3-3-2 (rows 6-24, 190 seats)
   - 6 doors, 3 aisles, galleys, lavatories

6. **H2-001** - H₂ Tank Upper Deck Plan
   - 4 tanks × 88 m³ = 350 m³ total
   - 8m × 4m × 3.5m per tank
   - 500mm safety spacing, ventilated voids
   - 4 refueling access panels

7. **GC-001** - Ground Clearance Side View
   - MTOW level attitude
   - Belly clearance: 0.8m (+300mm above CS-25.231)
   - Wingtip clearance: 2.8m
   - Main gear height: 3.2m

## Drawing Standards

**Format:**
- Digital format: PNG (300 DPI)
- Drawing sheet size: A0 equivalent digital
- Coordinate system: ATA 100 / ISO 1151
- Units: Millimeters (mm) primary, meters (m) for annotations
- Tolerancing: ASME Y14.5 / ISO 1101

**Metadata on Each Drawing:**
- Drawing number (e.g., GE-001)
- Title
- Date
- Revision
- Status (Released, In Review, Preliminary)

**Content Standards:**
- Clear dimension annotations
- Technical data boxes
- Material specifications (where applicable)
- Reference to related drawings
- Compliance notes (CS-25, ICAO, etc.)
