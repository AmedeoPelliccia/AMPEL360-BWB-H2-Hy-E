# Pressurization Boundary

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-02-11-00-DES-CB-005  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Pressurized Volume Definition

**Total Pressurized Volume:** 1,850 m³

### Boundary Description

**Upper Boundary:**
- H₂ tank barrier (non-pressurized above)
- Structural bulkhead at fuselage station 6.0m
- Composite sandwich construction
- Thermal insulation integrated

**Lower Boundary:**
- Cabin floor level
- Cargo compartment below (pressurized)
- Load-bearing floor structure

**Forward Boundary:**
- Pressure bulkhead at station 2.5m
- Nose cone unpressurized (avionics bay vented)
- Door seal integration

**Aft Boundary:**
- Pressure bulkhead at station 27.0m
- APU bay unpressurized
- Control surface actuation bays sealed

## Design Pressure

**Maximum Differential:** 62 kPa (9.0 psi)

**Operating Conditions:**
- Maximum cabin altitude: 8,000 ft (2,438 m)
- Cruise altitude: 41,000 ft (12,497 m)
- Design altitude: 45,000 ft (margin)

## Structural Design

**Pressure Vessel Shape:** Double-bubble configuration

**Advantages:**
- Efficient load distribution
- Optimal space utilization  
- Reduced structural weight
- Natural H₂ separation

**Skin Thickness:**
- Primary: 4-6mm carbon/epoxy
- Frames: 0.6m spacing
- Stringers: 0.2m pitch
- Ultimate load: 1.5x design pressure

## Safety Features

**H₂ Tank Isolation:**
- Physical barrier (structural bulkhead)
- Double-wall construction
- Ventilation to atmosphere
- Pressure relief valves

**Emergency Depressurization:**
- Controlled vent system
- Outflow valves: Redundant (3x)
- Emergency dump capability
- CAOS monitoring integration

## Certification Compliance

**CS-25.365 Requirements:**
- Pressure proof test: 1.33x design
- Pressure ultimate test: 1.5x design
- Fatigue testing: 3x life cycles
- Damage tolerance: Validated
