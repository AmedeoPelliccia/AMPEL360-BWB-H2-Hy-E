# AFM 1.1 - Weight Limits

**Document:** Aircraft Flight Manual Section 1.1  
**ATA Chapter:** 02-10-00 - Aircraft General Data  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-05

## Maximum Weights

### Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW)
- **185,000 kg** (407,855 lb)
- Certified per CS-25.25 and FAR 25.25

### Maximum Landing Weight (MLW)
- **155,000 kg** (341,717 lb)
- Structural limit per CS-25.473

### Maximum Zero Fuel Weight (MZFW)
- **145,000 kg** (319,670 lb)
- Wing structural bending moment limit

### Maximum Ramp Weight
- **186,500 kg** (411,162 lb)
- Includes taxi fuel allowance

## Minimum Weights

### Minimum Operating Weight
- **105,000 kg** (231,485 lb)
- Basic Empty Weight + Minimum Crew + Required Equipment

### Minimum Flight Weight
- **110,000 kg** (242,508 lb)
- With minimum fuel for flight operations

## Hydrogen Fuel Weight Considerations

### Maximum Hydrogen Fuel Load
- **4,500 kg** (9,921 lb) H₂
- Total capacity: 6 tanks in BWB center section
- Equivalent to ~22,500 kg of Jet-A energy

### Fuel System Weight Impact
- Cryogenic H₂ tanks (structural): 8,500 kg
- Insulation and support systems: 2,200 kg
- Fuel cell integration: 3,800 kg

## Load Factor Limits by Weight

| Weight Range | Load Factor (n) | Certification |
|--------------|-----------------|---------------|
| MTOW (185t)  | +2.5g / -1.0g  | CS-25.337     |
| MLW (155t)   | +2.8g / -1.2g  | CS-25.337     |
| MZFW (145t)  | +3.0g / -1.5g  | CS-25.337     |

## Weight Verification Requirements

- Pre-flight weight and balance calculation mandatory
- CAOS digital twin weight monitoring system
- Real-time fuel consumption tracking
- Load distribution verification per AMM procedures

## Compliance and Limitations

- All weights include standard fuel density correction
- H₂ weight varies with temperature (-253°C nominal)
- Weight checks required per 02-10-20-00A-520A-A
- Digital twin verification per CAOS Integration parameters
