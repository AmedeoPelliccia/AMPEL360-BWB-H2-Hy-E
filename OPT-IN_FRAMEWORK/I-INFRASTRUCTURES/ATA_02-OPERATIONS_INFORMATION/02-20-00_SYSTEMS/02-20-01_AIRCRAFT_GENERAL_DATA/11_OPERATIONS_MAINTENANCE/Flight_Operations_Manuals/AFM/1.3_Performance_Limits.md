# AFM 1.3 - Performance Limits

**Document:** Aircraft Flight Manual Section 1.3  
**ATA Chapter:** 02-10-00 - Aircraft General Data  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-05

## Airspeed Limitations

### Maximum Operating Speed (VMO)
- **350 KIAS** (Knots Indicated Airspeed)
- Below 26,000 ft altitude

### Maximum Operating Mach (MMO)
- **Mach 0.82**
- Above 26,000 ft altitude
- BWB optimized cruise efficiency

### Design Maneuvering Speed (VA)
- At MTOW: 280 KIAS
- At MLW: 265 KIAS
- At MZFW: 255 KIAS

### Maximum Flap Extended Speeds
- Approach configuration: 220 KIAS
- Landing configuration: 180 KIAS
- BWB integrated flap system

### Never Exceed Speed (VNE)
- **380 KIAS** or **Mach 0.86** (whichever is lower)
- Structural limit per CS-25.335

## Altitude Limitations

### Maximum Certified Altitude
- **43,000 ft** (13,106 m)
- Pressurization and fuel cell performance limit

### Minimum Safe Altitude
- **500 ft AGL** for cruise operations
- Terrain clearance per regulations

### Optimal Cruise Altitude Range
- **35,000 - 41,000 ft**
- Maximum fuel cell efficiency
- BWB aerodynamic optimization

## Temperature Limitations

### Maximum Operating Temperature
- **ISA +35°C** at sea level
- Fuel cell cooling system limit

### Minimum Operating Temperature
- **ISA -65°C** or -70°C absolute
- H₂ system cold weather operations

### Hydrogen System Temperature Limits
- H₂ Storage: -253°C (20K) nominal
- Fuel Cell Operating: 60-80°C
- Ambient Air: -65°C to +50°C

## Engine/Fuel Cell Limitations

### Fuel Cell Stack Limits
- Maximum Power Output: 18 MW total (6×3MW stacks)
- Continuous Rating: 15 MW (83% of max)
- Maximum Duration: Unlimited at continuous rating
- Transient Power: 20 MW for 5 minutes (emergency)

### Hydrogen Consumption Limits
- Maximum Flow Rate: 0.8 kg/s total
- Normal Cruise: 0.35 kg/s
- Reserve Requirement: 500 kg H₂

### Electric Motor Limits
- Maximum RPM: 3,600 (distributed propulsion)
- Maximum Torque: 85 kNm per motor unit
- Temperature Limit: 150°C winding temperature

## Structural Limitations

### Load Factor Limits
- Positive: +2.5g at MTOW, +3.0g at MZFW
- Negative: -1.0g at MTOW, -1.5g at MZFW
- BWB structural design per CS-25.337

### Maximum Wing Span for Ground Operations
- **52.0 m** (170.6 ft)
- Gate compatibility: ICAO Code E
- Wingtip clearance requirements

### Maximum Crosswind Component
- Takeoff: 35 knots
- Landing: 38 knots
- BWB lateral stability advantages

## Range and Endurance Limits

### Maximum Range
- **4,000 km** (2,160 nm) with 220 passengers
- ISA conditions, optimal altitude
- 500 kg reserve fuel included

### Maximum Endurance
- **7.5 hours** at optimal cruise
- Includes 45-minute reserve
- Fuel cell continuous operation validated

### Diversion Capability
- 200 nm alternate airport range
- Per EASA operational requirements

## Special Performance Limitations

### One Fuel Cell Stack Inoperative
- Maximum altitude: 30,000 ft
- Maximum speed: Mach 0.75
- Range reduction: 35%

### Degraded Power Operations
- Minimum power: 60% (9 MW)
- Landing assured with 2 stacks
- Emergency power: Battery backup (5 minutes)

### BWB Configuration Limits
- No asymmetric fuel cell operations
- Balanced thrust distribution required
- Digital twin monitoring mandatory

## Environmental Performance Limits

### Noise Limitations
- Approach: 85 dB EPNdB
- Sideline: 80 dB EPNdB
- Overfly: 75 dB EPNdB
- Significantly below Stage 5 limits

### Emissions (Operating)
- CO₂: **-5 kg per flight** (net negative with DAC)
- NOx: 0 kg (hydrogen fuel cells)
- Particulates: 0 kg
