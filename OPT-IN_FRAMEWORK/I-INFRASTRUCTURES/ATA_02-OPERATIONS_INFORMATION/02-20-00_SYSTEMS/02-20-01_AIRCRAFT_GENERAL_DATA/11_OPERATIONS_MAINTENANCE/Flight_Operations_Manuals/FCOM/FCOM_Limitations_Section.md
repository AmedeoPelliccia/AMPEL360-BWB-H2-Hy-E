# FCOM - Limitations Section

**Document:** Flight Crew Operations Manual - Limitations  
**ATA Chapter:** 02-10-00 - Aircraft General Data  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-05

## Purpose

This section provides flight crew with operational limitations for the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 aircraft in a condensed, quick-reference format suitable for daily operations.

## Quick Reference Limitations

### Weight Limitations

| Parameter | Value | Unit |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW) | 185,000 | kg |
| Maximum Landing Weight (MLW) | 155,000 | kg |
| Maximum Zero Fuel Weight (MZFW) | 145,000 | kg |
| Maximum Ramp Weight | 186,500 | kg |

### Center of Gravity Limits

| Weight | Forward CG | Aft CG |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| MTOW (185t) | 18% MAC | 38% MAC |
| MLW (155t) | 17% MAC | 40% MAC |
| MZFW (145t) | 16% MAC | 41% MAC |
| All weights | 15% MAC | 42% MAC |

**MAC (Mean Aerodynamic Chord):** 12.5 m  
**CAOS CG Monitoring:** Continuous automatic tracking

### Speed Limitations

| Speed Type | Value | Altitude Limit |
|------------|-------|----------------|
| VMO (Max Operating Speed) | 350 KIAS | Below 26,000 ft |
| MMO (Max Operating Mach) | Mach 0.82 | Above 26,000 ft |
| VNE (Never Exceed) | 380 KIAS / M0.86 | All altitudes |
| Flap Extended (Approach) | 220 KIAS | All altitudes |
| Flap Extended (Landing) | 180 KIAS | All altitudes |

### Altitude Limitations

- **Maximum Certified Altitude:** 43,000 ft
- **Optimal Cruise Range:** 35,000 - 41,000 ft
- **Minimum Safe Altitude (Cruise):** 500 ft AGL

### Temperature Limitations

- **Maximum Operating:** ISA +35°C
- **Minimum Operating:** ISA -65°C or -70°C absolute
- **Fuel Cell Operating Range:** 60-80°C internal

### Load Factor Limitations

| Weight Condition | Positive | Negative |
|------------------|----------|----------|
| MTOW (185t) | +2.5g | -1.0g |
| MLW (155t) | +2.8g | -1.2g |
| MZFW (145t) | +3.0g | -1.5g |

### Wind Limitations

- **Maximum Crosswind (Takeoff):** 35 kt
- **Maximum Crosswind (Landing):** 38 kt
- **Maximum Tailwind:** 10 kt

## Fuel Cell and Power System Limitations

### Normal Operations - All Stacks

| Parameter | Limit | Duration |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Maximum Power | 18 MW | 5 min (Takeoff) |
| Continuous Power | 15 MW | Unlimited |
| Minimum Power (Cruise) | 8 MW | - |
| Emergency Power (Battery) | 2 MW | 5 min |

### Degraded Operations

**One Stack Inoperative:**
- Maximum altitude: 30,000 ft
- Maximum speed: Mach 0.75
- Range reduction: 35%
- Landing assured with 2 stacks minimum

### Hydrogen Fuel System

- **Maximum Fuel Load:** 4,500 kg H₂
- **Usable Fuel:** 4,300 kg H₂
- **Reserve Requirement:** 500 kg H₂ minimum
- **Maximum Flow Rate:** 0.8 kg/s
- **Storage Temperature:** -253°C (20K)

**CAUTION:**
- Do not attempt refueling above -240°C tank temperature
- Automatic fuel transfer system must be operative for all flights
- Fuel temperature monitoring mandatory

## BWB Configuration Limitations

### Ground Operations

- **Wing Span:** 52.0 m (ICAO Code E gates required)
- **Parking Gate Clearance:** Minimum 3 m wingtip clearance
- **Taxiway Width:** Minimum 23 m required
- **Turning Radius:** 32 m minimum

### Flight Operations

- **No asymmetric fuel cell operations permitted**
- **Balanced thrust distribution required at all times**
- **CG must remain within 15-42% MAC envelope**
- **Digital twin monitoring mandatory for flight**

## Structural Limitations

### Maximum Dimensions

- **Overall Length:** 48.5 m
- **Wing Span:** 52.0 m
- **Height:** 12.8 m
- **Door Opening Heights:** 2.1 m standard

### Runway Requirements

- **Minimum Runway Width:** 45 m
- **Minimum Taxiway Width:** 23 m
- **Maximum Runway Slope:** ±2°
- **Pavement Strength:** PCN 75/R/B/W/T minimum

## Environmental Limitations

### Weather Minimums

**Takeoff:**
- Visibility: 400 m RVR minimum
- Ceiling: 200 ft minimum (CAT I)
- No operations in severe icing conditions
- Lightning: Avoid active thunderstorms by 20 nm

**Landing:**
- CAT I: RVR 550 m, DH 200 ft
- CAT II: RVR 300 m, DH 100 ft (if equipped)
- CAT III: Not certified

### Runway Surface Conditions

**Approved for Operations:**
- Dry runway: Normal operations
- Wet runway: +15% landing distance
- Compacted snow: +20% landing distance (max 5mm)

**NOT Approved:**
- Standing water >3mm depth
- Slush or wet snow
- Ice (unless de-iced)

## CAOS System Requirements

### Mandatory for Flight

✓ Digital twin connection established  
✓ Real-time CG monitoring active  
✓ Fuel management system operative  
✓ Performance prediction available

### Advisory Only

○ Route optimization recommendations  
○ Weather impact analysis  
○ Predictive maintenance alerts  
○ Fleet data sharing

**NOTE:** Flight may continue if CAOS advisory functions fail, but mandatory monitoring must remain operative.

## Emergency Limitations

### Ditching

- **Ditching Speed:** 130 KIAS
- **Power Setting:** Minimum for controlled flight
- **BWB Configuration Advantage:** Large flat bottom, better flotation

### Evacuation

- **Maximum Evacuation Time:** 90 seconds (220 passengers)
- **Emergency Exits:** 8 Type-A exits
- **Slide Deployment:** Automatic below 12 ft

### Fire Protection

- **Fuel Cell Compartment:** Automatic halon suppression
- **Hydrogen Leak Detection:** Automatic shutdown and venting
- **Battery Compartment:** Automatic cooling activation

## Crew Requirements

### Minimum Crew

- **Flight Crew:** 2 pilots (Captain + First Officer)
- **Cabin Crew:** 4 minimum (1 per 50 passengers)

### Qualifications

- Type rating: AMPEL360 BWB H2
- Hydrogen systems training: 40 hours
- BWB handling qualification: 20 hours simulator
- CAOS system certification: 8 hours

## Operational Restrictions

### Special Authorizations Required

- Operations above 41,000 ft: RVSM approval
- Extended range operations: ETOPS equivalent approval
- Single-pilot operations: NOT APPROVED
- Autoland below CAT I: NOT CERTIFIED

### Airport Requirements

- Hydrogen refueling capability mandatory
- Emergency response training for H2 incidents
- Fire rescue category: RFF Category 9 minimum
- Ground power compatible with H2 fuel cell APU
