# RQ-02-10-00-001: Aircraft Identification

**Requirement ID:** RQ-02-10-00-001  
**Category:** General  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Status:** Approved

## Requirement

The aircraft shall be identified by the model designation **AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA** in all documentation, type certificate data, and operational materials.

**Model Designation:** AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA  
**Type Certificate Number:** [To Be Assigned by EASA]  
**Manufacturer:** AMPEL360 Aviation Technologies  
**Aircraft Category:** Transport Category (CS-25)

## Rationale

Unique and consistent aircraft identification is required for:
- Type certification process
- Regulatory compliance tracking
- Configuration management
- Operational documentation
- Airworthiness directives
- Service bulletins

## Verification

- **Method:** Inspection
- **Procedure:** Visual inspection of type plate, documentation review
- **Acceptance Criteria:** 
  - Type plate affixed per CS-25.1543
  - Model designation consistent across all documentation
  - Certification documents match aircraft configuration

## Compliance

- CS-25.1543: Instrument markings
- CS-25.1583: Operating limitations and information
- EASA Part 21: Certification procedures
