# RQ-02-10-30-004: Crew Complement

**Requirement ID:** RQ-02-10-30-004  
**Category:** Capacities  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Approved

## Requirement

The aircraft shall be operated with a minimum crew complement as specified below to ensure safe operations and passenger service.

**Flight Crew (Minimum):**
- Captain: 1
- First Officer: 1
- **Total Flight Crew: 2**

**Cabin Crew (Typical):**
- Purser/Lead Flight Attendant: 1
- Flight Attendants: 5
- **Total Cabin Crew: 6**

**Total Crew Complement: 8**

## Flight Deck Configuration

**Two-Crew Cockpit Design:**
- Side-by-side seating arrangement
- Advanced avionics with CAOS integration
- Reduced workload through automation
- Jump seat for observer/check pilot

**CAOS Enhancement:**
- AI-assisted flight management
- Predictive maintenance alerts
- Real-time performance optimization
- Enhanced situational awareness

## Cabin Crew Requirements

**FAA/EASA Requirements:**
- Minimum 1 flight attendant per 50 passengers
- For 220 passengers: Minimum 5 flight attendants required
- Plus 1 purser for safety management

**Emergency Duties:**
- Evacuation leadership
- Emergency equipment operation
- Passenger assistance
- Communication with flight deck

## Crew Rest Facilities

**Flight Crew:**
- 2 crew rest seats in cockpit (observer positions)

**Cabin Crew (Long Range Operations):**
- 6 crew rest seats in designated area
- Privacy curtains
- Reading lights
- Stowage for personal items

## Rationale

Crew complement ensures:
- Regulatory compliance (FAA/EASA)
- Safe aircraft operation
- Adequate passenger service
- Emergency response capability
- Crew fatigue management (extended operations)
- CAOS system monitoring

## Verification

- **Method:** Demonstration and Analysis
- **Procedure:** 
  - Operational procedures validation
  - Emergency evacuation demonstration
  - Crew resource management assessment
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Two-crew operation validated through simulation
  - Emergency procedures executable by crew
  - CAOS workload reduction demonstrated
  - Cabin crew adequate for evacuation (90 sec)

## Crew Training Requirements

**Flight Crew:**
- Type rating per CS-FCL
- BWB-specific characteristics training
- CAOS system operation
- H₂ systems emergency procedures

**Cabin Crew:**
- Safety and emergency procedures
- H₂ safety awareness
- BWB-specific evacuation procedures
- Passenger service standards

## Compliance

- CS-25.1523: Minimum flight crew
- CS-OPS: Commercial air transport operations
- FAR Part 91.529: Flight attendant requirements
- CS-FCL: Flight crew licensing
