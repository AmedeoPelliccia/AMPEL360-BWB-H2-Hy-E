# RQ-02-10-30-002: Cargo Capacity

**Requirement ID:** RQ-02-10-30-002  
**Category:** Capacities  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Approved

## Requirement

The aircraft shall provide cargo capacity of at least 40 m³ (1,413 ft³) with a maximum payload of 22,000 kg for baggage and cargo operations.

**Cargo Compartments:**

| Compartment | Volume (m³) | Max Load (kg) | Location | Container Type |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|----------|----------------|
| Forward Hold | 18 | 9,000 | FS 8-15 | LD3-45, Bulk |
| Aft Hold | 22 | 13,000 | FS 28-36 | LD3-45, Bulk |
| **Total** | **40** | **22,000** | - | - |

**Container Compatibility:**
- LD3-45 containers: Up to 12 positions
- Bulk loading area: 15 m³
- Containerized + bulk mix capability

## Rationale

Cargo capacity ensures:
- Competitive baggage allowance (220 pax × 23kg checked + 15kg carry-on)
- Cargo revenue generation potential
- Operational flexibility
- Market requirements satisfaction

## Loading System

- **Floor System:** Roller tracks and ball mats
- **Loading Method:** Front and aft cargo doors with loaders
- **Restraint System:** Certified cargo nets and straps
- **Fire Protection:** Class C cargo compartment (CS-25.857)

## Verification

- **Method:** Inspection and Test
- **Procedure:** 
  - Volume measurement verification
  - Floor loading test
  - Container fit check
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Actual volume ≥ 40 m³
  - Floor loading capacity verified
  - Container compatibility demonstrated
  - Fire protection systems tested

## Compliance

- CS-25.787: Stowage compartments
- CS-25.855: Cargo and baggage compartments
- CS-25.857: Cargo compartment classification
- CS-25.1447: Equipment standards for oxygen
