# RQ-02-10-60-003: Operational Limitations

**Requirement ID:** RQ-02-10-60-003  
**Category:** Operational  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Status:** Approved

## Requirement

The aircraft shall be operated within defined limitations to ensure safety, reliability, and regulatory compliance throughout the operational envelope.

## Airspeed Limitations

**Maximum Operating Speeds:**
- VMO (Maximum Operating Speed): 350 KIAS
- MMO (Maximum Operating Mach): M0.85
- Maximum Flap Extended Speed (VFE):
  - Flaps 15°: 230 KIAS
  - Flaps 25°: 200 KIAS
  - Flaps 40° (Full): 170 KIAS
- Maximum Landing Gear Extended Speed (VLE): 250 KIAS
- Maximum Landing Gear Operating Speed (VLO):
  - Extension: 250 KIAS
  - Retraction: 220 KIAS

**Minimum Speeds:**
- VS1 (Stall speed clean): 128 KIAS
- VS0 (Stall speed landing config): 104 KIAS

## Weight Limitations

**Maximum Weights:**
- MTOW: 185,000 kg (407,855 lb)
- MLW: 155,000 kg (341,713 lb)
- MZFW: 135,000 kg (297,624 lb)
- Maximum Taxi Weight: 186,000 kg

**Minimum Operating Weight:**
- Minimum crew + required equipment: 95,000 kg

## Altitude Limitations

- Maximum Operating Altitude: 45,000 ft (FL450)
- Maximum Cabin Altitude: 8,000 ft
- Minimum Operating Altitude: Sea Level

## Center of Gravity Limits

Per RQ-02-10-20-005:
- Forward Limit: 15.0% MAC
- Aft Limit: 42.0% MAC
- Lateral Imbalance: ±500 kg

## Temperature Limitations

**Operating Temperature Range:**
- Maximum: ISA +40°C (up to 54°C at ground)
- Minimum: ISA -70°C (-54°C SAT at cruise altitude)

**H₂ System Temperatures:**
- H₂ Tank Operating: -253°C to -240°C
- Fuel Cell Operating: 60-80°C
- Maximum fuel cell temperature: 90°C (caution)

## Crosswind Limitations

**Demonstrated Crosswind:**
- Takeoff and Landing: 38 kt (direct crosswind)
- Tailwind: 15 kt maximum

**Recommended Operational Limits:**
- Dry Runway: 35 kt crosswind
- Wet Runway: 28 kt crosswind
- Contaminated Runway: 20 kt crosswind

## Runway Surface Limitations

**Approved Surfaces:**
- Paved (asphalt, concrete): No restrictions
- Grooved runway: Approved
- Dry grass (emergency): Maximum weight 140,000 kg

**Contamination Limits:**
- Standing water: Max 13 mm depth
- Slush: Max 13 mm depth
- Snow (dry): Max 50 mm depth
- Snow (wet): Max 13 mm depth
- Ice: Not approved (requires de-icing)

## Fuel System Limitations

**H₂ Fuel:**
- Minimum Usable Fuel: 500 kg (reserves)
- Maximum Imbalance: 500 kg between tank pairs
- Boil-off Rate: < 0.5% per day (ground operations)
- Refueling: Only by qualified H₂ ground crew

**Fuel Temperature:**
- Maximum: -240°C (approaching gaseous state)
- Minimum: -253°C (storage temperature)

## Fuel Cell Limitations

**Power Limitations:**
- Maximum Continuous Power: 12 MW
- Maximum Transient Power (5 min): 14 MW
- Single Fuel Cell Failure: Continued flight authorized
- Dual Fuel Cell Failure: Land at nearest suitable airport

**Starting Limitations:**
- Minimum Battery Voltage for Start: 24V DC
- Maximum Start Attempts: 3 (then 30 min cool-down)
- Ambient Temperature for Start: -40°C to +54°C

## Flight Maneuver Limitations

**Load Factors (Flaps Retracted):**
- Positive: +2.5g to +3.0g (weight dependent)
- Negative: -1.0g to 0.0g

**Load Factors (Flaps Extended):**
- Positive: +2.0g maximum
- Negative: 0.0g minimum

**Bank Angle:**
- Normal operations: 30° maximum
- Unusual attitudes: 60° maximum
- Steep turns demonstration: 45°

## Special Operations

**ETOPS (Extended Operations):**
- ETOPS-180 Approved (after service experience)
- Maximum Diversion Time: 180 minutes
- H₂ fuel reserves per regulations
- Enhanced maintenance program required

**Low Visibility Operations:**
- CAT I: DH 200 ft, RVR 550m
- CAT II: DH 100 ft, RVR 350m (with appropriate equipment)
- CAT III: Future approval after service experience

**Reduced Runway Operations:**
- Performance-limited operations approved
- Detailed performance calculations required
- CAOS performance optimization recommended

## Equipment Requirements for Operations

**Day VFR:**
- Standard equipment per MEL
- CAOS basic functionality

**Night and IFR:**
- All required instruments operative
- Dual navigation systems
- CAOS full functionality
- Weather radar
- TCAS II

## H₂ Specific Limitations

**Ground Operations:**
- No refueling with passengers on board
- 30m safety zone during refueling
- H₂ leak check before each flight
- No open flames within 30m of aircraft

**In-Flight:**
- Monitor H₂ system continuously
- Respond to all H₂ warnings immediately
- Land as soon as practical if H₂ leak detected
- Follow emergency procedures for H₂ fire

## CAOS System Limitations

**Operational Status:**
- CAOS Full Functionality: Normal operations
- CAOS Degraded: Operations with restrictions (per MEL)
- CAOS Inoperative: Operations authorized with performance penalties

**Decision Authority:**
- Crew always retains final authority
- CAOS recommendations are advisory only
- Crew must be able to operate without CAOS

## Environmental Limitations

**Icing Conditions:**
- Operations approved in icing conditions with ice protection ON
- Maximum icing severity: Moderate (per JAR-OPS/EASA OPS)
- Ice accumulation monitoring required

**Turbulence:**
- Severe Turbulence: Reduce speed to VA (maneuvering speed)
- Thunderstorms: Avoid by at least 20 NM

**Volcanic Ash:**
- Operations not authorized in visible ash
- Deviation required if ash encounter suspected

## Noise Abatement

**Noise Preferential Runways:**
- Follow published procedures
- CAOS provides noise-optimized profiles
- Departures: Reduced thrust available if performance permits

## Emergency Equipment Required

- Life vests: All occupants
- Life rafts: Extended overwater operations
- Emergency locator transmitter (ELT)
- Fire extinguishers: 6 minimum
- Emergency lighting
- First aid kits: 3 minimum

## Rationale

Operational limitations ensure:
- Safe operations within design envelope
- Compliance with certification basis
- Protection of aircraft systems
- Crew and passenger safety
- Regulatory compliance

## Verification

- **Method:** Analysis and Documentation
- **Procedure:**
  - Limitations derived from certification tests
  - Flight Manual documentation
  - Crew training on limitations
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - All limitations documented in AFM
  - Crew demonstrates understanding
  - Operations Manual procedures comply
