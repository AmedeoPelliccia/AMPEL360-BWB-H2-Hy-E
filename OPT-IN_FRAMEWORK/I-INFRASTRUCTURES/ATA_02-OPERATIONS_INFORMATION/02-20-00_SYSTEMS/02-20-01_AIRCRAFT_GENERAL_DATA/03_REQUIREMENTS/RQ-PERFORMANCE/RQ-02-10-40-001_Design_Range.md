# RQ-02-10-40-001: Design Range

**Requirement ID:** RQ-02-10-40-001  
**Category:** Performance  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Approved

## Requirement

The aircraft shall achieve a design range of 3,500 nautical miles (6,482 km) with 220 passengers and baggage at long-range cruise conditions.

**Design Range:** 3,500 NM (6,482 km)  
**Payload:** 220 passengers + baggage (22,000 kg total)  
**Reserves:** 45 min holding + 200 NM alternate + final reserve

**Range Performance:**
- Maximum Range (max fuel, reduced payload): 4,200 NM
- Economic Range (220 pax): 3,500 NM
- Minimum Range (max payload): 2,800 NM

## Mission Profile

**Typical Mission:**
1. Taxi: 10 min (200 kg H₂)
2. Takeoff and Climb: To FL410 (800 kg H₂)
3. Cruise: M0.78 at FL410 (5,500 kg H₂)
4. Descent: From FL410 (300 kg H₂)
5. Approach and Landing: 20 min (200 kg H₂)
6. Reserves: 700 kg H₂

**Total Fuel: 8,000 kg H₂ (usable)**

## Rationale

Design range ensures:
- Trans-continental route capability
- Key city-pair coverage (e.g., Paris-Dubai, London-New York)
- Market competitiveness
- ETOPS-capable for oceanic routes
- Operational flexibility
