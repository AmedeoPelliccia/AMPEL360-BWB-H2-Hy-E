# RQ-02-10-10-003: Airport Compatibility

**Requirement ID:** RQ-02-10-10-003  
**Category:** Dimensions  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Approved

## Requirement

The aircraft shall be compatible with ICAO Aerodrome Code E airports and standard ground support equipment without requiring special airport modifications.

**Airport Compatibility Requirements:**
- ICAO Aerodrome Reference Code: 4E
- Pavement Classification Number (PCN): Design ACN ≤ 85 (Flexible), ≤ 95 (Rigid)
- Gate compatibility: Standard Code E gates
- Taxiway width requirement: 44 m minimum
- Turning radius: Compatible with standard taxiway geometry
- Apron space: 90m × 90m minimum

**Ground Support Equipment:**
- Standard passenger boarding bridges (3-5 positions)
- Conventional baggage loaders
- Standard ground power units
- H₂ refueling infrastructure (specialized)
- Standard towing equipment (with adapters)

## Rationale

Airport compatibility ensures:
- Access to major international airports
- No infrastructure modifications required
- Standard ground handling procedures
- Operational flexibility
- Cost-effective ground operations
- Market viability

## Verification

- **Method:** Analysis and Demonstration
- **Procedure:** Airport compatibility assessment and ground trials
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Demonstrated compatibility with reference airports
  - PCN/ACN analysis approved
  - Gate fit verification completed
  - Ground equipment interface tested

## Compliance

- ICAO Annex 14 Volume I: Aerodrome Design and Operations
- ICAO Doc 9157: Aerodrome Design Manual
- CS-ADR-DSN: Certification Specifications for Aerodrome Design
- Local airport operating regulations
