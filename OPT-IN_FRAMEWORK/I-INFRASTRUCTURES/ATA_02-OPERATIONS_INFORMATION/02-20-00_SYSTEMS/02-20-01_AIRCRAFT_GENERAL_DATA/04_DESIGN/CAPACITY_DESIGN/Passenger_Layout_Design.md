# Passenger Layout Design

## Executive Summary

The AMPEL360 BWB's unique 38-meter-wide cabin enables innovative passenger layout designs that maximize comfort, operational flexibility, and CG control while maintaining emergency egress requirements.

## Layout Philosophy

### Design Drivers
- **Width Advantage:** 38m cabin width vs 5.5m conventional
- **CG Control:** Distributed loading across three zones
- **Comfort:** Wide-body experience throughout
- **Flexibility:** Multiple configuration options
- **Safety:** Enhanced egress with multiple aisle options

## Standard Configuration (220 Passengers)

### Zone A - Business Class (Forward)
**Location:** Stations 8-13m  
**Capacity:** 60 passengers  
**Layout:** 2-3-2-3 (10 abreast)

**Seat Specifications:**
- Pitch: 42 inches (1.07m)
- Width: 21 inches (0.53m)
- Recline: 8 inches (20cm)
- Type: Lie-flat capable

**Amenities:**
- Personal storage: Oversized bins
- Power: AC + USB-C per seat
- IFE: 18" screens
- Galley: Forward service

### Zone B - Economy Plus (Center)

**Seat Specifications:**

## Aisle Configuration

### Six-Aisle Design
1. **Outer Left:** Full length (emergency egress)
2. **Center-Left:** Full length (primary access)
3. **Center-Center Left:** Zones B & C
4. **Center-Center Right:** Zones B & C
5. **Center-Right:** Full length (primary access)
6. **Outer Right:** Full length (emergency egress)

**Aisle Width:** 20 inches (0.51m) standard, 24 inches (0.61m) outer

**Benefits:**
- Rapid boarding/deboarding
- Excellent emergency egress
- Enhanced passenger comfort
- Service efficiency

## Emergency Equipment

### Exit Distribution
- **Type A Exits:** 4 (forward & aft, both sides)
- **Type III Exits:** 8 (overwing, both sides)
- **Emergency Slides:** All exits
- **Evacuation Time:** < 90 seconds (all exits, half blocked)

- **Location:** Overhead bins at exits
- **Type:** Slide/raft combinations

- Exit signs: Photoluminescent
