## Detailed Analysis: 52m Selection

### Why NOT Larger (56m, 60m)?

**Airport Limitations:**
- Code F airports: Only 35-45% of network
- Major routes excluded
- Business case: NEGATIVE

**Economic Impact:**
- Lost revenue: >15%
- Efficiency gain: <5%
- Net benefit: NEGATIVE

**Conclusion:** Airport compatibility dominates

### Why NOT Smaller (48m)?

**Performance Penalty:**
- L/D: 19.5 (7% below target)
- Range: 7,450 NM (7% reduction)
- Fuel burn: +8%

**Competitive Position:**
- Not differentiated enough
- Operating cost: Higher
- Market acceptance: Questionable

**Conclusion:** Performance insufficient

### Optimal Choice: 52m

**Aerodynamic:**
- L/D = 21.0 exceeds target
- Induced drag: Well-managed
- Profile drag: Minimized

**Operational:**
- 87% airport compatibility
- ICAO Code E (broad access)
- Ground handling: Manageable

**Economic:**
- Best business case
- Structural weight: Acceptable
- Manufacturing: Feasible

**Risk:**
- Technology: Mature
- Certification: Clear path
- Schedule: Low risk
