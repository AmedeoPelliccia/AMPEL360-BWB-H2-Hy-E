# Thickness Distribution

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-02-11-00-DES-CB-004  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Thickness-to-Chord Ratio Distribution

**Design Philosophy:** Optimize structural depth while maintaining aerodynamic efficiency and internal volume.

## Distribution Profile

| Station | T/C | Height (m) | Primary Function |
|---------|-----|------------|------------------|
| Nose (0m) | 8% | 2.0 | Aerodynamic nose, avionics bay |
| Forward (7m) | 12% | 6.8 | Flight deck, nose gear well |
| Center Max (15m) | 15% | 8.5 | Passenger cabin, H₂ tanks |
| Aft Center (21m) | 13% | 7.4 | Galley, systems, tail cone |
| Transition (26m) | 9% | 3.2 | Wing blend, control surfaces |

## Design Rationale

### Aerodynamic Considerations

**Nose Region (T/C 8%):**
- Sharp nose for laminar flow
- Low drag at cruise
- Acceptable pitch moment

**Center Body (T/C 15%):**
- Adequate structural depth
- Pressure vessel efficiency
- Lift generation optimized
- Minimal drag penalty

**Aft Taper (T/C 13% to 9%):**
- Smooth pressure recovery
- Control surface integration
- Reduced interference drag

### Structural Requirements

**Maximum T/C 15% Selected:**
- Adequate spar depth (2.8m)
- Efficient pressure vessel
- Acceptable stress levels
- Fatigue life: 60,000 cycles

**Wing Box Depth:**
- Root: 2.8m (sufficient)
- Mid-span: 1.4m
- Tip: 0.3m
- Load paths optimized

### Internal Volume

**Pressure Vessel:**
- Upper deck: 4.2m high (H₂ tanks)
- Lower deck: 4.3m high (cabin)
- Total height: 8.5m (T/C 15%)

**Space Allocation:**
- Passenger cabin: 2.1m ceiling height
- H₂ tank clearance: 3.5m (insulated)
- Systems bays: As required
- Cargo holds: LD3-45 compatible

## Validation

**CFD Analysis:**
- Pressure distribution validated
- Drag prediction accurate
- L/D = 21.0 confirmed

**Structural Testing:**
- Full-scale pressure test
- Ultimate load: 1.5x limit
- Fatigue: 180,000 cycles (3x life)

**Decision:** Thickness distribution frozen  
**Status:** Production baseline
