## Length Selection

**Selected Length:** 28.5 meters (center body chord)

## Design Drivers

### Aerodynamic Chord

**BWB Planform Requirements:**
- Root chord drives lift distribution
- Optimal L/D at cruise
- Smooth blending to wing
- **28.5m provides optimal chord for 38m width**

### Cabin Layout

**Passenger Accommodation:**
- 220 seats in 2-class configuration
- Longitudinal seating zones
- Galley/lavatory placement
- Emergency exit distribution
- **28.5m accommodates all requirements**

### Structural Optimization

**Load Path Efficiency:**
- Wing box integration
- Landing gear placement
- Center of gravity range
- **28.5m optimal for structural efficiency**
