## Design Optimization

### Passenger Comfort

**Volume per Passenger: 3.1 m³**
- CS-25 minimum: 2.8 m³
- AMPEL360: +11% over minimum
- Competitive with wide-body aircraft

**Benefits:**
- Spacious cabin feel
- Reduced claustrophobia
- Better air quality (volume/person)

### Cargo Capacity

**Volume: 260 m³ total**
- LD3-45 containers: 16
- Revenue capacity: 22,000 kg
- Competitive with A321 (18,000 kg)

### H₂ Tank Integration

**Upper Deck Placement:**
- Available volume: 350 m³
- Required (8,000 kg LH₂): 132.5 m³
- **Margin: 164% (excellent)**

**Benefits:**
- Physical separation from cabin
- Easy refueling access (top surface)
- CG management flexibility
- Thermal management efficient
