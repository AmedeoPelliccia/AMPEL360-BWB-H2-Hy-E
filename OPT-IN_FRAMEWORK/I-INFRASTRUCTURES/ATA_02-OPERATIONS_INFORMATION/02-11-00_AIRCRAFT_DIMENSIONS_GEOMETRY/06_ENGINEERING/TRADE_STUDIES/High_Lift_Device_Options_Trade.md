# High-Lift Device Options Trade Study

**Trade Study ID:** 02-11-00-TS-005  
**Platform:** AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100  
**Status:** Draft  
**Date:** 2025-11-10

---

## 1. Objective

Select high-lift devices (leading and trailing edge) to achieve:
- **Take-off CLmax:** ≥ 1.8
- **Landing CLmax:** ≥ 2.4
- **Minimize weight and complexity**

---

## 2. Options Evaluated

### 2.1 Leading Edge Options

| Option | Device Type | CLmax Increment | Weight (kg) | Complexity | Score |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|------------|-------|
| LE-1 | Fixed leading edge | 0.0 | 0 | Low | 3 |
| LE-2 | Krueger flap | +0.3 | 1,200 | Medium | 7 |
| LE-3 | Slat | +0.4 | 1,500 | High | 9 |

### 2.2 Trailing Edge Options

| Option | Device Type | CLmax Increment | Weight (kg) | Complexity | Score |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|------------|-------|
| TE-1 | Plain flap | +0.6 | 800 | Low | 5 |
| TE-2 | Single-slotted flap | +0.9 | 1,400 | Medium | 8 |
| TE-3 | Double-slotted flap | +1.2 | 2,100 | High | 9 |

---

## 3. Configuration Matrix

| Config | LE Device | TE Device | CLmax (TO) | CLmax (LDG) | Total Weight (kg) | Score |
|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|-------------|-------------------|-------|
| A | Fixed | Plain | 1.4 | 1.8 | 800 | 4 |
| B | Krueger | Single-slot | 1.7 | 2.2 | 2,600 | 7 |
| C | Slat | Double-slot | 1.9 | 2.5 | 3,600 | 9 |

---

## 4. Recommendation

**Selected Configuration: C (Slats + Double-Slotted Flaps)**

- **LE device:** Slats (25° TO, 30° LDG)
- **TE device:** Double-slotted flaps (20° TO, 30° LDG)

**Rationale:**
- Achieves required CLmax for both take-off and landing
- Proven technology on large aircraft
- Acceptable weight penalty (3,600 kg)
