# Center Body Depth vs. Volume Trade Study

**Trade Study ID:** 02-11-00-TS-003  
**Platform:** AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100  
**Status:** Draft  
**Date:** 2025-11-10

---

## 1. Objective

Determine optimal center body depth balancing:
- Cabin volume and passenger comfort
- Structural efficiency (pressure vessel)
- Aerodynamic drag
- Integration with wing

---

## 2. Requirements

### 2.1 Cabin Requirements
- **Passengers:** 400 in dual-aisle layout
- **Headroom:** ≥ 2.1 m minimum cabin height
- **Cargo:** 80 m³ lower deck volume

### 2.2 Structural Requirements
- **Cabin pressure:** 8.0 psi differential
- **CS-25.365 compliance:** Adequate strength margins

---
