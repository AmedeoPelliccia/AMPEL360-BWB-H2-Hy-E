## Key Technologies

### CAOS (Computer Aided Operations & Services)

The fourth pillar of digital engineering after CAD, CAE, and CAM:

- **Real-Time Intelligence**: Continuous monitoring of 1000+ aircraft parameters
- **Predictive Capabilities**: 500 flight hours ahead failure prediction (85% accuracy)
- **Digital Twin**: Physics-based simulation with <100ms latency
- **Fleet Learning**: Cross-aircraft optimization and knowledge sharing
- **Human-AI Collaboration**: Advisory systems with crew override authority

### Neural Network Operations

Advanced AI systems supporting:

- Route optimization considering weather, traffic, and fuel efficiency
- Performance prediction and optimization
- Automated flight planning with regulatory compliance
- Crew decision support for complex scenarios
- Real-time performance monitoring and alerting

### Digital Twin Operations

Real-time digital representation enabling:

- Physics-based flight simulation
- Predictive performance calculations
- What-if scenario analysis
- Optimal trajectory planning
- System health monitoring
