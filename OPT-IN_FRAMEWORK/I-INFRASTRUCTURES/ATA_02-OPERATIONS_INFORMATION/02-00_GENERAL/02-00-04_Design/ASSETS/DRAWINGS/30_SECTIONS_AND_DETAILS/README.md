## Purpose

This directory contains **detailed construction drawings and sections**:

- Installation details for equipment mounting and anchoring
- Construction details for raised floors, cable management, and finishes
- Section cuts through critical areas showing vertical relationships
- Connection details between systems and disciplines
- Enlarged views of complex assemblies or interfaces

---

## Drawing Domain

All drawings in this directory use domain code: **DET** (Sections and details)

---

## Typical Content

Detail drawings typically include:

- Section views (vertical cuts) showing multi-level relationships
- Enlarged detail views at larger scales (1:10, 1:5, 1:1)
- Mounting and fixing details with fastener specifications
- Material callouts and finish specifications
- Dimensional tolerances and installation clearances
- Construction sequencing notes where relevant
- Coordination notes with other disciplines
- Waterproofing, fire-stopping, and safety details

---

## Naming Convention

```text
02-00-04-DWG-DET-[Location]-[DetailName]-[SheetID]-R[rev].[ext]
```

**Examples:**

- `02-00-04-DWG-DET-OPSCTR-ConsoleFixingDetail-SHT01-R01.dwg`
- `02-00-04-DWG-DET-DATACENTER-RaisedFloorDetail-SHT01-R01.dwg`
- `02-00-04-DWG-DET-H2ROOM-PipePenetrationDetail-SHT01-R01.dwg`

## Sidecar Descriptions

Each `.dwg` file should have a corresponding `.md` file with the same basename providing:

- Drawing identification and metadata
- Purpose and scope
- Source BIM models and references
- Content summary
- Assumptions and constraints
- Revision history
- Approval information

See `DRAWING_DESCRIPTION_TEMPLATE.md` in `00_ADMIN/` for the standard template.
