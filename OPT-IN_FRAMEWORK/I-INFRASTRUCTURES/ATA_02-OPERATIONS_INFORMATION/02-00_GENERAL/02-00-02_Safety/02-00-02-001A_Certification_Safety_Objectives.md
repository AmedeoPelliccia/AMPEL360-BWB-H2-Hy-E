# Certification Safety Objectives

## 1. Top-level Objectives
- Protect safe ground/flight **operations information** (accuracy, completeness, timeliness).
- Avoid misleading ops content that could degrade safety margins.
- Ensure clear **responsibility and change control** for published ops data.

## 2. Means of Compliance (MoC)
- FHA/CCA/FMEA/FTA analyses completed and approved.
- Human Factors assessment (crew workload, SA, error tolerance).
- Validation campaigns (ops scenarios, corner cases, degraded conditions).
