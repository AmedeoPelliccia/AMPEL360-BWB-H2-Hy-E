# Emergency Response Procedures (ERP)

- **Triggering events:** critical ops data inconsistency, airport constraints mismatch, weather/NOTAM conflicts.
- **Immediate actions:** freeze publication, issue bulletin to crews/MRO, revert to last approved dataset.
- **Escalation:** Safety Board, Certification focal, Airport liaison.
- **Communication pack:** templates for operator alerts, change notes, and EFB updates.
