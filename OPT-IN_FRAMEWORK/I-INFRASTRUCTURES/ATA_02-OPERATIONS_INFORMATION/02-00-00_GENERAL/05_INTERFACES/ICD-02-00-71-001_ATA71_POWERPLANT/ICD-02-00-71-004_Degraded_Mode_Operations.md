# ICD-02-00-71-004: Degraded Mode Operations
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Status:** Active

## Interface Description
Operations procedures and limitations with degraded fuel cell capability.

## Degradation Scenarios

### Single Stack Failure (3 of 4 operative)
- **Power Available**: 7.5 MW
- **Performance Impact**: Reduced climb performance
- **Operations**: Normal ops with restrictions
- **MEL**: Category B (3 days)

### Two Stack Failure (2 of 4 operative)
- **Power Available**: 5 MW
- **Performance Impact**: Significantly reduced
- **Operations**: Ferry flight only, no passengers
- **MEL**: Not allowed for revenue service

### Three Stack Failure (1 of 4 operative)
- **Power Available**: 2.5 MW + battery
- **Performance Impact**: Emergency power only
- **Operations**: Land immediately
- **MEL**: Aircraft grounded

## Operational Restrictions

**One Stack Inoperative:**
- Maximum altitude: FL350 (vs FL410 normal)
- Reduced MTOW: 90% normal
- Increased takeoff distance: +15%
- Extended flight planning required

**RQ-ICD-02-71-030:** Degraded mode automatic detection  
**RQ-ICD-02-71-031:** Crew notification immediate  
**RQ-ICD-02-71-032:** Performance limitations enforced
