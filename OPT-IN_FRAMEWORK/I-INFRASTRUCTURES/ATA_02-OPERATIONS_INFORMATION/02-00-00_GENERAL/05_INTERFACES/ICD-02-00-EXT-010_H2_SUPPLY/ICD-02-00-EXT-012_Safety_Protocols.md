# ICD-02-00-EXT-012: H2 Safety Protocols
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Status:** Active

## Interface Description
Safety protocols for hydrogen ground operations and refueling.

## Safety Zones
- **Exclusion Zone**: 50m radius during refueling
- **Restricted Zone**: 100m radius (no ignition sources)
- **Monitoring Zone**: 200m radius (leak detection)

## Personnel Requirements
- H2 safety training (initial + annual)
- Personal H2 detectors worn
- Fire-resistant clothing required
- Communications equipment (intrinsically safe)

## Emergency Procedures
- Emergency disconnect procedure
- Fire response (H2-specific)
- Evacuation procedures
- Communication protocols

**RQ-ICD-02-EXT-120:** Exclusion zone 50m enforced  
**RQ-ICD-02-EXT-121:** Personnel H2-safety certified
