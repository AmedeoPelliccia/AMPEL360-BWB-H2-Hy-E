# ICD-02-00-EXT-010: H2 Refueling Equipment
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Status:** Active

## Interface Description
Ground-based hydrogen refueling equipment specifications and interface requirements.

## Refueling Equipment Specifications
- **Capacity**: 10,000 kg LH2 minimum
- **Transfer Rate**: 100-150 kg/min
- **Pressure**: 5-8 bar delivery
- **Temperature**: -253°C (20K)
- **Connection**: SAE J2600 compatible

## Safety Equipment
- H2 leak detectors (0-100% LEL)
- Fire suppression (foam system)
- Emergency shutoff (manual + automatic)
- Grounding/bonding equipment
- Personnel protective equipment

## Compliance Standards
- ISO 19881: Hydrogen refueling stations
- SAE J2719: Hydrogen safety requirements
- NFPA 2: Hydrogen technologies code

**RQ-ICD-02-EXT-100:** ISO 19881 compliance  
**RQ-ICD-02-EXT-101:** Refueling time ≤45 minutes
