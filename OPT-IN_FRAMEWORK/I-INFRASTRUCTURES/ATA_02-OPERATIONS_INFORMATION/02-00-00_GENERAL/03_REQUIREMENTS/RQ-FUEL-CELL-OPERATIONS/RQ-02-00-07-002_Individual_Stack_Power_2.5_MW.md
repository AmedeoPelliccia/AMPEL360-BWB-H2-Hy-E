# RQ-02-00-07-002: Individual Stack Power 2.5 MW

## Requirement Details

**ID:** RQ-02-00-07-002  
**Category:** Fuel Cell Ops  
**Subcategory:** Power  
**Title:** Individual Stack Power 2.5 MW  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Status:** Approved

## Description

The PEM fuel cell propulsion system shall achieve individual stack power 2.5 mw for reliable electric power generation.

## Rationale

This fuel cell requirement ensures reliable, efficient electric power generation from hydrogen fuel.
