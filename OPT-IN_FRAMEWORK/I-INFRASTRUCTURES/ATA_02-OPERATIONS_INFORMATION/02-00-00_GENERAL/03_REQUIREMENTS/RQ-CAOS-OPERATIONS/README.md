# RQ-CAOS-OPERATIONS
## CAOS AI Operations Requirements

**Category ID:** 08  
**Requirement Range:** RQ-02-00-08-001 to RQ-02-00-08-025  
**Total Requirements:** 25

## Overview

Computer Aided Operations & Services (CAOS) requirements define the AI system capabilities, optimization targets, safety oversight, and human-AI interaction principles.

## Key Requirements

### Integration & Architecture (001-005)
- Digital Twin Real-Time Integration
- Synchronization: 100ms
- Predictive Analytics Enabled
- Neural Network Models: 20+ Active
- Fleet Learning Enabled

### Optimization Capabilities (006-011)
- Route Optimization: 3-7% improvement
- Fuel Optimization: 8-15% improvement
- Altitude Optimization: Continuous
- Speed Optimization: Real-Time
- Weather Avoidance AI
- Turbulence Prediction & Avoidance

### Prediction Accuracy (012-015)
- Fuel Burn: Within 2%
- Arrival Time: Within 2 minutes
- Maintenance: 500 FH advance warning
- Anomaly Detection: 85% accuracy

### Human Authority & Safety (016-022)
- Human Authority: Final Decision
- Override: Single Action
- AI Transparency with Explanations
- Confidence Levels Displayed
- Independent Safety Monitor
- Continuous Validation
- Decision Audit Trail

### Operations & Performance (023-025)
- Model Updates: Monthly maximum
- Performance Monitoring: Continuous
- Crew Workload Reduction: 30%
