# RQ-02-00-09-017: Training Effectiveness Measured

## Requirement Details

**ID:** RQ-02-00-09-017  
**Category:** Human Factors  
**Subcategory:** Training  
**Title:** Training Effectiveness Measured  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Approved

## Description

The aircraft design shall incorporate training effectiveness measured to optimize crew performance and safety.

## Rationale

This human factors requirement optimizes the human-machine interface and reduces crew workload and errors.

## Source

- **Document:** Training Requirements
- **Parent ATA:** 02-00-00
- **Criticality:** Operations

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Training curriculum developed and approved
2. Training materials and procedures documented
3. Instructor qualifications defined and met
4. Student competency assessment procedures established
5. Training records maintained per regulatory requirements
