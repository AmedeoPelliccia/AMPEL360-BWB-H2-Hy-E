# RQ-REGULATORY
## Regulatory Compliance Requirements

**Category ID:** 01  
**Requirement Range:** RQ-02-00-01-001 to RQ-02-00-01-020  
**Total Requirements:** 20

## Overview

This category contains all regulatory compliance requirements for the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA aircraft. These requirements ensure compliance with international aviation standards, hydrogen-specific regulations, and emerging AI regulations.

## Requirement Subcategories

### Airworthiness Standards (001-002)
- CS-25 (EASA) Compliance
- FAR-25 (FAA) Compliance

### International Standards (003-005)
- ICAO Annex 6 Operations
- EU 965/2012 Air Operations
- Safety Management System (SMS)

### Documentation Requirements (006-008)
- Aircraft Flight Manual (AFM)
- Flight Crew Operating Manual (FCOM)
- Minimum Equipment List (MEL)

### Operational Approvals (009-014)
- ETOPS 180min
- CAT II/III Approach
- RVSM
- TCAS II v7.1
- ADS-B Out
- CPDLC Datalink

### Hydrogen-Specific Standards (015-017)
- ISO 19881:2018 - Hydrogen Fuel Containers
- SAE J2719 - Hydrogen Fuel Quality
- NFPA 2:2020 - Hydrogen Technologies Code

### AI and Future Technology (018-019)
- EU AI Act Compliance
- EASA AI Roadmap

### Environmental Regulations (020)
- ICAO Annex 16 Environmental Protection
