# RQ-02-00-01-001: CS25 Airworthiness Compliance

## Requirement Details

**ID:** RQ-02-00-01-001  
**Category:** Regulatory  
**Subcategory:** CS-25  
**Title:** CS25 Airworthiness Compliance  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Status:** Approved

## Description

The aircraft shall comply with all applicable requirements of EASA Certification Specifications CS-25 for large aeroplanes, particularly CS-25.1581 regarding general aircraft operating information and flight crew operations manual requirements.

## Rationale

CS-25 compliance is mandatory for aircraft certification in Europe and many other jurisdictions. This is a foundational requirement for type certification.

## Source

- **Document:** EASA CS-25
- **Section:** 25.1581 - General
- **Effective Date:** Amendment 27

## Acceptance Criteria

1. All CS-25 requirements applicable to the aircraft are identified
2. Compliance demonstration methods defined for each requirement
3. Aircraft Flight Manual (AFM) complies with CS-25.1581
4. Type Certificate Data Sheet (TCDS) approved by EASA
