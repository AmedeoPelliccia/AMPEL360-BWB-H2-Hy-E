**Title:** CS25 Airworthiness Compliance  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Status:** Approved

## Description

The aircraft shall comply with all applicable requirements of [EASA](https://www.easa.europa.eu/) Certification Specifications [CS-25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes) for large aeroplanes, particularly [CS-25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes).1581 regarding general aircraft operating information and flight crew operations manual requirements.

## Rationale

[CS-25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes) compliance is mandatory for aircraft certification in Europe and many other jurisdictions. This is a foundational requirement for type certification.

- **Effective Date:** Amendment 27

## Acceptance Criteria

1. All [CS-25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes) requirements applicable to the aircraft are identified
2. Compliance demonstration methods defined for each requirement
3. Aircraft Flight Manual (AFM) complies with [CS-25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes).1581
4. Type Certificate Data Sheet (TCDS) approved by [EASA](https://www.easa.europa.eu/)
