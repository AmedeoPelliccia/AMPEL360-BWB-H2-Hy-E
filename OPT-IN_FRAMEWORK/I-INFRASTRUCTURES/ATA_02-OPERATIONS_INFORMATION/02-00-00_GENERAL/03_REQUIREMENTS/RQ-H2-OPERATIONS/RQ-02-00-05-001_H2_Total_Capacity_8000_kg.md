# RQ-02-00-05-001: H2 Total Capacity 8000 kg

## Requirement Details

**ID:** RQ-02-00-05-001  
**Category:** H2 Operations  
**Subcategory:** Capacity  
**Title:** H2 Total Capacity 8000 kg  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Status:** Approved

## Description

The hydrogen fuel system shall meet the requirement for h2 total capacity 8000 kg to enable safe and efficient hydrogen operations.

## Rationale

This hydrogen-specific requirement is necessary for safe handling and operation of cryogenic liquid hydrogen fuel.
