# RQ-02-00-05-012: Leak Detection Operational Before Refueling

## Requirement Details

**ID:** RQ-02-00-05-012  
**Category:** H2 Operations  
**Subcategory:** Safety  
**Title:** Leak Detection Operational Before Refueling  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Status:** Approved

## Description

The hydrogen fuel system shall meet the requirement for leak detection operational before refueling to enable safe and efficient hydrogen operations.

## Rationale

This hydrogen-specific requirement is necessary for safe handling and operation of cryogenic liquid hydrogen fuel.
