## Description

The hydrogen fuel system shall meet the requirement for personnel h2 certification 8 hours minimum to enable safe and efficient hydrogen operations.

## Rationale

This hydrogen-specific requirement is necessary for safe handling and operation of cryogenic liquid hydrogen fuel.
