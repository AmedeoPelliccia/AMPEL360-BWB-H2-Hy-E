# Audit Trail Policy

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-02-00-00-META-ATP  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective Date:** 2025-09-01

## Purpose
Establish audit trail requirements for complete traceability of all documentation activities.

## Scope
All document access, modifications, approvals, and distributions are logged.

## Retention
- Active logs: 2 years online
- Historical logs: 10 years archive

## Log Types
1. Document Access Log
2. Document Modification Log
3. Document Approval Log
4. Document Distribution Log
5. User Activity Log
6. System Changes Log

## Compliance
- ISO 9001: Records control
- AS9100: Configuration management
- 21 CFR Part 11: Electronic records (if applicable)
