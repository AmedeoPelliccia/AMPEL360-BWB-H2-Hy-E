# Review and Approval Workflow

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-02-00-00-META-RAW  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective Date:** 2025-09-01

## Purpose
Defines the review and approval workflow for all ATA 02-00-00 documentation.

## Workflow Stages
1. Author submits document for review
2. Technical review (minimum 2 reviewers)
3. Safety review (if safety-critical)
4. Regulatory review (if certification-related)
5. Quality review (standards compliance)
6. Final approval by authority
7. Release to controlled repository

## Review Timeline
- Technical Review: 5 business days
- Safety Review: 3 business days
- Regulatory Review: 7 business days
- Quality Review: 2 business days
- Final Approval: 3 business days
