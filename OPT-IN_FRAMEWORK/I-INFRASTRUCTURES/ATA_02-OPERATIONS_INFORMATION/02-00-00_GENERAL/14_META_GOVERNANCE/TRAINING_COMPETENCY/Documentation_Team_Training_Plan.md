# Documentation Team Training Plan

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-02-00-00-META-TTP  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective Date:** 2025-09-01

## Purpose
Establish training requirements for all documentation personnel.

## Required Training

### All Documentation Personnel
- Document control procedures (4 hours)
- Quality standards overview (2 hours)
- Tool training (DMS, version control) (4 hours)

### Authors
- S1000D authoring (16 hours + certification)
- ATA iSpec 2200 standards (8 hours)
- Technical writing best practices (8 hours)

### Reviewers
- Review procedures and techniques (8 hours)
- Standards compliance checking (4 hours)

### Quality Auditors
- Audit methodology (16 hours)
- Standards deep dive (16 hours)

## Training Schedule
- Initial training: Within 30 days of role assignment
- Refresher training: Annual
- Competency assessment: Every 2 years
