## 9. OPERATIONAL APPROVALS

### 9.1 ETOPS (Extended Range Operations)

**Requirement:** ETOPS 180 approval for trans-oceanic routes

**Compliance Requirements:**
- Proven reliability (fuel cells, H2 system)
- Two-communication systems
- Crew training (ETOPS-specific)
- Maintenance program enhancements
- MEL considerations

**AMPEL360 ETOPS:**
- Target: ETOPS 180 at entry into service
- Fuel cell redundancy (4 stacks, 3 required)
- H2 system redundancy
- CAOS predictive maintenance

### 9.2 Low Visibility Operations (LVO)

**Approvals:** CAT II and CAT III

**Requirements:**
- Aircraft equipment (autoland, HUD)
- Ground equipment (ILS Cat II/III)
- Crew training and qualification
- Operations manual procedures
- Maintenance program

**AMPEL360 LVO:**
- CAT IIIb capable (RVR 75m)
- Dual HUD (Head-Up Display)
- CAOS-assisted approach monitoring
- Auto-land capable

### 9.3 Performance-Based Navigation (PBN)

**Capabilities:**
- RNP 0.3 (Terminal)
- RNP 1 (En-route)
- RNP AR (Authorization Required)
- RNP APCH (Approach)

**Benefits:**
- Optimized routing
- CAOS route optimization
- Reduced ATC workload
- Fuel efficiency
