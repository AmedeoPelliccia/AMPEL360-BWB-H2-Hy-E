## Concerns Raised

1. **CAOS Dependency**
   - Concern: Over-reliance on CAOS
   - Response: Emphasized pilot authority, manual procedures always available

2. **H2 Safety**
   - Concern: Adequate emergency training
   - Response: Enhanced H2 safety training, realistic scenarios

3. **BWB Handling**
   - Concern: Different from conventional aircraft
   - Response: Extended BWB-specific training, more simulator time

4. **Workload**
   - Concern: Information overload
   - Response: Simplified displays, customization options
