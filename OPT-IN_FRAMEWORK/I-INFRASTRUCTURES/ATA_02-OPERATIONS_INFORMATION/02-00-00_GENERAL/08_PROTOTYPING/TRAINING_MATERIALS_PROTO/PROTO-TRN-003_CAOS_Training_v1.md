## CAOS System Training Course

### Course Overview
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)
- **Target:** Flight crew transitioning to AMPEL360
- **Level:** Foundational to Advanced
- **Certification:** CAOS Operator Qualification

## Day 1: CAOS Fundamentals

### Session 1: Introduction to CAOS (2 hours)

**What is CAOS?**
- Computer Aided Operations & Services
- Fourth pillar of digital engineering (CAD, CAE, CAM, CAOS)
- AI-powered flight operations assistant
- Philosophy: Advisory, not control

**Key Capabilities**
- Predictive maintenance (85% accuracy, 500 hours ahead)
- Real-time optimization
- Digital twin simulation (<100ms latency)
- Fleet learning
- Explainable AI decisions

**Human-AI Collaboration Model**
- Pilot retains final authority
- CAOS provides recommendations
- Override capability always available
- Transparency in decision-making
- Trust building through explainability
