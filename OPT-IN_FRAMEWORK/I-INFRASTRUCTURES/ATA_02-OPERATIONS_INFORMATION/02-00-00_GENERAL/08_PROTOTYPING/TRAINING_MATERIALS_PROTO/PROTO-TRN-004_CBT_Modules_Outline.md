## Computer-Based Training (CBT) Module Outline

### Overview
Interactive e-learning modules for self-paced study, designed to complement classroom and simulator training.

**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Prerequisites:** None

**Content:**

- Revolutionary technologies

- H₂ propulsion advantages

**Interactive Elements:**

- Knowledge check questions (10)
