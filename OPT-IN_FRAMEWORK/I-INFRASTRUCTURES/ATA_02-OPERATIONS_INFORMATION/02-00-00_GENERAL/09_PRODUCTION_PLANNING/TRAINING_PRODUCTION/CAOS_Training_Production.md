# CAOS Training Production Plan

**System:** Computer Aided Operations & Services (CAOS)  
**Target Audience:** All operational personnel  
**Production Period:** 2025-12-01 to 2026-06-30  
**Status:** Planning  
**Owner:** Training / CAOS_Team

## Overview
Training program for the revolutionary CAOS AI-powered operations system. Ensures all personnel can effectively use, trust, and override CAOS advisory functions.

## Training Philosophy
### Human-AI Collaboration
- CAOS as advisory system, not autopilot
- Human retains ultimate authority
- Trust calibration is critical
- Understand system limitations
- Know when to override
- Effective communication with AI

### Competency-Based Approach
- Practical system use
- Scenario-based learning
- Decision-making exercises
- Override practice
- System limitation awareness
- Continuous learning mindset

## Risks and Mitigation
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| CAOS software delays | High | Simulator version sufficient for initial training |
| User resistance to AI | Medium | Strong change management, demonstrate value |
| Over-reliance on CAOS | High | Trust calibration emphasis, override training |
| System changes during training | Medium | Flexible content, version control |
| Instructor readiness | Medium | Early instructor training, CAOS experts |
