# Instructor Training Plan

**Target Audience:** Type Rating Instructors, Simulator Instructors  
**Production Period:** 2026-03-01 to 2026-04-15  
**Status:** Planning  
**Owner:** Training / Chief_Instructor

## Overview
Comprehensive training plan for instructors who will deliver AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E training programs. Ensures instructors have deep technical knowledge, instructional skills, and regulatory compliance.

## Instructor Categories
### Type Rating Instructors (TRI)
- Ground school instruction
- Simulator instruction
- Aircraft instruction
- Competency assessment
- EASA/FAA TRI qualified

### Simulator Instructors (SFI)
- Simulator-only instruction
- Scenario management
- Debriefing skills
- EASA/FAA SFI qualified

### Ground Instructors
- Classroom instruction
- CBT facilitation
- Knowledge assessment
- Subject matter expertise

## Training Program Structure
### Phase 1: Technical Training (40 hours)
#### Aircraft Systems Deep Dive
- BWB aerodynamics and structures (8 hours)
- H₂ fuel system comprehensive (8 hours)
- Fuel cell propulsion detailed (6 hours)
- Flight control systems (6 hours)
- CAOS system comprehensive (8 hours)
- All other systems (4 hours)

#### Operations and Performance
- Flight operations (4 hours)
- Performance calculations (4 hours)
- Weight and balance (2 hours)
- Fuel planning (H₂) (2 hours)
- Emergency procedures (4 hours)

### Phase 2: Instructional Skills (24 hours)
#### Teaching Methodology
- Adult learning principles (4 hours)
- Instructional techniques (4 hours)
- Scenario-based training (4 hours)
- Competency-based assessment (4 hours)
- Effective debriefing (4 hours)
- Human factors in training (4 hours)

### Phase 3: Simulator Training (32 hours)
#### Simulator Familiarization
- Instructor operating station (8 hours)
- Scenario management (8 hours)
- Failure insertion techniques (4 hours)
- Freeze/reposition functions (4 hours)
- Recording and playback (4 hours)
- Troubleshooting (4 hours)

### Phase 4: Supervised Practice (40 hours)
#### Teaching Under Supervision
- Ground school delivery (8 hours)
- Simulator scenario delivery (24 hours)
- Debriefing practice (8 hours)

### Phase 5: Standardization (16 hours)
#### Instructor Standardization
- Training standards review (4 hours)
- Evaluation criteria standardization (4 hours)
- Mock check rides (4 hours)
- Final standardization check (4 hours)

## Instructor Qualification Requirements
### Prerequisites
- ATPL (Airline Transport Pilot License)
- Minimum 2,000 flight hours
- Previous type rating instructor experience preferred
- Current TRI or SFI rating
- H₂ systems familiarization
- CAOS system orientation

### Competencies Required
- Deep technical knowledge
- Effective communication
- Scenario management
- Student assessment
- Debriefing skills
- Continuous learning mindset
