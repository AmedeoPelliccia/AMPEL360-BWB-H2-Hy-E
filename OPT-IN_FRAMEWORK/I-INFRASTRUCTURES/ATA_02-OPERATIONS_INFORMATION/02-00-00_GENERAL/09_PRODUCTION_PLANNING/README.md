## Key Production Streams

### 1. Documentation Production
Technical publications development following S1000D standards:
- **AFM** (Aircraft Flight Manual) - 450 pages
- **FCOM** (Flight Crew Operating Manual) - 800 pages
- **QRH** (Quick Reference Handbook) - 120 pages
- **WBM** (Weight and Balance Manual) - 200 pages
- **MEL** (Minimum Equipment List) - 180 pages

### 2. Training Production
Comprehensive training programs:
- Type Rating Course (42 days)
- H₂ Systems Training (multiple audience levels)
- CAOS Training (20 hours CBT + practical)
- CBT Modules (25 hours total)
- Simulator Scenarios (60 scenarios)
- Instructor Training Program

### 3. Procedure Rollout
Operational procedures deployment:
- Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
- Emergency Procedures
- Checklists (normal, abnormal, emergency)
- Digital Procedures (EFB integration)
- Update Management Process

### 4. CAOS Deployment
AI-powered operations system:
- Software rollout (v1.0)
- Digital Twin implementation
- Fleet integration (phased)
- Ground systems deployment
- Training rollout

### 5. Quality Control
Ensuring excellence:
- Documentation review processes
- Translation quality (French, German, Spanish)
- Technical accuracy verification
- Format standards compliance (S1000D)
- Change control procedures

### 6. Distribution
Multi-channel distribution:
- Digital distribution (EFB, CAOS, Portal)
- Electronic Flight Bag loading
- Print production
- Update distribution processes
- Distribution matrix management

### 7. Resource Management
Planning and tracking:
- Personnel (15+ FTE)
- Technical writers and SMEs
- Software tools and infrastructure
- Budget tracking ($2.78M total)

### 8. Milestones
Program oversight:
- Production milestones tracking
- Delivery schedule management
- Critical path analysis
- Risk mitigation planning
