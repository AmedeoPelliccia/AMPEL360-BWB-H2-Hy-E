# Translation Quality Plan

**Languages:** French, German, Spanish (initial)

## Translation Scope
- AFM (Flight Manual)
- FCOM (Operations Manual)
- QRH (Quick Reference)
- Training materials

## Translation Process
1. Source text finalization
2. Translation by certified translators
3. Technical review by bilingual SMEs
4. Back-translation verification
5. Final quality check
6. Publication

## Quality Standards
- Aviation terminology accuracy
- Technical precision
- Cultural appropriateness
- Regulatory compliance
- Consistency across documents

## Quality Metrics
- Translation accuracy >99%
- SME approval rate >95%
- Turnaround time adherence
- Cost efficiency
