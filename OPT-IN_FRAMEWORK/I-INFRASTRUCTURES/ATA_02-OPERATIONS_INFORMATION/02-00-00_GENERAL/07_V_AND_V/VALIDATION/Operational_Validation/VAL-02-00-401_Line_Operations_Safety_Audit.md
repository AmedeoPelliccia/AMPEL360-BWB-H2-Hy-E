## 1. Test Objective

Validate operational safety through Line Operations Safety Audit (LOSA) in actual service environment with launch customer.

---

## 2. Test Scope

**Duration:** 6 months  
**Target Flights:** 500+ operational flights  
**Observation Method:** Non-jeopardy LOSA methodology

### 2.1 Observation Areas
- Threat and error management
- Procedural compliance
- Crew performance
- Safety culture
- Normal operations
- Non-normal situations

---

## 3. Success Criteria

- Zero Category A/B safety events
- Threat management effectiveness >95%
- Error management effectiveness >90%
- Procedural compliance >98%
- Acceptable safety culture indicators

---

## 4. LOSA Methodology

### 4.1 Observation Protocol
- Trained LOSA observers
- Non-punitive environment
- Confidential data collection
- Crew debriefs
- Data analysis and reporting

### 4.2 Threat Categories
- Environmental (weather, terrain, ATC)
- Airline (scheduling, dispatch, maintenance)
- Aircraft (malfunctions, equipment)

### 4.3 Error Categories
- Aircraft handling
- Procedural
- Communication
- Proficiency
- Operational decision making
