# H₂ Systems Operations

## H₂ Fuel Management
### Tank Selection
- CAOS automatically sequences tanks
- Manual override available
- Monitor pressures and temperatures

### Refueling
- Ground crew operation only
- Automated safety interlocks
- Typical refueling time: 15-20 minutes

### Fuel Monitoring
- Continuous quantity indication
- Pressure and temperature monitoring
- Leak detection system active

## Fuel Cell Operations
### Normal Operation
- All 4 fuel cells online
- Load distributed automatically
- Efficiency optimized by CAOS

### Single Fuel Cell Operation
- Minimum 2 fuel cells for flight
- Performance degraded with <3 cells
- Land at nearest suitable airport

## H₂ Safety
### Normal Operations
- System fully automated and safe
- Multiple redundant safety systems
- Continuous leak monitoring

### Abnormal Indications
- H₂ LEAK - Follow QRH immediately
- High Pressure - CAOS will manage
- Low Temp - Monitor trend

### Emergency Procedures
- Refer to AFM Section 3
- Fuel cells can be shut down individually
- Battery provides emergency power
