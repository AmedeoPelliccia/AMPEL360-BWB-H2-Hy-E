- Pressure: 350-700 bar
- Temperature: -253°C (20K)
- Automatic tank sequencing by CAOS

- Total power: 10 MW
- Efficiency: 60% (typical)
- Only byproduct: pure water

## 3. Electrical Power
- Primary: Fuel cells
- Backup: Lithium-ion batteries (500 kWh)

## 4. CAOS System

- Digital twin integration
- Predictive maintenance
- Fleet learning capability

## 5. Flight Controls

- BWB-optimized control laws
- CAOS-enhanced performance
