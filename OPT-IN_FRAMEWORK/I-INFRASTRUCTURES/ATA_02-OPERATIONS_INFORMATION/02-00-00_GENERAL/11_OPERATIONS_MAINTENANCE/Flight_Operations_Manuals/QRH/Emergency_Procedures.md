# Emergency Procedures

## Rapid Depressurization
**Memory Items:**
□ Oxygen Masks - DON, 100%  
□ Crew Communication - ESTABLISH  
□ Descent - INITIATE (Emergency)  
□ Nearest Airport - IDENTIFY

## Engine Fire (N/A - Fuel Cell Aircraft)
See FUEL CELL FIRE procedure

## Electrical Emergency
**Condition:** Multiple fuel cell failures

**Memory Items:**
□ Battery Power - ON  
□ Load Shed - Per ECAM  
□ Declare Emergency  
□ Land Immediately

## Emergency Descent
□ Thrust - IDLE  
□ Spoilers - FULL  
□ Descend - MAX RATE  
□ Target Altitude - 10,000 ft or MEA

## Emergency Landing
□ CAOS - NEAREST AIRPORT  
□ Fuel Remaining - ASSESS  
□ Declare Emergency  
□ Follow CAOS guidance or standard procedures
