# H₂ Emergency Procedures

## H₂ LEAK DETECTED

**Memory Items:**
□ H₂ Master Switch - OFF  
□ Affected Fuel Cell - OFF  
□ Ventilation - MAX  
□ Land ASAP

**Additional Actions:**
- Assess remaining power capability
- Calculate diversion fuel
- Declare emergency if required
- Avoid ignition sources

## FUEL CELL FIRE

**Memory Items:**
□ Affected Fuel Cell - OFF  
□ Fire Suppression - ACTIVATE  
□ H₂ to Affected Cell - ISOLATE  
□ Declare Emergency  
□ Land Immediately

## MULTIPLE FUEL CELL FAILURE

**Memory Items:**
□ Battery Power - ON  
□ Essential Systems Only  
□ Declare Emergency  
□ Immediate Landing Required

**Note:** With 2+ fuel cells, flight can continue to nearest suitable airport

## H₂ HIGH PRESSURE

**Condition:** Tank pressure >750 bar

□ CAOS Advisory - MONITOR  
□ Pressure Relief - AUTOMATIC  
□ Affected Tank - ISOLATE if required  
□ Monitor pressure trend

## H₂ LOW QUANTITY

**Condition:** H₂ fuel below minimum

□ CAOS - NEAREST H₂ STATION  
□ Declare Emergency if critical  
□ Prepare for battery-only landing if necessary
