# H₂ Systems Training
## Specialized Training for Hydrogen Aircraft

**Course Code:** H2-TRAIN-001  
**Duration:** 3 days  
**Target:** Pilots, maintenance personnel

---

## Day 1: H₂ Fundamentals

### Module 1: Hydrogen Properties (4 hours)
- Physical and chemical properties
- Cryogenic characteristics
- Flammability and safety
- Comparison with conventional fuels

### Module 2: H₂ Safety (4 hours)
- Hazards and risks
- Safety precautions
- PPE requirements
- Emergency response
- Leak detection and response

## Day 2: H₂ Aircraft Systems

### Module 3: H₂ Storage and Distribution (4 hours)
- Cryogenic tank design
- Insulation systems
- Pressure management
- Flow distribution
- Boil-off management

### Module 4: Fuel Cell Propulsion (4 hours)
- PEM fuel cell operation
- Power generation
- System integration
- Performance characteristics

## Day 3: Operations and Maintenance

### Module 5: Operational Procedures (4 hours)
- Refueling procedures
- Pre-flight inspections
- In-flight monitoring
- Emergency procedures

### Module 6: Maintenance Practices (4 hours)
- Maintenance requirements
- Inspection techniques
- Troubleshooting
- Documentation requirements

## Assessment
- Written exam (90% pass)
- Practical assessment
- Safety procedure demonstration

## Recurrent Training (Annual, 1 day)
- Safety review
- Procedure updates
- Incident/accident review
- Hands-on practice
