## 2. AI Trustworthiness Principles

### 2.1 Human Agency and Oversight
- **CAOS Implementation:**
  - Crew maintains final authority
  - Single-action override
  - Clear AI/human role distinction
- **Status:** Implemented and validated

### 2.2 Technical Robustness and Safety
- **CAOS Implementation:**
  - Independent safety monitor
  - Continuous validation
  - Cybersecurity measures
  - Fault tolerance
- **Status:** Active validation

### 2.3 Privacy and Data Governance
- **CAOS Implementation:**
  - Data protection compliance
  - Secure data handling
  - Access control
- **Status:** Complete

### 2.4 Transparency
- **CAOS Implementation:**
  - Explainable AI (XAI) framework
  - Confidence levels displayed
  - Decision rationale provided
- **Status:** Implemented

### 2.5 Diversity, Non-discrimination, Fairness
- **CAOS Implementation:**
  - Training data diversity
  - Bias testing and mitigation
  - Performance monitoring across scenarios
- **Status:** Active monitoring

### 2.6 Societal and Environmental Wellbeing
- **CAOS Implementation:**
  - Fuel optimization (environmental)
  - Safety enhancement (societal)
  - Crew workload reduction
- **Status:** Performance validated

### 2.7 Accountability
- **CAOS Implementation:**
  - Complete audit trail
  - Decision traceability
  - Responsibility framework
- **Status:** Implemented
