**Document Control:**

---

## 1. EU AI Act Overview

**Regulation (EU) 2024/1689** - Artificial Intelligence Act

CAOS is classified as a **high-risk AI system** under Annex III (aviation safety).

---

## 2. High-Risk AI Requirements

### 2.1 Risk Management System (Article 9)
- Continuous risk identification and mitigation
- Testing throughout AI lifecycle
- Post-market monitoring
- **Status:** Implemented

### 2.2 Data Governance (Article 10)
- Training data quality and relevance
- Data bias mitigation
- Data provenance tracking
- **Status:** Implemented

### 2.3 Technical Documentation (Article 11)
- AI system design and capabilities
- Development methodology
- Validation and testing results
- **Status:** Active development

### 2.4 Record-Keeping (Article 12)
- Automatic logging of AI operations
- Decision audit trail
- Traceability requirements
- **Status:** Implemented

### 2.5 Transparency (Article 13)
- Human-readable AI explanations
- Confidence levels displayed
- Limitations communicated
- **Status:** Implemented in CAOS UI

### 2.6 Human Oversight (Article 14)
- Crew maintains final authority
- Single-action override capability
- AI recommendations clearly distinguished
- **Status:** Implemented and validated

### 2.7 Accuracy and Robustness (Article 15)
- Performance monitoring
- Resilience to errors
- Cybersecurity measures
- **Status:** Continuous validation program
