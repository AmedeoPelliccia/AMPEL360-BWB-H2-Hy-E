## 10. Notes

### Note 1: H₂ Fuel System
Special conditions SC-H2-001 through SC-H2-010 apply. Refueling must comply with EN 17127 protocols. Personnel must be H₂-certified (8 hours minimum training).

### Note 2: BWB Configuration
Special conditions SC-BWB-001 through SC-BWB-007 apply. CG management critical - real-time CG calculation system required. Ground clearance monitoring system mandatory.

### Note 3: CAOS AI System
Special conditions SC-AI-001 through SC-AI-004 apply. Crew maintains final authority. Single-action override capability required. AI transparency requirements per EU AI Act.

### Note 4: Operations
This aircraft requires type-specific training including BWB handling, H₂ systems, and CAOS operations.
