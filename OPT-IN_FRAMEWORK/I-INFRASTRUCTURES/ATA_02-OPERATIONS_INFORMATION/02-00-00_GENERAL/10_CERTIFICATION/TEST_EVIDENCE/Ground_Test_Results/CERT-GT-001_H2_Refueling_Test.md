# CERT-GT-001: H₂ Refueling Test
## AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA

**Document Control:**
- Version: 1.0
- Status: Planning
- Test Date: 2026-Q2 (planned)

---

## 1. Test Objective

Validate H₂ refueling procedures, safety systems, and operational timeline.

---

## 2. Test Scope

### 2.1 Refueling Performance
- Refueling time target: ≤45 minutes
- Flow rate target: 120 kg/min
- Total capacity: 8000 kg
- Usable fuel verification: ≥7850 kg

### 2.2 Safety Systems
- Leak detection system verification (8 sensors/zone)
- Grounding system verification (≤3 milliohm)
- Emergency shutdown testing
- Safety zone procedures (50m radius)

### 2.3 Procedures
- Connection procedures (EN 17127 protocol)
- Pre-refueling checks
- During-refueling monitoring
- Post-refueling procedures (10-minute cooldown)
- Emergency disconnect procedures

---

## 3. Test Plan

### 3.1 Test Conditions
- Location: Ground test facility
- Temperature: ISA conditions
- Fuel quality: SAE J2719 compliant
- Personnel: H₂ certified (8 hours minimum)
- Equipment: Fire suppression on standby

### 3.2 Test Sequence
1. Pre-refueling inspection and setup
2. Safety zone establishment
3. Grounding verification
4. Leak detection system check
5. Refueling connection
6. Fuel transfer and monitoring
7. Completion and cooldown
8. Post-refueling inspection

### 3.3 Success Criteria
- Refueling time ≤45 minutes: PASS/FAIL
- Flow rate 120 kg/min ±10%: PASS/FAIL
- Zero leaks detected: PASS/FAIL
- Usable fuel ≥7850 kg: PASS/FAIL
- All safety systems functional: PASS/FAIL
- Procedures executable: PASS/FAIL
