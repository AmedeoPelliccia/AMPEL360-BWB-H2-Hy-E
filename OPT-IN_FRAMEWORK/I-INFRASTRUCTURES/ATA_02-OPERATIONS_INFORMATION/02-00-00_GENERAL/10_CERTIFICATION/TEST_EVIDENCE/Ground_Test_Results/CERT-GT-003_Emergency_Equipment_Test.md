# CERT-GT-003: Emergency Equipment Test
## AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA

**Document Control:**
- Version: 1.0
- Status: Planning
- Test Date: 2026-Q4 (planned)

---

## 1. Test Objective

Validate emergency evacuation systems and procedures for BWB wide-body cabin configuration.

---

## 2. Test Scope

### 2.1 Evacuation Systems
- Emergency exit functionality
- Evacuation slides deployment
- Emergency lighting
- Exit signage and marking

### 2.2 Evacuation Procedures
- 90-second evacuation demonstration
- Wide-body cabin evacuation flow
- Emergency exit distribution effectiveness
- Crew procedures validation

---

## 3. Test Plan

### 3.1 Equipment
- Full-scale cabin mockup
- Functional emergency exits
- Evacuation slides
- Participants: 220 passengers + crew

### 3.2 Test Sequence
1. Participants briefed (minimal emergency info)
2. Emergency scenario initiated
3. Evacuation commenced
4. Time to full evacuation measured
5. Flow patterns analyzed
6. Exit effectiveness evaluated

### 3.3 Success Criteria
- Total evacuation time ≤90 seconds: PASS/FAIL
- All exits usable: PASS/FAIL
- No injuries during evacuation: PASS/FAIL
- Procedures executable: PASS/FAIL
