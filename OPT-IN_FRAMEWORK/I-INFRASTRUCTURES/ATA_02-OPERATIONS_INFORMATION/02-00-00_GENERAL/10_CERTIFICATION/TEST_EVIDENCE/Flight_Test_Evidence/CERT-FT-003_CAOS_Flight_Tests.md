## 1. Test Objective

Validate CAOS AI system performance, human-machine interface, and safety features in flight operations.

---

## 2. Test Scope

### 2.1 CAOS Functions
- Route optimization (3-7% improvement target)
- Fuel optimization (8-15% improvement target)
- Weather avoidance
- Turbulence prediction
- Anomaly detection (85% accuracy target)
- Predictive maintenance alerts

### 2.2 Human Factors
- Crew workload assessment (30% reduction target)
- Situational awareness evaluation
- Trust calibration
- Override capability testing
- AI transparency validation

### 2.3 Safety Systems
- Independent safety monitor operation
- Override mechanism functionality
- Graceful degradation testing
- Failure mode testing

---

## 3. Test Plan

### 3.1 Flight Scenarios
- Normal operations (100 flights)
- Optimization scenarios (50 flights)
- Weather avoidance (25 flights)
- Emergency scenarios (25 flights)
- CAOS unavailable scenarios (10 flights)

### 3.2 Pilot-in-the-Loop Testing
- Test pilots: 20 pilots (various experience)
- Scenarios: Routine, challenging, emergency
- Evaluation: Workload, SA, trust, usability

### 3.3 Success Criteria
- CAOS performance targets met: PASS/FAIL
- Human factors validated: PASS/FAIL
- Safety systems functional: PASS/FAIL
- Override effective: PASS/FAIL
- Pilot acceptance achieved: PASS/FAIL
