## 1. Applicable Standards

### 1.1 EN 17127
European standard for hydrogen refueling protocols (aviation adaptation)

### 1.2 ISO 19880-1
Gaseous hydrogen fueling stations (ground infrastructure)

---

## 2. Refueling Requirements

### 2.1 Specifications
- Refueling time: ≤45 minutes
- Flow rate: 120 kg/min
- Total capacity: 8000 kg
- Usable fuel: 7850 kg minimum

### 2.2 Safety
- Safety zone: 50m radius
- Grounding resistance: ≤3 milliohm
- Fire equipment on standby
- Personnel H₂ certification: 8 hours minimum
- Leak detection operational before refueling

### 2.3 Quality Control
- SAE J2719 fuel quality verification
- Contamination monitoring
- Quality certification documentation

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Pre-Refueling
- Ground safety zone establishment
- Aircraft grounding verification
- Leak detection system check
- Fire equipment positioning
- Personnel protective equipment

### 3.2 Refueling Process
- Connection procedures (EN 17127)
- Flow rate monitoring
- Temperature monitoring
- Leak detection active
- Cooldown period: 10 minutes post-refuel

### 3.3 Emergency Procedures
- Leak response
- Connection failure
- Emergency disconnect
- Emergency defueling (if required)

---

**Status:** EN 17127 protocols adopted with aviation-specific procedures
