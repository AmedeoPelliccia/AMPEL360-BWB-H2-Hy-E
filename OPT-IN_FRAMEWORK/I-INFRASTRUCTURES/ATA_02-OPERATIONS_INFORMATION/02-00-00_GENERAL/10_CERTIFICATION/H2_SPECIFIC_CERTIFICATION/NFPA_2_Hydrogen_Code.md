## 1. Standard Overview

**NFPA 2:2020** - Hydrogen Technologies Code

Safety baseline for H₂ systems applied to aircraft design and operations.

---

## 2. Applicable Sections

### 2.1 General Safety Requirements
- Risk assessment and hazard analysis
- Defense-in-depth safety architecture
- Emergency response planning

### 2.2 System Design
- Leak detection and monitoring
- Ventilation systems (10/50 air changes per hour)
- Fire detection and suppression
- Emergency shutdown systems

### 2.3 Operational Safety
- Personnel training and certification
- Safety procedures and protocols
- Emergency response procedures
- Maintenance safety requirements

---

## 3. Aviation-Specific Considerations

### 3.1 Airborne Environment
- Altitude effects on H₂ behavior
- Confined space management
- Emergency egress considerations

### 3.2 Ground Operations
- Refueling safety zones (50m radius)
- Fire equipment requirements
- Personnel protective equipment
- Grounding and bonding (≤3 milliohm)
