## 1. Standard Overview

**ISO 19881:2018** - Gaseous hydrogen — Land vehicle fuel containers

This standard is adapted for aviation use for the AMPEL360 aircraft H₂ fuel containers.

---

## 2. Aviation Adaptations

### 2.1 Structural Requirements
- Pressure rating: 2.8-3.2 bar (cryogenic liquid H₂)
- Temperature: -253°C operational
- Combined loads: Flight loads + pressure + thermal
- Fatigue life: Aviation cycle requirements

### 2.2 Safety Requirements
- Double-wall vacuum-insulated construction
- Pressure relief system
- Tank integrity monitoring
- Emergency venting capability

### 2.3 Testing Requirements
- Proof pressure test: 5 bar for 30 seconds
- Burst pressure test
- Cycle testing (aviation profile)
- Environmental testing (altitude, temperature)

---

## 3. Compliance Status

**Status:** ISO 19881 baseline with aviation-specific enhancements
**Evidence:** Tank design analysis and test program (2026-Q3)
