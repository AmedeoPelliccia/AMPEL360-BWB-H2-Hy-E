## Hydrogen Fuel Quality for Fuel Cell Vehicles (Aviation Application)

**Document Control:**
- Version: 1.0
- Status: Active
- Last Updated: 2025-11-05

---

## 1. Standard Overview

**SAE J2719** - Hydrogen Fuel Quality for Fuel Cell Vehicles

This standard defines H₂ fuel quality requirements adopted for aviation use.

---

## 2. Fuel Quality Specifications

### 2.1 Purity Requirements
- H₂ purity: ≥99.97%
- Total non-H₂ gases: ≤300 ppm
- Water content: ≤5 ppm
- Total hydrocarbons: ≤2 ppm
- Oxygen: ≤5 ppm
- Nitrogen: ≤100 ppm
- Carbon dioxide: ≤2 ppm
- Carbon monoxide: ≤0.2 ppm
- Total sulfur compounds: ≤0.004 ppm

### 2.2 Particulates
- Particulate size: ≤10 μm
- Particulate concentration: ≤1 mg/kg

---

## 3. Quality Control

### 3.1 Refueling
- Fuel quality verification before each refueling
- Contamination monitoring
- Quality certification documentation

### 3.2 Monitoring
- Continuous fuel quality monitoring
- Fuel cell performance tracking
- Degradation detection

**Status:** SAE J2719 adopted for aviation operations
