## Scenario Set Overview
Comprehensive normal operations scenarios for AMPEL360 BWB H2 Hy-E training.

## Scenario 1.1: Standard Flight - Short Range

**Flight Profile:**
- Route: LFPG (Paris CDG) to EHAM (Amsterdam)
- Distance: 220 NM
- Duration: 45 minutes
- Weather: VMC, light winds

**Training Objectives:**
- Complete normal procedures
- CAOS interaction in routine operations
- BWB handling characteristics
- Standard communications

**Initial Conditions:**
- Aircraft at gate, cold and dark
- Full passenger load (220 pax)
- H₂ fuel: 1,200 kg
- Weight: 98,500 kg
- CG: 44.5

**Key Events:**
- Normal startup and taxi
- Standard departure
- Cruise at FL280
- CAOS fuel optimization advisory
- Normal approach and landing

**Success Criteria:**
- All procedures completed correctly
- CAOS advisories acknowledged
- Within flight tolerances
- Smooth BWB handling demonstrated
