# ICD-02-00-71-002: Thermal Management Interface
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Status:** Active

## Interface Description
Fuel cell thermal management system coordination with operations for optimal performance.

## Thermal Management
- **Cooling System**: Liquid cooling (60/40 glycol/water)
- **Operating Range**: 60-80°C optimal
- **Heat Rejection**: 5 MW total at full power
- **Startup Heating**: Pre-heat to 60°C before full power

## Temperature Monitoring
- Stack temperatures per cell (10 Hz)
- Coolant inlet/outlet temperatures
- Heat exchanger performance
- Thermal efficiency tracking

## Thermal Modes
- **Cold Start**: Pre-heat mode (<0°C ambient)
- **Normal**: Active cooling (60-80°C)
- **Hot Weather**: Enhanced cooling (>35°C ambient)
- **Emergency**: Maximum cooling capacity

**RQ-ICD-02-71-010:** Temperature control ±5°C  
**RQ-ICD-02-71-011:** Overheat protection <85°C  
**RQ-ICD-02-71-012:** Cold start capability -40°C
