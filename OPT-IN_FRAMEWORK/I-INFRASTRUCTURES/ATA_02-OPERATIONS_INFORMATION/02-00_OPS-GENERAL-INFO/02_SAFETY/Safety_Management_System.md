## 1. INTRODUCTION

This document describes the Safety Management System (SMS) for AMPEL360 operations in accordance with ICAO Annex 19 and EASA Part-ORO.

### 2.1 Four Pillars of SMS

**Pillar 3: Safety Assurance**

**Pillar 4: Safety Promotion**

## 3. SAFETY POLICY

### 3.1 Safety Policy Statement

*"Safety is our first priority. We are committed to achieving the highest standards of aviation safety through:*
- *Compliance with all applicable regulations*
- *Proactive identification and management of safety risks*
- *Continuous improvement of our safety performance*
- *Provision of adequate resources for safety management*
- *A just culture that encourages open safety reporting*
- *Effective communication of safety information"*
