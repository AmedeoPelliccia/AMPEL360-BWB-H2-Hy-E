## 2. CREW RESOURCE MANAGEMENT (CRM)

### 2.1 CRM Principles

**Communication:**
- Clear, concise, standardized
- Closed-loop communication
- Assertiveness when needed
- Active listening

**Teamwork:**
- Shared mental models
- Mutual support
- Task delegation
- Workload management

**Decision-Making:**
- Structured decision-making process
- Risk assessment
- Consider all inputs
- Timely decisions

**Situational Awareness:**
- Monitor aircraft state
- Anticipate future states
- Detect deviations early
- Maintain big picture

### 2.2 CAOS-Enhanced CRM

**Human-AI Teaming:**
- CAOS as team member
- Appropriate trust calibration
- Clear role delineation
- Mutual monitoring

## 3. WORKLOAD MANAGEMENT

### 3.1 Workload Assessment

**High Workload Phases:**
- Taxi out and takeoff
- Approach and landing
- Abnormal/emergency situations
- Weather avoidance
- Complex airspace

**Workload Reduction Strategies:**
- Task prioritization (aviate, navigate, communicate)
- Task delegation
- Use of automation (CAOS)
- Deferred non-critical tasks
- ATC assistance

### 3.2 Workload Distribution

**Two-Pilot Operations:**
- Pilot Flying (PF) / Pilot Monitoring (PM)
- Clear task allocation
- Dynamic reallocation as needed
- Mutual support

---

## 4. FATIGUE MANAGEMENT

### 4.1 Fatigue Risk Factors

**Operational Factors:**
- Duty time and rest
- Circadian disruption
- Workload intensity
- Sleep quality

**Mitigation:**
- Flight and duty time limitations
- Adequate rest periods
- Fatigue reporting system
- Scheduling optimization
- Crew alertness monitoring

### 4.2 Fatigue Countermeasures

**In-Flight:**
- Controlled rest (if approved)
- Cockpit environment management
- Workload management
- Engagement strategies

**Between Flights:**
- Quality rest facilities
- Sleep hygiene education
- Rest optimization strategies

---

## 5. DECISION-MAKING UNDER PRESSURE

### 5.1 Decision-Making Models

**FOR-DEC Model:**
- **F**acts: What do we know?
- **O**ptions: What can we do?
- **R**isks & Benefits: What are consequences?
- **D**ecide: Choose best option
- **E**xecute: Implement decision
- **C**heck: Monitor outcome

**CAOS Support:**
- Provides data (Facts)
- Suggests options
- Assesses risks/benefits
- Supports decision execution monitoring

### 5.2 Stress Management

**Stress Recognition:**
- Physiological signs
- Cognitive effects
- Emotional indicators
- Performance degradation

**Stress Management:**
- Breathing techniques
- Task prioritization
- Team support
- Training and preparation
