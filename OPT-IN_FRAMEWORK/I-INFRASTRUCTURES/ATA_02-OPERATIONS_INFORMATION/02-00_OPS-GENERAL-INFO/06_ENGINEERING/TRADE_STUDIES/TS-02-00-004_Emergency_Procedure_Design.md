## Executive Summary

Trade study of emergency procedure design approaches. Evaluated traditional detailed checklists, simplified condition-based checklists, and fully electronic procedures. Selected simplified checklists with CAOS decision support.

---

## Trade Study Objectives

Define and evaluate alternatives for key operational decisions to support optimal design and operational choices.

---

## Options Evaluated

Multiple options evaluated against criteria including:
- Safety
- Cost
- Operational efficiency
- Regulatory compliance
- Technical feasibility
- Schedule impact

---

## Analysis Method

- Define evaluation criteria with weighting
- Score each option against criteria
- Perform sensitivity analysis
- Document rationale for selection
- Obtain stakeholder review

---

## Selected Option

Detailed in Trade_Studies_Documentation.md overview document.

---

## Rationale

Selection based on comprehensive multi-criteria analysis balancing safety, efficiency, cost, and regulatory requirements.

## Implementation

Selected option to be implemented per engineering plans and operational procedures.
