# RQ-SAFETY
## Safety Requirements

**Category ID:** 02  
**Requirement Range:** RQ-02-00-02-001 to RQ-02-00-02-020  
**Total Requirements:** 20

## Overview

Safety requirements define targets, systems, and processes to ensure aircraft safety throughout its operational life. This includes general aviation safety, hydrogen-specific safety measures, and CAOS AI safety oversight.

## Requirement Subcategories

### Safety Targets (001-002)
- Zero Accident Target
- Incident Rate Target Below 1 per Million FH

### H₂ Safety Systems (003-010)
- H₂ Leak Rate Target
- Fuel Cell Reliability
- Emergency Evacuation (90 sec)
- Fire Detection and Suppression
- Leak Detection (8 Sensors/Zone)
- Emergency Ventilation (50 ACH)

### Safety Programs (011-015)
- Safety Reporting System
- Just Culture Policy
- Flight Data Monitoring
- LOSA Program
- Safety Risk Assessment

### H₂ Safety Design (016)
- Defense in Depth H₂ Safety

### CAOS Safety (017-020)
- Independent Safety Monitor
- CAOS Override Capability
- Crew Final Authority
- Safety Audit Program

## Critical Requirements

All 20 requirements in this category are **Critical** priority as they directly impact safety of flight.
