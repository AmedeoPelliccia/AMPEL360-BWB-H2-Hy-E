# RQ-BWB-OPERATIONS
## Blended Wing Body Operations Requirements

**Category ID:** 06  
**Requirement Range:** RQ-02-00-06-001 to RQ-02-00-06-015  
**Total Requirements:** 15

## Overview

BWB-specific operational requirements addressing the unique characteristics of the Blended Wing Body configuration.

## Key Requirements

- **CG Range:** 15-42% MAC
- **Wingspan:** 80m (Code F)
- **Rotation Rate:** 5 deg/sec maximum
- **Lateral Imbalance:** 500 kg limit
- **Evacuation Time:** 90 seconds
- BWB-specific handling and ground operations procedures
