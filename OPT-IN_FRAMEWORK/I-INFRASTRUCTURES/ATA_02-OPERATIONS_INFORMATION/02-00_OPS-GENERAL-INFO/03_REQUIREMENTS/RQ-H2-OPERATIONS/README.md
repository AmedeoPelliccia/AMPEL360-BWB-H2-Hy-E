# RQ-H2-OPERATIONS
## Hydrogen Operations Requirements

**Category ID:** 05  
**Requirement Range:** RQ-02-00-05-001 to RQ-02-00-05-020  
**Total Requirements:** 20

## Overview

Hydrogen operations requirements cover the safe and efficient handling, storage, and management of liquid hydrogen fuel throughout ground operations and flight.

## Key Requirements

### Capacity & Storage (001-005)
- H₂ Total Capacity: 8000 kg
- Usable Fuel: 7850 kg minimum
- Storage Temperature: -253°C
- Storage Pressure: 2.8-3.2 bar
- Boil-off Rate: 0.3%/day maximum

### Refueling Operations (006-007)
- Refueling Time: 45 minutes maximum
- Flow Rate: 120 kg/min

### Safety Procedures (008-012)
- Safety Zone: 50m radius
- Grounding Resistance: 3 milliohm max
- Fire Equipment on Standby
- Personnel Certification: 8 hours minimum
- Leak Detection Operational

### Ventilation & Monitoring (013-015)
- Normal Ventilation: 10 ACH
- Emergency Ventilation: 50 ACH
- LEL Monitoring Continuous

### Testing & Maintenance (016-020)
- Pressure Test: 5 bar for 30 seconds
- Cooldown Period: 10 minutes
- Emergency Defueling: 60 minutes max
- H₂ Quality per SAE J2719
- Tank Integrity Continuous Monitoring
