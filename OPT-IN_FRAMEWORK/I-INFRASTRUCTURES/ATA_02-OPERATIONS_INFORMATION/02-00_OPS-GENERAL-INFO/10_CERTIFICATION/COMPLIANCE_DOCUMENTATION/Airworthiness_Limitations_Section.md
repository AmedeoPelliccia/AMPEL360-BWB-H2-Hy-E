## 1. Purpose

The Airworthiness Limitations Section (ALS) defines mandatory inspection and replacement requirements for continued airworthiness.

---

## 2. H₂ System Limitations

### 2.1 Cryogenic Fuel Tanks
- **Item:** H₂ Tank Pressure Test
- **Interval:** 2000 flight hours or 5 years (whichever first)
- **Procedure:** Pressure test 5 bar for 30 seconds
- **Reason:** Tank integrity critical for H₂ safety

- **Item:** Vacuum Insulation Integrity
- **Interval:** 1000 flight hours
- **Procedure:** Boil-off rate measurement
- **Reason:** Thermal performance degradation monitoring

### 2.2 Fuel Cell Stacks
- **Item:** Fuel Cell Performance Test
- **Interval:** 500 flight hours
- **Procedure:** Power output and efficiency verification
- **Reason:** Degradation monitoring, predictive maintenance

- **Item:** Fuel Cell Stack Replacement
- **Interval:** 10,000 flight hours or performance degradation
- **Procedure:** Complete stack replacement
- **Reason:** Expected stack life limit

---

## 3. BWB Structure Limitations

### 3.1 Wing-Body Structure
- **Item:** Pressurization Fatigue Inspection
- **Interval:** Per structural analysis (fatigue life)
- **Procedure:** NDT inspection of critical areas
- **Reason:** Combined pressure and flight loads

- **Item:** Wing-Body Joint Inspection
- **Interval:** 5000 flight hours
- **Procedure:** Visual and NDT inspection
- **Reason:** Critical structural interface

---

## 4. CAOS System Limitations

### 4.1 AI Model Updates
- **Item:** CAOS Model Validation
- **Interval:** After each model update (monthly maximum)
- **Procedure:** Safety validation testing
- **Reason:** AI model change control
