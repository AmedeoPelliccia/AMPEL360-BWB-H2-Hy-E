## 1. Purpose

Operations Specifications define special operational requirements for the AMPEL360 aircraft.

---

## 2. Operator Requirements

### 2.1 Training Requirements
- Type rating: 40 hours ground school + 16 hours simulator
- BWB handling: 5 hours specific training
- H₂ systems: 3 hours training
- CAOS operations: 3 hours training
- H₂ safety: Mandatory certification
- Recurrent training: Annual

### 2.2 Operational Procedures
- Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) BWB-specific
- H₂ refueling procedures
- CAOS engagement protocols
- Emergency procedures (H₂-specific)

### 2.3 Maintenance Requirements
- H₂ system maintenance personnel certification
- Special inspection requirements
- Predictive maintenance via CAOS

---

## 3. Airport Requirements

### 3.1 Infrastructure
- H₂ refueling capability (EN 17127)
- Code F gate compatibility (80m wingspan)
- Fire equipment (H₂-capable)
- Safety zones (50m radius during refueling)

### 3.2 Personnel
- H₂-certified ground crew (8 hours minimum)
- Fire/rescue H₂ training

---

## 4. Operational Limitations

### 4.1 Weather Minimums
- Standard Category I/II/III (per approval)
- Special considerations: Ground clearance in crosswind

### 4.2 Performance
- Runway length requirements (MTOW dependent)
- Fuel planning (H₂-specific procedures)
- Altitude and range limitations
