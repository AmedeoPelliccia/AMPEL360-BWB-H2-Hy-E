## 1. Cryogenic System Overview

### 1.1 H₂ Storage Conditions
- Temperature: -253°C (20 K)
- Pressure: 2.8-3.2 bar
- Storage type: Liquid hydrogen (LH₂)
- Tank configuration: Double-wall vacuum-insulated

### 1.2 System Components
- Cryogenic fuel tanks (8000 kg capacity)
- Vacuum insulation system
- Boil-off management system
- Cryogenic transfer lines
- Thermal management system

## 2. Certification Challenges

### 2.1 Material Considerations
- Cryogenic temperature material compatibility
- Thermal expansion/contraction
- Material embrittlement prevention
- Thermal stress analysis

### 2.2 Insulation System
- Vacuum maintenance
- Boil-off rate: ≤0.3% per day
- Thermal performance validation
- Long-term degradation resistance

### 2.3 Safety Systems
- Pressure relief system
- Vacuum monitoring
- Temperature monitoring
- Boil-off gas management
