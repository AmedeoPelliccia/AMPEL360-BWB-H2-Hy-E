# CS-25.1585 Operating Procedures Compliance
## AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA

**Document Control:**
- Version: 1.0
- Status: Active
- Last Updated: 2025-11-05

---

## 1. Regulatory Requirement

**CS-25.1585 Operating Procedures**

Operating procedures must be established for:
(a) Normal procedures
(b) Emergency procedures
(c) Operating limitations and information

---

## 2. Compliance Approach

### 2.1 Normal Procedures
- Pre-flight inspection (BWB-specific, H₂ system checks)
- Starting and ground operations (H₂ fuel cell startup)
- Taxi, takeoff, and landing (BWB handling characteristics)
- H₂ refueling procedures
- CAOS system engagement and monitoring

### 2.2 Emergency Procedures
- H₂ leak detection and response
- Fuel cell malfunction procedures
- Emergency evacuation (BWB wide-body cabin)
- CAOS override and manual control reversion
- Emergency defueling

### 2.3 Compliance Method
**Demonstration:** Operational validation with crew procedures testing

---
