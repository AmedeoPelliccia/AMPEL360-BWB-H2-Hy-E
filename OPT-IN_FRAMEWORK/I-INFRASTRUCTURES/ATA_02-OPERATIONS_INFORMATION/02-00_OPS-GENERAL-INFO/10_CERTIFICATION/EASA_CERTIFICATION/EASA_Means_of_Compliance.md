## 3. Novel Technology Compliance

### 3.1 BWB Configuration
**Special Conditions Required:**
- Wing-body structural design
- Pressurized cabin for BWB geometry
- Emergency evacuation wide cabin
- Ground handling characteristics

**Means of Compliance:**
- Extensive FEA and structural analysis
- Full-scale pressure testing
- Evacuation demonstration with mockup
- Ground handling testing

### 3.2 H₂ Propulsion System
**Special Conditions Required:**
- H₂ fuel system installation
- Cryogenic tank requirements
- Fuel cell powerplant certification
- Safety systems and procedures

**Means of Compliance:**
- Standards adaptation (ISO 19881, SAE J2719, NFPA 2)
- H₂ safety case development
- Ground and flight testing
- Operational demonstrations

### 3.3 CAOS AI System
**Special Conditions Required:**
- AI system architecture and oversight
- ML model validation
- Human authority maintenance
- AI transparency

**Means of Compliance:**
- EU AI Act compliance framework
- EASA AI Roadmap alignment
- DO-178C adapted for ML
- Human factors validation
