# Section 2 - Normal Procedures

## 2.1 Pre-Flight
- Check H₂ fuel quantity and pressure
- Verify CAOS system status
- Review weather and NOTAM
- Confirm weight and balance

## 2.2 Engine Start (Fuel Cell Activation)
1. H₂ System Master - ON
2. Fuel Cell Switches - ON (sequential)
3. Monitor fuel cell temperatures
4. Verify power generation

## 2.3 Taxi
- Normal taxi procedures
- Monitor H₂ system status on ECAM
- CAOS taxi guidance available

## 2.4 Takeoff
- Standard takeoff procedures
- Monitor fuel cell performance
- CAOS advisory for optimal power settings

## 2.5 Cruise
- CAOS provides optimized cruise recommendations
- Monitor H₂ consumption
- Adjust altitude/speed per CAOS advisories

## 2.6 Approach and Landing
- Standard approach procedures
- Monitor H₂ reserves
- CAOS landing performance calculations
