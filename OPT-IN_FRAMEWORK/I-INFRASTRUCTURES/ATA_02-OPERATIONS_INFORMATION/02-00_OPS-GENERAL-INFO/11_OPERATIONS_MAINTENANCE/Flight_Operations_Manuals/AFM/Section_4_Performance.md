# Section 4 - Performance

## 4.1 Takeoff Performance
- Runway requirements calculated for:
  - Weight
  - Temperature
  - Pressure altitude
  - Wind
  - Runway condition

**Typical Values (ISA, Sea Level, Max Weight):**
- Takeoff Distance: 2,400 m
- Accelerate-Stop Distance: 2,800 m

## 4.2 Climb Performance
- Typical Climb Rate: 2,500 fpm (low altitude)
- Time to FL350: 28 minutes
- Fuel to FL350: 180 kg H₂

## 4.3 Cruise Performance
- Optimum Altitude: FL370-FL410
- Cruise Speed: M0.78-0.82
- Fuel Consumption: 0.8 kg H₂ per km

## 4.4 Range
- Maximum Range: 4,000 km (2,160 NM)
- With Reserves: 3,600 km (1,944 NM)

## 4.5 Landing Performance
- Typical Landing Distance: 1,800 m
- Approach Speed: 140-160 KIAS (weight dependent)
