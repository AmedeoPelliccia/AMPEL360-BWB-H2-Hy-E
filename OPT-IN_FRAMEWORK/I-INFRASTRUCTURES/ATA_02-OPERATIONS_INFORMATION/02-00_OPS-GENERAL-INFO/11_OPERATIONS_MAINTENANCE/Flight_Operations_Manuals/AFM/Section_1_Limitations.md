# Section 1 - Limitations

## 1.1 Operating Limitations
- Maximum Operating Altitude: 41,000 ft
- Maximum Operating Speed: Mach 0.82 / 340 KIAS
- Minimum Flight Crew: 2 (Captain, First Officer)

## 1.2 H₂ Fuel System Limitations
- Maximum H₂ Tank Pressure: 700 bar
- Minimum H₂ Tank Pressure for flight: 50 bar
- Maximum H₂ Temperature: -240°C
- Minimum fuel for takeoff: 200 kg

## 1.3 Weight Limitations
- Maximum Takeoff Weight: 95,000 kg
- Maximum Landing Weight: 80,000 kg
- Maximum Zero Fuel Weight: 72,000 kg

## 1.4 Environmental Limitations
- Maximum Crosswind: 35 kts
- Maximum Tailwind for Takeoff: 10 kts
- Maximum Tailwind for Landing: 15 kts
- Maximum Operating Temperature: ISA +35°C
- Minimum Operating Temperature: ISA -55°C
