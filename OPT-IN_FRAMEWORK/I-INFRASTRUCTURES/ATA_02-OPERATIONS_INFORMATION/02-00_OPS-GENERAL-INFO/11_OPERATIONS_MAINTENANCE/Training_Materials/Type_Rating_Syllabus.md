# Type Rating Syllabus
## AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100

**Course Code:** TR-AMP-Q100  
**Duration:** 6 weeks (Ground + Simulator + Aircraft)  
**Prerequisites:** ATPL, Multi-engine rating

---

## Week 1-2: Ground School (80 hours)

### Module 1: Aircraft Systems (32 hours)
- BWB aerodynamics and characteristics
- H₂ fuel system
- Fuel cell propulsion
- Electrical power systems
- Flight controls
- Environmental systems

### Module 2: CAOS Operations (16 hours)
- CAOS system architecture
- Flight planning with CAOS
- Energy management
- Predictive maintenance system
- Human-AI collaboration

### Module 3: Normal Procedures (16 hours)
- Pre-flight through shutdown
- Standard operating procedures
- CAOS integration procedures
- H₂ fuel management

### Module 4: Abnormal and Emergency Procedures (16 hours)
- H₂ emergencies
- Fuel cell failures
- Electrical emergencies
- CAOS malfunction procedures

## Week 3-4: Full Flight Simulator (40 hours)

### Normal Operations (20 hours)
- Standard procedures practice
- CAOS operations
- Normal flight profiles
- Landings (various configurations)

### Abnormal/Emergency (20 hours)
- H₂ leak procedures
- Fuel cell failures
- Electrical emergencies
- Engine-out operations
- Emergency descents

## Week 5: Aircraft Training (20 hours)

### Ground Training (8 hours)
- Cockpit familiarization
- External walk-around
- H₂ system inspection
- CAOS system operation

### Flight Training (12 hours, 6 flights)
- Normal takeoffs and landings
- CAOS-assisted operations
- Abnormal procedures (simulated)
- Emergency procedures (simulated)

## Week 6: Proficiency Check

### Ground Exam (4 hours)
- Systems knowledge
- Procedures
- Regulations

### Simulator Check (4 hours)
- Normal procedures
- Abnormal/emergency procedures

### Aircraft Check (2 hours)
- Line-oriented flight training (LOFT)
- Skill demonstration

## Recurrent Training (Annual, 3 days)

### Ground Refresher (8 hours)
- Systems review
- Procedure updates
- CAOS updates

### Simulator (4 hours)
- Procedures review
- Emergency scenarios

### Proficiency Check (4 hours)
- Simulator proficiency check
