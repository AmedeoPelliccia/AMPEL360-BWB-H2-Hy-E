# Maintenance Training Syllabus
## AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 Aircraft

**Course Code:** MX-TRAIN-AMP-001  
**Duration:** 4 weeks  
**Target:** Maintenance technicians

---

## Week 1: Aircraft Overview and Safety

### Aircraft Systems Overview (20 hours)
- BWB design principles
- Major systems introduction
- Documentation structure (S1000D)
- CAOS maintenance integration

### H₂ Safety Training (20 hours)
- See H2_Systems_Training.md
- Additional hands-on safety practice
- Emergency response drills

## Week 2: Powerplant and Fuel Systems

### Fuel Cell Systems (20 hours)
- PEM fuel cell theory
- Maintenance procedures
- Troubleshooting
- Overhaul procedures

### H₂ Fuel System (20 hours)
- Tank maintenance
- Pressure system servicing
- Leak detection and repair
- Valve maintenance

## Week 3: Airframe and Systems

### BWB Airframe (20 hours)
- Structural inspections
- Composite repair techniques
- Access and panels
- NDT methods

### Aircraft Systems (20 hours)
- Electrical systems
- Flight controls
- Environmental systems
- Avionics systems

## Week 4: CAOS and Practical Training

### CAOS Maintenance (10 hours)
- Digital twin operation
- Predictive maintenance system
- Software updates
- Troubleshooting CAOS issues

### Practical Training (30 hours)
- Hands-on task card execution
- Tool and equipment operation
- Documentation completion
- Quality inspection

## Assessment
- Written exams (each module)
- Practical assessments
- Task card demonstration
- Safety procedure verification

## Specialized Ratings

### H₂ System Technician
- Additional 40 hours H₂ training
- Certification exam
- Practical demonstration

### Fuel Cell Technician
- Additional 40 hours fuel cell training
- Manufacturer certification
- Overhaul qualification
