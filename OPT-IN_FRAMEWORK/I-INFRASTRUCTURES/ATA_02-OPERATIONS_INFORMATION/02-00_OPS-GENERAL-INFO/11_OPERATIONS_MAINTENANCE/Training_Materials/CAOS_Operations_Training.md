# CAOS Operations Training
## AI-Assisted Flight Operations

**Course Code:** CAOS-OPS-001  
**Duration:** 2 days  
**Target:** Flight crew

---

## Day 1: CAOS Fundamentals

### Morning: System Overview (4 hours)
- CAOS architecture
- AI and machine learning basics
- Digital twin concept
- Fleet learning
- Human-AI collaboration philosophy

### Afternoon: CAOS Interface (4 hours)
- Cockpit integration
- Display symbology
- Advisory types and colors
- Accepting/rejecting advisories
- Override procedures

## Day 2: CAOS Operations

### Morning: Normal Operations (4 hours)
- Flight planning with CAOS
- Energy management
- Route optimization
- Performance monitoring
- Post-flight analysis

### Afternoon: Abnormal Operations (4 hours)
- CAOS degraded mode
- CAOS system failures
- Emergency operations with CAOS
- Manual reversion procedures
- Scenario-based training

## Simulator Practice (Optional, +4 hours)
- CAOS-assisted flight
- Various CAOS failure modes
- Integration with standard procedures

## Assessment
- Computer-based test
- Scenario responses
- CAOS operation demonstration
