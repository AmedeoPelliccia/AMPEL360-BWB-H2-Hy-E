## Change Description

### Current State
Current H₂ refueling procedures in 02-32-00 based on preliminary safety analysis. Recent detailed FHA identified additional safety controls needed.

### Proposed Change
Enhance H₂ refueling procedures with:
- Additional pre-refueling safety checks
- Enhanced leak detection monitoring
- Updated emergency shutdown procedures
- New atmospheric monitoring requirements
- Enhanced PPE requirements for ground crew

### Justification
Safety analysis (FHA-28-001) identified gaps in current procedures. Changes required to meet EASA CS-25 and ISO 19881 hydrogen safety standards.
