# ATA 116 - Flight Simulator Cuing System

## Overview
This chapter contains specifications for the visual, motion, and aural systems of the Full Flight Simulator (FFS). These systems provide sensory cues to pilots during training.

## Structure

### 01-SYSTEM_ARCHITECTURE_AND_SPECS
Technical architecture and specifications of cuing systems

### 02-QUALIFICATION_AND_COMPLIANCE
Qualification standards and regulatory compliance

### 03-CALIBRATION_AND_ALIGNMENT
Calibration procedures and alignment requirements

### 04-SOFTWARE_AND_MODELS
Software and mathematical models for cuing systems

### 05-OPERATIONS_AND_MAINTENANCE
Operating procedures and maintenance requirements

### 06-REGISTRY_AND_LOGS
Registry of system configurations and operational logs

## Governance
The cuing systems must provide high-fidelity sensory feedback to ensure effective training and meet regulatory qualification requirements for Level D simulators.
