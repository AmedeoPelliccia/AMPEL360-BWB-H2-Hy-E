# ATA 10 - Parking, Mooring, Storage, and Return to Service

## Overview
This chapter contains procedures for non-operational aircraft handling, including parking, mooring, long-term storage, and return to service procedures.

## Structure

### 01-PARKING_AND_MANEUVERING
Procedures for parking the aircraft and ground maneuvering

### 02-MOORING
Mooring procedures and equipment specifications for securing aircraft

### 03-STORAGE_PROCEDURES
Long-term and short-term storage procedures and preservation

### 04-RETURN_TO_SERVICE
Procedures for returning aircraft to service after storage

### 05-DIAGRAMS_AND_DATA
Diagrams, data sheets, and reference information

## Governance
All procedures must account for the AMPEL360's unique BWB configuration and hydrogen fuel system requirements during extended ground operations.
