# Propulsion Practices Checklist

## Installation Checklist
- [ ] Pylon-to-wing attachment torque verified (±5%)
- [ ] Engine mount alignment checked (±0.5mm)
- [ ] H₂ fuel lines leak-tested (1e-6 mbar·L/s)
- [ ] Electrical connections torqued and inspected
- [ ] FADEC power supply redundancy verified
- [ ] Vibration isolation mounts installed correctly

## Pre-Flight Checklist
- [ ] Oil level within normal range
- [ ] H₂ fuel pressure nominal
- [ ] FADEC BIT passed
- [ ] No active faults or warnings
- [ ] Vibration sensors functional

## Post-Flight Checklist
- [ ] Review health monitoring data
- [ ] Check for anomalies or advisories
- [ ] Log flight hours and cycles
- [ ] Inspect for visible damage or leaks
