# 95-70-70-002: Standard Practices for Propulsion Installations

## 1. Installation Standards

### 1.1 Mechanical Installation
- Pylon-to-wing attachment torque specifications
- Engine mount alignment tolerances: ±0.5 mm
- Vibration isolation requirements
- Thermal expansion provisions

### 1.2 Fluid Connections
- **H₂ Fuel Lines**: Vacuum-insulated, leak-tested to 1e-6 mbar·L/s
- **Oil Lines**: AN fittings, torqued per manufacturer specs
- **Bleed Air Ducts**: V-band clamps, temperature-rated to 500°C

### 1.3 Electrical Connections
- Shielded cables for FADEC control
- Redundant power supplies (28 VDC, dual channel)
- Grounding per DO-160 requirements

## 2. Quality Assurance

- First article inspection for all new installations
- Non-destructive testing (NDT) of critical attachments
- Documentation of torque values and inspector sign-off
