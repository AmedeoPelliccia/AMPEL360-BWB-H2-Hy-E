# 95-70-70-001: Propulsion Practices Overview

## 2. Scope

ATA 70 encompasses general practices applicable to all propulsion systems (ATA 71-79):
- Installation standards
- Safety margins and limits
- Configuration management
- Quality assurance procedures

## 3. Relationship to Other ATA Chapters

| ATA Chapter | Relationship |
|-------------|--------------|
| ATA 71-79 | Specific propulsion systems that implement these practices |
| ATA 24 | Electrical power generation interfaces |
| ATA 28 | Fuel system interfaces |
