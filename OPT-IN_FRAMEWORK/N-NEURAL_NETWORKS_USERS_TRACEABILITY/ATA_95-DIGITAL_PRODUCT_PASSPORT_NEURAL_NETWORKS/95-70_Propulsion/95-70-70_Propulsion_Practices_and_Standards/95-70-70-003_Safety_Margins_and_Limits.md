# 95-70-70-003: Safety Margins and Limits

## 1. Operational Limits

### 1.1 Thrust Limits
| Parameter | Normal | Maximum (5 min) | Emergency (30 sec) |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Thrust (%) | 100 | 110 | 120 |
| N1 Speed (RPM) | 1200 | 1320 | 1440 |

### 1.2 Temperature Limits
| Parameter | Normal | Caution | Maximum |
|-----------|--------|---------|---------|
| EGT (°C) | 650 | 750 | 850 |
| Oil Temp (°C) | 90 | 110 | 125 |
| H₂ Fuel (°C) | -253 | -245 | -240 |

### 1.3 Pressure Limits
| Parameter | Minimum | Normal | Maximum |
|-----------|---------|--------|---------|
| Oil Pressure (psi) | 30 | 60 | 90 |
| Fuel Pressure (bar) | 5 | 10 | 15 |

## 2. Safety Margins

- **Thrust Derating**: 10% margin for continuous operation
- **Temperature Margin**: 50°C below material limits
- **Structural Margin**: 1.5× ultimate load (2.25× limit load per CS-25)
