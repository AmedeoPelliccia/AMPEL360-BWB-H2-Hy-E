## Purpose

This index provides a comprehensive reference to all regulatory frameworks, standards, and guidance materials applicable to the certification of the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 aircraft, with particular emphasis on AI/ML systems, hydrogen propulsion, and Digital Product Passport integration.

### Anticipated Special Conditions

2. **SC-H2-001**: Hydrogen fuel system installation and safety
3. **SC-H2-002**: Cryogenic hydrogen storage and handling
4. **SC-AI-001**: Neural network-based flight control augmentation
5. **SC-AI-002**: AI-based predictive maintenance system
6. **SC-DPP-001**: Digital Product Passport integration and data integrity
