## Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive overview of hardware components used in the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E aircraft system.

## Hardware Component Categories

### 1. Compute Boards and Modules
- Flight control computers
- Integrated modular avionics (IMA)
- Graphics processing units
- AI accelerators
- Single-board computers

### 2. Sensors
- Inertial measurement units (IMUs)
- GNSS receivers
- Air data sensors
- Temperature sensors
- Pressure sensors
- Optical cameras
- LiDAR systems

### 3. Actuators
- Electric servo motors
- Hydraulic actuators
- Pneumatic actuators
- Control surface actuators
- Valve actuators

### 4. Power Components
- H₂ fuel cells
- Battery systems
- Power distribution units
- DC/DC converters
- AC/DC converters
- Protection devices

### 5. Communication Hardware
- VHF radios
- Data link transceivers
- Satcom terminals
- Ethernet switches
- CAN bus controllers
- ARINC 429 interfaces

### 6. Interface Devices
- Displays (LCD, OLED)
- Control panels
- Keyboards
- Touchscreens
- Indicator lights
- Connectors and cables
