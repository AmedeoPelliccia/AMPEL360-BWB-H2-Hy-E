# 04_Regulatory_Compliance — Regulatory Compliance Requirements

## Purpose

This folder contains **regulatory compliance requirements** — mandatory requirements derived from laws, regulations, and certification standards.

## Scope

### ID Range: RQ-95-00-03-300 to RQ-95-00-03-399

Regulatory compliance requirements address:
- **EU AI Act**: High-risk AI documentation (Regulation EU 2024/1689)
- **EU DPP Regulation**: Digital product passport mandates (Regulation EU 2024/1781)
- **EASA regulations**: Aviation AI certification requirements
- **FAA regulations**: US aviation AI certification
- **Data protection**: GDPR, privacy regulations
- **Export control**: ITAR, EAR compliance

## What Belongs Here

**Regulatory compliance requirements** are mandated by external authorities. Examples:
- "The DPP SHALL include all EU AI Act Annex IV fields"
- "The DPP SHALL comply with EASA AI certification guidance"
- "The DPP SHALL support GDPR data subject rights"

## What Doesn't Belong Here

- **Voluntary functional capabilities** → 01_Functional
- **Internal safety processes** → 03_Safety_and_AAI
- **Performance targets** → 02_NonFunctional
