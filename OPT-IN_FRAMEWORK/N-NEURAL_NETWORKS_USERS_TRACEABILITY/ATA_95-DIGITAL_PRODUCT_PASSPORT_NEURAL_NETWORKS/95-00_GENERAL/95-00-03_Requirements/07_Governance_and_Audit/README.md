# 07_Governance_and_Audit — Governance and Audit Requirements

## Purpose

This folder contains **governance and audit requirements** — requirements for change control, audit trails, and configuration management.

## Scope

### ID Range: RQ-95-00-03-600 to RQ-95-00-03-699

Governance and audit requirements address:
- **Change control**: CCB approval processes
- **Audit trails**: Immutable logging of all operations
- **Access control**: Authentication and authorization
- **Configuration management**: Version control and baselines
- **Compliance monitoring**: Ongoing compliance verification
- **Incident management**: Response and investigation procedures

## What Belongs Here

**Governance and audit requirements** define control and oversight mechanisms. Examples:
- "DPP changes SHALL require CCB approval"
- "The DPP SHALL maintain an immutable audit trail"
- "Access to DPP SHALL be role-based and logged"

## What Doesn't Belong Here

- **Functional system operations** → 01_Functional
- **System performance** → 02_NonFunctional
- **Safety monitoring** → 03_Safety_and_AAI
