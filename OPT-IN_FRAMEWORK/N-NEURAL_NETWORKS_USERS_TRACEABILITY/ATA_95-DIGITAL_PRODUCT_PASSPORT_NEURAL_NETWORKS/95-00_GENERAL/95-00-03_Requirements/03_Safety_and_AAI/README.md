# 03_Safety_and_AAI — Safety and AI Assurance Requirements

## Purpose

This folder contains **safety and AI assurance** requirements — requirements ensuring AI systems operate safely within defined boundaries.

## Scope

### ID Range: RQ-95-00-03-200 to RQ-95-00-03-299

Safety and AAI requirements address:
- **Operational Design Domain (ODD)**: Safe operating boundaries
- **Runtime monitoring**: Performance tracking, anomaly detection
- **Safety assessments**: FHA, FMEA, FTA documentation
- **Failure modes**: Identification and mitigation
- **Risk management**: Safety risk controls

## What Belongs Here

**Safety and AI assurance requirements** define safety-critical aspects. Examples:
- "The DPP SHALL capture ODD for each model"
- "The DPP SHALL store runtime monitoring configuration"
- "The DPP SHALL link to safety assessment documents"

## What Doesn't Belong Here

- **General functional requirements** → 01_Functional
- **Regulatory compliance mandates** → 04_Regulatory_Compliance
- **Performance requirements** → 02_NonFunctional
