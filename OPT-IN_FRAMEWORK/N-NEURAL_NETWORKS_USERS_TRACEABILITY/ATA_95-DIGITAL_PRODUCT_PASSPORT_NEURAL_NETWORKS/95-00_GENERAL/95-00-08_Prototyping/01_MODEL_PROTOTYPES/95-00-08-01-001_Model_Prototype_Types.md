## 1. Purpose

This document defines the types of model prototypes used in the ATA 95 Digital Product Passport Neural Networks framework.

## 2. Prototype Types

### 2.1 Baseline Models

Simple models serving as performance benchmarks:
- Linear regression
- Decision trees
- Simple neural networks (MLPs)

### 2.2 Advanced Models

Complex architectures for production:
- Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
- Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs/LSTMs)
- Transformer-based models
- Ensemble models

### 2.3 Domain-Specific Models

Models tailored to specific aviation domains:
- **Flight Path Prediction**: Time-series forecasting
- **Anomaly Detection**: Autoencoder-based models
- **Predictive Maintenance**: Multi-task learning models
- **Fuel Optimization**: Reinforcement learning models

## 3. Selection Criteria

- **Complexity**: Match model complexity to problem complexity
- **Interpretability**: Prefer interpretable models for safety-critical applications
- **Performance**: Balance accuracy, speed, and resource usage
- **Maintainability**: Consider long-term maintenance costs
