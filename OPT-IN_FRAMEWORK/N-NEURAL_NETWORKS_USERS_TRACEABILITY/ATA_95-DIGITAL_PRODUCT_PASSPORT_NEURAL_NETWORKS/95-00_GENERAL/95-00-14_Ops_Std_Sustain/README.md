# 95-00-14_Ops_Std_Sustain

## Purpose

Comprehensive operational standards, governance frameworks, risk management, sustainability principles, and continuous improvement practices for Neural Network operations and Digital Product Passport lifecycle management within the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 aircraft program.

## Scope

This folder is part of the **95-00_GENERAL** layer (14 of 14 in the canonical lifecycle), providing operational excellence and sustainability governance for:

- Neural Network (NN) operational standards and procedures
- Digital Product Passport (DPP) operational management
- Hydrogen propulsion operational guidelines
- Sustainability and circularity operations
- Governance structures and decision-making processes
- Risk management and regulatory compliance
- Continuous improvement and learning systems
- Stakeholder communication and ESG reporting
