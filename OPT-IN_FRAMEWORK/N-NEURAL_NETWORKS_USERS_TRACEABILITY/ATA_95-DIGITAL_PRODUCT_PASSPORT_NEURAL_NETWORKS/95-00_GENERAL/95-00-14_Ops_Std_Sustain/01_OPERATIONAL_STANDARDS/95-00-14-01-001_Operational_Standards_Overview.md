## Purpose
Provides comprehensive overview of operational standards for Neural Network operations, Digital Product Passport management, and hydrogen propulsion systems within the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 aircraft.

## Scope
- Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for NN-enhanced operations
- Operational limits and envelopes for novel systems
- Interface specifications with traditional ATA operations
- Links to verification, validation, and certification processes

## Key Standards

### 1. NN Operations Standards
- AI decision-making protocols
- Human-AI interaction procedures  
- Explainability requirements
- Fail-safe and degradation modes

### 2. H₂ Propulsion Operations
- Cryogenic fuel handling procedures
- Refueling protocols and safety measures
- Fuel cell performance optimization
- Emergency procedures for H₂ systems

### 3. DPP Operational Standards
- Real-time data collection requirements
- Lifecycle event logging protocols
- Circularity tracking procedures
- Blockchain update mechanisms
