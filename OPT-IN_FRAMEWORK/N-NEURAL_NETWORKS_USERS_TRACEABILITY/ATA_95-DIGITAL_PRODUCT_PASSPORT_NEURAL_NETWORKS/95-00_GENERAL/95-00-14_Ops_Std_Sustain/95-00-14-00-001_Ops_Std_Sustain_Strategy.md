## Purpose

This document defines the overarching strategy for operational standards, governance, and sustainability within the AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 project, specifically for Neural Network (NN) operations and Digital Product Passport (DPP) systems.

## Scope

This strategy covers:
- Operational excellence and standardization
- Governance frameworks for NN operations
- Risk management and compliance
- Sustainability and circularity principles
- Continuous improvement practices
- Incident management protocols
- Training and competence management
- Knowledge management systems
- Vendor and partner governance
- Data privacy and ethical operations
- Operational analytics and KPIs
- Stakeholder communication
- Regulatory and ESG reporting
- Strategic roadmap and maturity evolution

## Strategic Objectives

### 1. Operational Excellence
- Establish robust Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for all NN-integrated operations
- Define clear operational limits and envelopes for hydrogen propulsion and AI systems
- Ensure seamless integration with traditional ATA operational chapters (02, 31, 45)

### 2. Governance & Accountability
- Implement transparent decision-making processes
- Define clear roles, responsibilities, and RACI matrices
- Establish governance bodies for oversight and strategic direction

### 3. Risk & Compliance
- Proactive risk identification and mitigation for NN operations
- Maintain compliance with aviation regulations (EASA CS-25, FAA regulations)
- Integrate AI assurance and safety case requirements
- Address EU AI Act and DPP Regulation compliance

### 4. Sustainability & Circularity
- Achieve carbon-negative operations through H₂ propulsion and CO₂ capture
- Implement circular economy principles in the DPP lifecycle
- Track and optimize energy, emissions, and waste metrics
- Support EU Green Deal and Circular Economy Action Plan objectives

### 5. Continuous Improvement
- Foster a culture of learning from incidents and operational events
- Implement Kaizen and retrospective practices
- Maintain improvement backlogs linked to DPP version control

### 6. Human-Centric Operations
- Prioritize human factors in NN operational standards
- Ensure adequate training for pilots, maintainers, and operations staff
- Balance AI assistance with human oversight and authority

## Strategic Pillars

### Pillar 1: Standards-Based Operations
**Goal**: Every operational activity follows documented, validated standards

**Key Components**:
- Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
- Operational limits and envelopes
- Interface specifications with ATA chapters
- Links to V&V and certification processes

### Pillar 2: Transparent Governance
**Goal**: Clear accountability and decision-making at all levels

**Key Components**:
- Governance organizational structure
- Roles and responsibilities (RACI)
- Governance bodies and committees
- Decision-making processes
- Links to risk and compliance frameworks

### Pillar 3: Proactive Risk Management
**Goal**: Identify, assess, and mitigate risks before they impact operations

**Key Components**:
- Operational risk register
- Compliance controls and audits
- Integration with safety case
- Risk review and audit processes

### Pillar 4: Sustainable & Circular Operations
**Goal**: Net carbon-negative operations with circular lifecycle management

**Key Components**:
- Sustainability KPIs (carbon, energy, waste)
- Circularity principles in DPP
- End-to-end lifecycle impact assessment
- Integration with production and circularity systems

### Pillar 5: Learning Organization
**Goal**: Continuously improve through systematic learning

**Key Components**:
- Incident and anomaly management
- Root cause analysis practices
- Continuous improvement framework
- Knowledge management systems

## Alignment with AMPEL360 Vision

This operational standards and sustainability strategy directly supports the AMPEL360 mission to create the world's first carbon-negative commercial aircraft through:

1. **Integration with CAOS**: Computer Aided Operations & Services as the fourth pillar of digital engineering

3. **Neural Network Operations**: Safe, explainable, and auditable AI systems
4. **Hydrogen Operations**: Specialized procedures for cryogenic fuel handling
5. **Blended Wing Body**: Novel operational considerations for BWB configuration

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Operational Excellence KPIs
- SOP adherence rate: >98%
- Operational incidents per 1000 flight hours: <0.5
- Procedure revision cycle time: <30 days

### Sustainability KPIs
- Net CO₂ per flight: <-5 kg (carbon negative)
- H₂ fuel efficiency: <0.8 kg/ASK
- Circularity rate: >85% components recyclable/reusable

### Governance KPIs
- Governance meeting attendance: >90%
- Decision turnaround time: <14 days
- Audit findings closure rate: 100% within 60 days

### Risk & Compliance KPIs

- Compliance audit score: >95%
