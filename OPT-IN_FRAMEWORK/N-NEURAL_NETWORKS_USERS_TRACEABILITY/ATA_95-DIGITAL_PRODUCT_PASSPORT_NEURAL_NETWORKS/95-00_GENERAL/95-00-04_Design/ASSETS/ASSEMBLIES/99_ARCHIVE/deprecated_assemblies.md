# Deprecated Assemblies - Archive

**Purpose**: This document tracks deprecated and retired assembly designs for historical reference.

## Archive Policy

- Assemblies are moved here when superseded by newer designs
- Retain for 5 years minimum for audit trail
- Include rationale for deprecation
- Link to successor assemblies

## Deprecated Assemblies

### None Currently
As of 2025-11-17, no assemblies have been deprecated. This is the initial design baseline.

## Future Deprecation Process

When an assembly is deprecated:

1. **Document Reason**: Why was it deprecated?
2. **Impact Analysis**: What systems were affected?
3. **Migration Path**: How to transition to new design?
4. **Historical Context**: What problem did it solve originally?
5. **Archive**: Move document here with timestamp

## Example Entry Format

```markdown
### 95-00-04-AXXX - Assembly Name (Deprecated YYYY-MM-DD)

**Reason**: Brief explanation of why deprecated

**Successor**: Link to new assembly that replaces this

**Impact**: List of systems/documents affected

**Migration**: Steps to migrate from old to new design

**Historical Context**: What this assembly accomplished
```
