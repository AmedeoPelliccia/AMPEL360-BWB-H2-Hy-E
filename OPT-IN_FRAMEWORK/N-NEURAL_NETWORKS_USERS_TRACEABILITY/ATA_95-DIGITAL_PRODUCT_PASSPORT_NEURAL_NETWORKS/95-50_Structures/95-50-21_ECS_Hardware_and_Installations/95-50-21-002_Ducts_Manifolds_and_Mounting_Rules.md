# 95-50-21-002 Ducts, Manifolds and Mounting Rules

**Document ID:** 95-50-21-002  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-17  
**Status:** Active

---

## 1. Duct Design Requirements

### 1.1 Material Selection
- Aluminum alloy for ambient temperature ducts
- Stainless steel for high-temperature zones
- Composite materials for weight reduction

### 1.2 Pressure Rating
- Maximum operating pressure: 15 psi
- Burst pressure: 45 psi (3x safety factor)
- Leak rate: <0.1 SCFM per joint

---

## 2. Mounting Standards

### 2.1 Support Spacing
- Horizontal ducts: Every 36 inches
- Vertical ducts: Every 48 inches
- Near vibration sources: Every 24 inches

### 2.2 Isolation Requirements
- Vibration isolators for rotating equipment
- Thermal expansion joints for temperature variations
- Flexible couplings at equipment interfaces
