# 95-50-21-003 Sensor and Probe Installations

**Document ID:** 95-50-21-003  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-17  
**Status:** Active

---

## 1. Sensor Types

### 1.1 Temperature Sensors
- RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) probes
- Thermocouples for high-temperature zones
- Digital temperature sensors with AFDX output

### 1.2 Pressure Sensors
- Differential pressure transducers
- Absolute pressure sensors
- Static pressure ports

### 1.3 Flow Sensors
- Hot-wire anemometers
- Venturi flow meters
- Turbine flow meters

---

## 2. Installation Requirements

### 2.1 Probe Insertion Depth
- Duct-mounted probes: 1/3 duct diameter minimum
- Plenum-mounted probes: Center of airflow
- Wall-mounted sensors: Flush with interior surface

### 2.2 Wiring and Cabling
- Shielded twisted-pair for analog sensors
- Fiber optic for high-EMI zones
- AFDX network for digital sensors
