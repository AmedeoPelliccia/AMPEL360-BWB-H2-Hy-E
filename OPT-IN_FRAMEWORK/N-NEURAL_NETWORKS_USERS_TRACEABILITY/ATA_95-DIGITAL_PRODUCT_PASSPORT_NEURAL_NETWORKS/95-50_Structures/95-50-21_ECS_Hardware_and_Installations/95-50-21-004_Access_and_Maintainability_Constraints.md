# 95-50-21-004 Access and Maintainability Constraints

**Document ID:** 95-50-21-004  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-17  
**Status:** Active

---

## 1. Access Requirements

### 1.1 Routine Inspection
- Visual inspection through access ports
- Borescope inspection for hidden areas
- Leak detection procedures

### 1.2 Component Replacement
- Sensor replacement without duct removal
- Filter access panels (quick-release fasteners)
- Actuator replacement clearances

---

## 2. Maintenance Constraints

### 2.1 Physical Access
- Minimum 6-inch clearance for hand tools
- 12-inch clearance for sensor probe removal
- Lifting provisions for heavy ducts (>30 lbs)

### 2.2 Operational Constraints
- ECS must remain operational during sensor swaps
- Redundant sensors for critical measurements
- Hot-swap capability for digital sensors
