# 95-80-24-003 Distribution Networks and Load Branches

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2025-11-17 | **ATA:** 95-80-24

## Load Classification
1. **Critical Loads** (DAL-A): Flight controls, avionics core
2. **Essential Loads**: Communications, navigation
3. **Non-Essential Loads**: IFE, galley, cabin services

## Distribution Topology
- **Ring Bus**: Cross-tie capability between buses
- **Selective Distribution**: SSPCs for each load branch
- **Emergency Bus**: Battery-backed for 30 minutes

## Load Branches
- Propulsion motors: 2.8 MW peak
- Avionics: 15-40 kW
- ECS/Hydraulics: 50-80 kW
- Cabin/IFE: 20-60 kW
