# 95-80-24-002 Generation Sources and Buses

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2025-11-17 | **ATA:** 95-80-24

## Fuel Cell Stacks
- **Type**: PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane)
- **Configuration**: 2× 1.25 MW stacks (redundant)
- **Efficiency**: 55-60% (electrical)
- **Operating Temperature**: 65-80°C
- **Output**: 270 VDC nominal

## DC Bus Architecture
- **Primary Buses**: 270 VDC, dual redundant
- **Secondary Buses**: 28 VDC for avionics
- **AC Buses**: 115/200 VAC, 400 Hz (minimal use)
- **Cross-Tie**: Automatic load balancing

## Power Conditioning
- **DC/DC Converters**: 96-98% efficiency
- **Voltage Regulation**: ±5% tolerance
- **Protection**: Solid-state power controllers (SSPCs)
