# 95-80-24-004 Protection and Power Quality Limits

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2025-11-17 | **ATA:** 95-80-24

## Protection Systems
- **Overcurrent**: SSPCs with <10ms trip time
- **Overvoltage**: 300 VDC trip threshold
- **Undervoltage**: 240 VDC warning, 220 VDC trip
- **Ground Fault**: Isolated return monitoring

## Power Quality Limits
- **Voltage Regulation**: ±5% on 270 VDC bus
- **THD**: <5% (target <3%)
- **Ripple**: <2% peak-to-peak
- **Frequency Stability** (AC): ±0.5 Hz at 400 Hz

## Compliance
- **DO-160**: Environmental qualification
- **MIL-STD-704**: Power characteristics
- **RTCA/DO-311**: Power quality requirements
