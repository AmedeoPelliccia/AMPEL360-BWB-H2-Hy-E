# 95-80-28-004 Energy Efficiency and Losses

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2025-11-17 | **ATA:** 95-80-28

## Loss Breakdown
1. **Tank Boil-Off**: 0.3-0.5% per day
2. **Vaporization**: 2-3% of energy for phase change
3. **Piping/Pressure Drop**: 1-2%
4. **Fuel Cell Stack**: 40-45% (inherent electrochemical)
5. **Power Conditioning**: 2-3%

## Efficiency Metrics
- **Tank-to-Bus Efficiency**: ~55% (LH₂ to 270 VDC)
- **H₂ Utilization**: >98% (minimal unreacted H₂)
- **System Efficiency**: 53-57% (accounting for all losses)

**Target**: Maintain >55% overall efficiency cruise conditions
