# 95-80-28-002 LH2 from Tanks to Conversion Units

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2025-11-17 | **ATA:** 95-80-28

## LH₂ Storage
- **Capacity**: 3× 400 kg tanks = 1200 kg total
- **Temperature**: 20 K (-253°C)
- **Pressure**: 3-5 bar (operational)
- **Energy Content**: 33.3 kWh/kg × 1200 kg = 39,960 kWh

## Flow Path
1. **Tank Outlets**: Pressure control valves
2. **Manifold**: Distribution to fuel cell stacks
3. **Vaporizers**: LH₂ → GH₂ (gaseous)
4. **Flow Measurement**: Coriolis mass flow meters (±2% accuracy)
5. **Fuel Cell Inlets**: Pressure regulation to stack requirements

## Boil-Off Management
- **Rate**: <0.5% per day (target: 0.3%)
- **Recovery**: Boil-off gas used for fuel cell or vented
- **Thermal Management**: Vacuum-insulated tanks with MLI
