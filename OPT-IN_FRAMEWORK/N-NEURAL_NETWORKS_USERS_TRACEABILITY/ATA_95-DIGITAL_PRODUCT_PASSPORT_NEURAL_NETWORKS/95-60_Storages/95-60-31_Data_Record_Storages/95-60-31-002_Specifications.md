# 95-60-31-002 Data Record Storages Specifications

**Document ID:** 95-60-31-002  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-17  
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

## 1. Purpose

This document provides detailed specifications for flight data recorders, cockpit voice recorders, and data hubs, including capacity, materials, operating ranges, and performance requirements.

---

## 2. Storage Specifications

### 2.1 Capacity Requirements

Detailed capacity data is provided in the ASSETS directory (see [ASSETS/](ASSETS/)).

### 2.2 Physical Specifications

- **Materials**: Specified per storage type (see individual storage datasheets)
- **Temperature Range**: Operating temperature limits per storage classification
- **Pressure Rating**: Maximum and minimum operating pressures
- **Volume/Capacity**: Nominal and usable capacity specifications

### 2.3 Performance Requirements

- **Fill/Drain Rates**: Maximum flow rates for filling and draining
- **Response Time**: Time to reach operational state
- **Efficiency**: Energy/fluid retention efficiency
- **Service Life**: Design service life in hours/cycles

---

## 3. Classification

All storages in this category are classified according to:
- **Storage Classes**: Defined in [95-60-00-004](../../00_META/95-60-00-004_Storage_Classes_and_Capacity_Bands.md)
- **Capacity Bands**: Sizing bands per storage type
- **Criticality Levels**: CRITICAL, ESSENTIAL, IMPORTANT, or AUXILIARY

---

## 4. Materials and Construction

### 4.1 Material Selection Criteria

- Compatibility with stored media
- Temperature and pressure requirements
- Corrosion resistance
- Weight optimization
- Sustainability and recyclability (95-30 Circularity)

### 4.2 Construction Standards

- Applicable standards: AS9100, ARP specifications, regulatory requirements
- Quality control: Inspection and testing protocols
- Certification: Required certifications and approvals

---

## 5. Interfaces

### 5.1 Mechanical Interfaces

- Mounting points and structural attachment
- Fluid/electrical connections
- Access panels and service points

### 5.2 Electrical Interfaces

- Power supply requirements
- Signal interfaces (sensors, control)
- Communication protocols (CAN, ARINC 429, Ethernet)

### 5.3 Fluid Interfaces

- Inlet/outlet connections
- Vent and relief systems
- Sampling and service ports
