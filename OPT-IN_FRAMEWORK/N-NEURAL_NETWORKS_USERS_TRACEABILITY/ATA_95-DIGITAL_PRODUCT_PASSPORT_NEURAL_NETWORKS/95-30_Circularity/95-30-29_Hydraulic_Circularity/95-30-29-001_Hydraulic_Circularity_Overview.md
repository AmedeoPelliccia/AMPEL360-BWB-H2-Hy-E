### 3.1 Primary Objectives
- **Objective 1**: Hydraulic Energy Recovery: 80% efficiency target
- **Objective 2**: Accumulator Management: Optimize charge/discharge
- **Objective 3**: Leak Prevention: <1% fluid loss
