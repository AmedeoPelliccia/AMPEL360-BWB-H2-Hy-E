## 2. System Description

The APU plays a critical role in the overall aircraft circularity strategy by focusing on energy recovery, emissions minimization, resource sharing.

## 3. Circularity Objectives

### 3.1 Primary Objectives
- **Objective 1**: APU Load Optimization: 90-100% optimal loading
- **Objective 2**: APU Heat Recovery: 68% efficiency target
- **Objective 3**: Power Sharing with Main Grid: Bidirectional flow

### 3.2 Cross-System Integration

The APU circularity implementation interfaces with:
- ATA-24 (Electrical)
- ATA-21 (ECS)

## 4. Circular Loops Overview

### 4.1 Material Flows
The system manages material flows to minimize waste and maximize reuse through closed-loop designs.

### 4.2 Energy Flows
Energy recovery and optimization are implemented throughout the system to minimize overall aircraft energy consumption.

### 4.3 Information Flows
All circularity data is captured in the Digital Product Passport (DPP) for lifecycle tracking and optimization.
