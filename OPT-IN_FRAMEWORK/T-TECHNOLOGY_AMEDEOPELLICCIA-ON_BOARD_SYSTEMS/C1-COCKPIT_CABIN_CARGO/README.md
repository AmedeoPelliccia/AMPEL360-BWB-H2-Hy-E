## Overview

The C1-COCKPIT_CABIN_CARGO domain encompasses all systems related to the cockpit, cabin, and cargo areas, including furnishings, equipment, lighting, oxygen, and passenger systems.

## Scope

Cockpit, cabin, and cargo systems include:
- **Cockpit**: Flight deck layout and equipment
- **Cabin**: Passenger areas and amenities
- **Cargo**: Cargo compartments and handling
- **Equipment**: Seats, galleys, lavatories
- **Systems**: Lighting, oxygen, cabin management
