## Overview

The P-PROPULSION domain encompasses all propulsion-related systems including engines, propellers/propulsors, fuel systems, and engine control systems for the hydrogen-electric aircraft.

## Scope

Propulsion systems include:
- **Engine Core**: Gas turbine or electric motor core systems
- **Propellers/Propulsors**: Open-fan or ducted-fan propulsion
- **Fuel Systems**: Hydrogen fuel delivery and control
- **Engine Controls**: FADEC and power management
- **Accessories**: Lubrication, cooling, starting systems
