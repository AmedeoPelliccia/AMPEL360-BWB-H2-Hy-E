## Overview

The M-MECHANICS domain encompasses all mechanical systems on board the aircraft, including actuation systems, hydraulic power, landing gear, and mechanical interfaces between systems.

## Scope

Mechanical systems include:
- **Actuation Systems**: Mechanical and hydraulic actuators for flight controls
- **Hydraulic Power**: Hydraulic power generation and distribution
- **Landing Gear**: Main and nose landing gear systems
- **Vacuum and Waste**: Mechanical waste disposal systems
- **Water Ballast**: Ballast management systems

## AMPEL360 Specific Considerations

The hydrogen-electric BWB configuration requires:
- Advanced actuation systems for the large control surfaces
- Electric and hydraulic hybrid actuators
- Specialized landing gear for the BWB configuration
- Integration with the distributed propulsion system
