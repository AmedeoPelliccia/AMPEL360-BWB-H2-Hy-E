# ATA 57 - WINGS

## Overview
Wing structures and systems for AMPEL360 BWB H2 Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA aircraft featuring:
- **Blended Wing Body (BWB) Configuration:** Highly integrated wing-body structure with aerodynamic blending
- **Advanced Composite Construction:** Carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP) with advanced materials
- **Distributed Electric Propulsion Integration:** Multiple propulsor mounting provisions
- **CAOS Integration:** Comprehensive structural health monitoring and load management

## BWB-Specific Considerations

### Blended Wing Body Integration
- **Aerodynamic Blending:** Smooth transition between wing and fuselage surfaces
- **Load Distribution:** Distributed loads across wide chord BWB structure
- **Structural Efficiency:** Box structure optimization for BWB configuration
- **Fuel Volume:** Large internal volume for fuel storage

### Distributed Electric Propulsion
- **Multiple Propulsors:** 8x open-fan electric propulsor mounting points
- **Load Management:** Individual propulsor thrust and moment loads
- **System Integration:** Electrical power distribution and control
- **Maintenance Access:** Service provisions for distributed propulsors
