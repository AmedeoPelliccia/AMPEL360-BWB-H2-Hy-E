# Stakeholder Identification - Door L3 Aft Emergency Exit

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-52-20-01-STAKE  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Date:** 2025-11-04

## 1. PRIMARY STAKEHOLDERS

### 1.1 Safety Critical

#### Passengers
- **Interest:** Safe, rapid evacuation
- **Requirements:** Intuitive operation, clear path, confidence
- **Influence:** Ultimate safety validation
- **Communication:** Via airline training

#### Cabin Crew
- **Interest:** Reliable, easy operation
- **Requirements:** <8 second opening, clear procedures
- **Influence:** Operational feedback
- **Communication:** Training materials, procedures

#### Pilots
- **Interest:** System status, remote monitoring
- **Requirements:** Clear indications, override capability
- **Influence:** Cockpit interface requirements
- **Communication:** Flight manual, ECAM/EICAS

### 1.2 Regulatory

#### EASA
- **Interest:** Compliance with CS-25
- **Requirements:** Certification evidence, test data
- **Influence:** Approval authority
- **Communication:** Formal certification documents

#### National Authorities
- **Interest:** Local compliance
- **Requirements:** Language, specific rules
- **Influence:** Operating approval
- **Communication:** Supplemental docs

### 1.3 Business

#### Airlines (Customers)
- **Interest:** Reliability, cost, efficiency
- **Requirements:** High dispatch reliability, low maintenance
- **Influence:** Purchase decisions
- **Communication:** Sales materials, business case

#### Insurance
- **Interest:** Safety record, risk assessment
- **Requirements:** Design validation, test evidence
- **Influence:** Operating costs
- **Communication:** Safety reports

## 2. SECONDARY STAKEHOLDERS

### 2.1 Technical Teams

#### Design Engineering
- **Role:** Create door system
- **Needs:** Clear requirements, FEA tools
- **Deliverables:** Design documents, 3D models

#### Manufacturing
- **Role:** Build door system
- **Needs:** Producible design, clear specs
- **Deliverables:** Production plans, quality procedures

#### Maintenance
- **Role:** Keep doors operational
- **Needs:** Access, diagnostics, procedures
- **Deliverables:** Maintenance manual, training

#### CAOS Team
- **Role:** Smart system integration
- **Needs:** Sensor data, control interfaces
- **Deliverables:** ML models, dashboards

### 2.2 Supply Chain

#### Slide Manufacturer
- **Role:** Provide evacuation slides
- **Needs:** Specifications, interfaces
- **Deliverables:** Certified slide system

#### Actuator Supplier
- **Role:** Provide actuation system
- **Needs:** Load requirements, control interface
- **Deliverables:** Qualified actuators

#### Material Suppliers
- **Role:** Composite materials, hardware
- **Needs:** Specifications, quantities
- **Deliverables:** Certified materials
