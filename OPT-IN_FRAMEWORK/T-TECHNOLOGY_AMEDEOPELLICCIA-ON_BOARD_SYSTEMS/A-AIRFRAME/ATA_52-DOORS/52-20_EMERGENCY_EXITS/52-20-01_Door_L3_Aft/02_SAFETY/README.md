## Critical Safety Aspects

⚠️ **EMERGENCY EXIT SPECIFIC**
- 90-second evacuation requirement ([CS 25.803](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27))
- Panic scenario management
- Power-loss operation critical
- Slide deployment hazards
- BWB aft flow dynamics
