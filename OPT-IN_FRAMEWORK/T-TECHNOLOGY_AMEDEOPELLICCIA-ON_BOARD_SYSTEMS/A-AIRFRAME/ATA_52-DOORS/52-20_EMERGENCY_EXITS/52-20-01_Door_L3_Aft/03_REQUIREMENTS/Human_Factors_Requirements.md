# Human Factors Requirements - Door L3 Aft Emergency Exit

**Document ID:** AMPEL360-52-20-01-REQ-HF  
**Version:** 0.9  
**Date:** 2025-11-04

## Human Factors Requirements

### Physical Operation

#### HF-52-20-01-001 Operating_Forces
- **Requirement:** Maximum 30 lbf for manual operation
- **Population:** 5th percentile female to 95th percentile male
- **Conditions:** All environmental conditions
- **Verification:** Human subject testing

#### HF-52-20-01-002 Handle_Accessibility
- **Requirement:** Reachable from floor level
- **Height Range:** 1000-1500mm from floor
- **Reach Envelope:** Per MIL-STD-1472
- **Verification:** Mockup evaluation

#### HF-52-20-01-003 Visual_Indicators
- **Requirement:** Visible in all lighting conditions
- **Luminance:** Min 10 cd/m² in dark
- **Contrast Ratio:** 3:1 minimum
- **Verification:** Photometric testing

### Cognitive Factors

#### HF-52-20-01-004 Operation_Intuitiveness
- **Requirement:** Operable without training
- **Error Rate:** <5% naive subjects
- **Decision Time:** <3 seconds
- **Verification:** Human subject testing

#### HF-52-20-01-005 Emergency_Marking
- **Requirement:** Universal emergency symbols
- **Standards:** ISO 7010, ATA Spec 100
- **Languages:** Pictograms only
- **Verification:** Recognition testing

### Panic Conditions

#### HF-52-20-01-006 Panic_Operation
- **Requirement:** Operable under high stress
- **Force Multiplication:** 2x normal anticipated
- **Crowd Pressure:** 250 lbf/ft²
- **Verification:** Simulation + analysis

#### HF-52-20-01-007 Flow_Management
- **Requirement:** Natural passenger flow
- **Bottleneck Prevention:** No single points
- **Guidance:** Intuitive path marking
- **Verification:** Evacuation demonstration

#### HF-52-20-01-008 Crew_Workload
- **Requirement:** Single crew member operation
- **Task Time:** <10 seconds total
- **Concurrent Tasks:** Door + commands
- **Verification:** Task analysis + demo
