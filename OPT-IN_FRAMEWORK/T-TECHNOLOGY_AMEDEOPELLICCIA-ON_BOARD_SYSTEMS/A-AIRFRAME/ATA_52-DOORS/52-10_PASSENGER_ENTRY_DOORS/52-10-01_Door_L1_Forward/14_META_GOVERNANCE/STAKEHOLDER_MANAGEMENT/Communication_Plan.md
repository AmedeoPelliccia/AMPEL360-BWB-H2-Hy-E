# Communication Plan
## Door L1 Forward Stakeholder Communications

### 1. COMMUNICATION OBJECTIVES

- Keep stakeholders informed of progress
- Facilitate decision-making
- Manage expectations
- Resolve issues promptly
- Build trust and collaboration

### 2. STAKEHOLDER COMMUNICATION MATRIX

| Stakeholder | Method | Frequency | Content | Owner |
|-------------|--------|-----------|---------|-------|
| Program Manager | Status reports | Weekly | Progress, issues, risks | Design Lead |
| Chief Engineer | Technical reviews | Bi-weekly | Design decisions | Design Lead |
| Design Team | Stand-ups | Daily | Tasks, blockers | Design Lead |
| Safety Engineer | Safety reviews | Weekly | Safety items | Safety Rep |
| Cert Engineer | Compliance status | Bi-weekly | Cert progress | Cert Lead |
| Quality Manager | Quality metrics | Weekly | QA status | QA Lead |
| Manufacturing | Design reviews | Monthly | Producibility | Design Lead |
| Maintenance | Maintainability | Monthly | Service items | Maint Rep |
| Customer | Progress reports | Quarterly | Major milestones | Program Mgr |
| Regulatory | Compliance reports | Quarterly | Cert status | Cert Lead |

### 3. COMMUNICATION CHANNELS

#### Meetings
- **Daily Stand-ups:** 15 minutes, design team
- **Weekly Status:** 1 hour, core team
- **Design Reviews:** 2-4 hours, extended team
- **CRB Meetings:** 2 hours, change review board
- **Quarterly Reviews:** Half day, all stakeholders

#### Written Communications
- **Status Reports:** Weekly email summary
- **Technical Memos:** As needed for decisions
- **Meeting Minutes:** Within 24 hours
- **Action Items:** Tracked in shared system
- **Newsletters:** Monthly program update

#### Collaboration Tools
- **Repository:** Git for documentation
- **Issue Tracking:** CSV-based system
- **File Sharing:** Shared drive
- **Video Conferencing:** For remote meetings
- **Email:** Formal communications

### 4. MEETING MANAGEMENT

#### Meeting Types

**Standing Meetings:**
- Daily stand-up (Design team)
- Weekly status (Core team)
- Bi-weekly technical review
- Monthly all-hands

**Ad-hoc Meetings:**
- Issue resolution
- Decision-making
- Urgent reviews
- Crisis management

#### Meeting Protocols

**Before Meeting:**
- Agenda distributed 24 hours prior
- Materials shared in advance
- Required attendees confirmed
- Video link tested

**During Meeting:**
- Start on time
- Follow agenda
- Document decisions
- Track action items
- End on time

**After Meeting:**
- Minutes published within 24 hours
- Actions assigned with due dates
- Materials archived
- Follow-up scheduled

### 5. ESCALATION PROCESS

**Level 1:** Team Lead (resolve within 2 days)  
**Level 2:** Program Manager (resolve within 1 week)  
**Level 3:** Chief Engineer (resolve within 2 weeks)  
**Level 4:** Executive Management (expedited)

### 6. REPORTING STRUCTURE

#### Weekly Status Report
- Accomplishments
- Planned activities
- Issues and risks
- Decisions needed
- Metrics snapshot

#### Monthly Progress Report
- Milestone status
- Budget vs. actual
- Schedule status
- Risk summary
- Quality metrics

#### Quarterly Executive Summary
- Program health
- Key achievements
- Major decisions
- Upcoming milestones
- Resource needs

### 7. CHANGE COMMUNICATION

All approved changes communicated via:
1. Change notification email
2. Update to affected documents
3. Discussion in next team meeting
4. Training if process changes
5. Documentation in lessons learned

### 8. CRISIS COMMUNICATION

**Trigger Events:**
- Safety issue identified
- Major schedule slip
- Budget overrun >10%
- Quality escape
- Stakeholder conflict

**Response:**
- Immediate notification to Program Manager
- Emergency meeting within 24 hours
- Action plan developed
- Daily updates until resolved
- Post-crisis review

### 9. FEEDBACK MECHANISMS

- Suggestion box (anonymous)
- Quarterly satisfaction surveys
- Open door policy
- Retrospectives after milestones
- Exit interviews

### 10. CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

Communication plan reviewed:
- After each major milestone
- Quarterly as part of process review
- When stakeholder changes occur
- Based on feedback received
