## Overview

The META_GOVERNANCE structure provides comprehensive oversight, control, and continuous improvement for all documentation related to the Door L1 Forward system. This governance layer ensures accuracy, compliance, traceability, and quality throughout the product lifecycle.

## Purpose

The 14_META_GOVERNANCE folder serves as the central control system for:

- **Document Management** - Version control, approval workflows, and lifecycle management
- **Change Control** - Systematic management of all changes with impact assessment
- **Quality Assurance** - Standards enforcement and continuous improvement
- **Compliance Tracking** - Regulatory and standards compliance monitoring
- **Risk Management** - Identification, assessment, and mitigation of risks
- **Stakeholder Management** - Communication, collaboration, and alignment
- **Knowledge Management** - Capture and preservation of organizational knowledge
- **Performance Monitoring** - Metrics, KPIs, and trend analysis

## Usage Guidelines

### For Document Authors
1. Review document management plan before creating documents
2. Use approved templates from `DOCUMENT_CONTROL/Document_Templates/`
3. Follow naming conventions
4. Submit changes through change control process
5. Ensure all reviews are completed

### For Reviewers
1. Use quality checklists from `QUALITY_ASSURANCE/Quality_Checks/`
2. Document all review comments
3. Track review status in document index
4. Complete reviews within agreed timeframes

### For Program Management
1. Monitor governance dashboard weekly
2. Review metrics monthly
3. Address critical issues promptly
4. Support continuous improvement initiatives

### For Auditors
1. All audit evidence is traceable
2. Compliance status is tracked by standard
3. Non-conformances are managed systematically
4. Quality records are retained per schedule
