## 2. SPACE REQUIREMENTS

### Production Areas

#### Clean Room (200 m²)
- **Classification:** ISO Class 8 (100,000 particles/ft³)
- **Temperature:** 23±2°C continuously controlled
- **Humidity:** 50±10% RH
- **Pressure:** Positive pressure +5 Pa
- **Air changes:** 15 per hour minimum
- **Access:** Gowning area with air shower
- **Lighting:** 800 lux minimum
- **Floor:** ESD-safe epoxy coating
- **Purpose:** Composite layup operations

#### Trim/Drill Area (150 m²)
- **Environment:** Standard shop (18-26°C)
- **Floor loading:** 500 kg/m²
- **Services:** Compressed air, dust collection
- **Equipment:** CNC trim capability, drill press
- **Purpose:** Part trimming, hole drilling

#### Assembly Area (200 m²)
- **Environment:** Standard shop
- **Ceiling height:** 5 m minimum
- **Overhead crane:** 2-ton capacity
- **Floor:** Painted concrete, level
- **Purpose:** Mechanism installation, final assembly

#### Material Storage (300 m²)
- **Climate controlled:** 150 m² at 23°C, 50% RH
- **Freezer storage:** 50 m² at -18±2°C
- **Racking system:** 4m high, fire-rated
- **Access control:** Limited personnel
- **Purpose:** Raw material and consumables storage

#### Quality Laboratory (50 m²)
- **Environment:** Clean, controlled (Class 100,000)
- **Temperature:** 20±1°C (for dimensional stability)
- **Vibration:** Isolated floor for CMM
- **Equipment space:** CMM, inspection benches
- **Purpose:** Dimensional inspection, documentation

---

## 3. SUPPORT AREAS

### Paint Booth (40 m²)
- **Type:** Downdraft paint booth
- **Filtration:** HEPA inlet, activated carbon exhaust
- **Airflow:** 0.4 m/s face velocity
- **Lighting:** Explosion-proof, 1000 lux
- **Purpose:** Primer and topcoat application

### Offices (100 m²)
- **Configuration:** Open plan + 2 private offices
- **Occupancy:** 5 workstations
- **IT infrastructure:** Network, phones, printers
- **Purpose:** Engineering, planning, management

### Break Room / Locker Room (50 m²)
- **Amenities:** Lockers, tables, kitchenette
- **Restrooms:** 2 (ADA compliant)
- **Purpose:** Employee facilities

### Utilities / Mechanical (100 m²)
- **Contents:** HVAC equipment, compressors, electrical panels
- **Access:** Maintenance only
- **Purpose:** Building systems

---

## 4. UTILITIES REQUIREMENTS

### Electrical
- **Main service:** 400A, 480V 3-phase
- **Distribution:** Multiple panels throughout
- **Backup power:** UPS for critical systems (freezer, clean room)
- **Consumption:** ~150 kW average, 250 kW peak

### HVAC
- **Clean room:** Dedicated 100% outside air system
- **Production:** Standard comfort HVAC
- **Paint booth:** Dedicated exhaust system
- **Capacity:** 500 kW cooling, 300 kW heating

### Compressed Air
- **Pressure:** 7 bar (100 psi)
- **Flow:** 50 CFM
- **Quality:** Oil-free, dried (-40°C dewpoint)
- **Distribution:** Piped to all workstations

### Water
- **Domestic:** Standard municipal supply
- **Fire suppression:** Sprinkler system throughout
- **Flow:** 100 gpm available

### Data/Telecom
- **Network:** 1 Gb fiber internet
- **WiFi:** Coverage throughout facility
- **Phone:** VoIP system
- **Purpose:** MES, email, documentation

---

## 5. ENVIRONMENTAL CONTROLS

### Temperature Monitoring
- **Sensors:** Every 50 m² in critical areas
- **Alarm:** Notify if out of range
- **Data logging:** Continuous recording
- **Calibration:** Annual

### Humidity Monitoring
- **Sensors:** Clean room, material storage
- **Alarm:** Notify if out of range
- **Dehumidification:** As needed in storage

### Particle Counting
- **Clean room:** Daily particle count
- **Alarm:** If exceeds Class 8 limits
- **Certification:** Annual by third party

---

## 6. SAFETY SYSTEMS

### Fire Protection
- **Sprinklers:** Throughout per NFPA 13
- **Fire extinguishers:** Per NFPA 10
- **Fire alarm:** Addressable system
- **Emergency lighting:** Battery backup

### Hazmat Storage
- **Flammable cabinet:** For solvents, adhesives
- **Ventilation:** Exhausted to exterior
- **Spill containment:** Secondary containment
- **Signage:** NFPA diamonds, GHS labels

### Emergency Response
- **Eyewash/shower:** Near chemical use areas
- **First aid:** Kits throughout facility
- **AED:** 1 unit centrally located
- **Evacuation plan:** Posted, practiced quarterly

## 7. SECURITY

### Access Control
- **Badge system:** RFID entry badges
- **Cameras:** Interior and exterior coverage
- **Recording:** 30-day retention
- **Hours:** 24/7 production access

### Controlled Areas
- **Material storage:** Limited access
- **Clean room:** Trained personnel only
- **Quality lab:** QC personnel only
- **IT room:** Management only

## 8. LAYOUT CONSIDERATIONS

### Material Flow
- **Receiving dock:** Adjacent to storage
- **Storage to clean room:** Short path
- **Production flow:** U-shaped, minimize backtracking
- **Shipping:** Near final assembly

### Personnel Flow
- **Entry:** Single point with gowning area for clean room
- **Break room:** Central location
- **Offices:** Overlooking production floor
- **Restrooms:** Accessible from all areas

### Equipment Flow
- **Overhead crane:** Covers assembly area
- **Forklifts:** Access to receiving, storage
- **Carts:** Throughout production areas
- **Elevators:** None required (single story)
