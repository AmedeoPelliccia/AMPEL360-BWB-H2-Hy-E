## Critical Path Items

### Priority 1 (Immediate - Next 6 Months)
1. **Material Validation**
   - Purchase prepreg samples
   - Conduct mechanical property tests
   - Validate material specifications
   
2. **Process Demonstration**
   - Hand layup trials on simple panels
   - Autoclave service provider qualification
   - NDT capability demonstration

3. **Design Validation**
   - Professional FEA analysis
   - Replace AI estimates with engineering calculations
   - Complete detailed drawings

### Priority 2 (6-12 Months)
1. **Tooling Design**
   - Cure mold conceptual design
   - Assembly fixture concepts
   - Trim and drill fixture concepts

2. **Facility Planning**
   - Identify potential manufacturing locations
   - Clean room requirements definition
   - Equipment layout studies

3. **Supplier Engagement**
   - Identify potential prepreg suppliers
   - Engage core material suppliers
   - Contact autoclave service providers

### Priority 3 (12-18 Months)
1. **Prototype Planning**
   - First article manufacturing plan
   - Test article requirements
   - Prototype validation plan

2. **Quality System**
   - AS9100 readiness assessment
   - Quality manual development
   - Inspection procedures
