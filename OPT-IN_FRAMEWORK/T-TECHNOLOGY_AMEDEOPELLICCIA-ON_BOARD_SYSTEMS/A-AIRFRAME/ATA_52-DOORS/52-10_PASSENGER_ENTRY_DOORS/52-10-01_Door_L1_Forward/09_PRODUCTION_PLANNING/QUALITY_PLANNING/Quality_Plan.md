## 1. SCOPE

This Quality Plan defines the quality management approach for the production of Door L1 Forward assemblies, ensuring compliance with AS9100 aerospace quality standards and customer requirements.

### Applicability
- Composite panel manufacturing
- Mechanism assembly and integration
- Final testing and delivery
- All production phases (prototype through full-rate)

## 7. INSPECTION AND TESTING

### Inspection Points
1. **Receiving:** Material C of C, visual inspection
2. **Post-Layup:** Ply count, orientation, photo documentation
3. **Post-Cure:** Dimensional check, NDT (100%)
4. **Post-Trim:** Edge quality, hole positions
5. **Final Assembly:** Function test, leak test, weight

### Inspection Methods

| Parameter | Method | Frequency | Acceptance Criteria |
|-----------|--------|-----------|---------------------|
| Dimensions | CMM | 100% | ±2 mm |
| Weight | Scale | 100% | 72±7 kg |
| NDT voids | C-scan | 100% | <2% |
| Delamination | C-scan | 100% | None >25mm |
| Leak rate | Pressure decay | 100% | <0.05 CFM |
| Function | Cycles | 100% | 50 cycles no fail |

### Test Equipment Calibration
- All measuring equipment calibrated per schedule
- NIST-traceable standards
- Calibration labels affixed
- Out-of-tolerance investigation and recall
- Annual calibration for most equipment

## 8. NON-CONFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

### Non-Conformance Report (NCR) Process
1. **Identification:** Any employee can initiate NCR
2. **Documentation:** NCR form with photos, dimensions
3. **Containment:** Quarantine non-conforming material
4. **Evaluation:** Quality engineer reviews
5. **Disposition:** Use-as-is, rework, repair, or scrap
6. **Verification:** Re-inspection after rework/repair
7. **Closure:** Sign-off and record retention

### Disposition Authority
- **Use-as-is:** Engineering + Quality Manager + Customer (if required)
- **Rework:** Quality Engineer
- **Scrap:** Quality Manager
- **Return to Supplier:** Quality Manager

### Corrective Action
- Root cause analysis (5-Why, Fishbone)
- Corrective action plan with timeline
- Verification of effectiveness
- Preventive action for systemic issues

---

## 9. FIRST ARTICLE INSPECTION (FAI)

### AS9102 Compliance
- Full FAI per AS9102 Form 3 for first production unit
- Partial FAI for design changes affecting form, fit, function
- Documented evidence of conformance to all requirements

### FAI Process
1. **Planning:** FAI plan developed
2. **Inspection:** 100% dimensional layout
3. **Testing:** All functional tests performed
4. **Documentation:** AS9102 forms completed
5. **Review:** Customer review and approval
6. **Retention:** Records retained per contract

### FAI Reporting
- Form 1: Part number and accountability
- Form 2: Product accountability
- Form 3: Characteristic accountability and verification
- Supporting data: Certs, test reports, photos

1. **Planning:** Audit schedule and checklist

3. **Reporting:** Written report with findings

5. **Follow-up:** Verify corrective action effectiveness

### Auditor Qualification
- Internal auditor training (AS9100)
- Independence from audited area
- Annual refresher training
- Lead auditor for system audits
