## 1. TEST OBJECTIVES

Demonstrate that door can sustain limit load (11.33 psi) with 50mm diameter damage without catastrophic failure.

---

## 2. TEST REQUIREMENTS

### 2.1 Damage Scenarios
- **Scenario 1:** 50mm circular hole at panel center
- **Scenario 2:** Impact damage (25J impact energy)
- **Scenario 3:** Delamination (75mm Ã— 75mm)

### 2.2 Acceptance Criteria
**PASS:** Structure sustains 11.33 psi limit load for 3 minutes with damage present

---

## 3. TEST PROCEDURE

### 3.1 Damage Introduction
1. Inspect undamaged panel
2. Create 50mm hole at specified location
3. Document damage configuration
4. Re-inspect with NDT

### 3.2 Residual Strength Test
1. Install in pressure chamber
2. Ramp to limit load (11.33 psi)
3. Hold for 3 minutes
4. Monitor crack propagation
5. Depressurize and inspect

### 3.3 Acceptance
- No catastrophic failure
- Controlled crack growth only
- Residual strength > limit load
