## 2. MOC CATEGORIES

### 2.1 Tests (T)
Physical testing of hardware to demonstrate compliance.

### 2.2 Analysis (A)
Engineering analysis using validated methods.

### 2.3 Inspection (I)
Physical examination and measurement.

### 2.4 Demonstration (D)
Functional demonstration of capability.
