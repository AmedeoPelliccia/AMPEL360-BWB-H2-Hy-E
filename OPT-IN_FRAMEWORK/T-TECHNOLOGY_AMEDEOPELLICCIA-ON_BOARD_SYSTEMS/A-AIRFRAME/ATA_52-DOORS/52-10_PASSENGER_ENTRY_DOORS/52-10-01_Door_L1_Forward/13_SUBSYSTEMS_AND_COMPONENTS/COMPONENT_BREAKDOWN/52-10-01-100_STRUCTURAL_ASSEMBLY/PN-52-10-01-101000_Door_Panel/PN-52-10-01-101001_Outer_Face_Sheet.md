# Door Panel - Outer Face Sheet
## PN-52-10-01-101001

### Specifications

| Parameter | Value | Standard |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Material | Carbon Fiber/Epoxy (CFRP) | AMS3895 |
| Dimensions | 2200 × 1800 × 3 mm | DWG-52-10-01-101001 |
| Weight | 25.8 kg | ±0.5 kg tolerance |
| Fiber orientation | [0/45/-45/90]s | Layup specification |
| Cure temperature | 180°C | Per process spec |
| Glass transition temp | 190°C | ASTM D4065 |

### Description
The outer face sheet forms the aerodynamic surface of Door L1 Forward, providing structural integrity, lightning protection, and environmental sealing.

### Key Features
- Lightning strike protection integrated
- Smooth Class A surface finish
- Erosion-resistant coating
- Fire barrier certification
- Impact resistance to 25 J

### Manufacturing Process
1. Ply layup on female mold
2. Lightning protection mesh integration
3. Vacuum bagging
4. Autoclave cure (180°C, 6 bar, 2 hours)
5. Trim to final dimensions
6. Surface preparation and coating
7. NDT inspection (ultrasonic C-scan)
8. Final inspection and serialization

### Quality Requirements
- Porosity: <2% by ultrasonic inspection
- Fiber volume fraction: 58-62%
- Surface waviness: <0.5mm per meter
- Delamination: None acceptable
- Lightning protection: <5 milliohms surface resistance

### Maintenance
- A-Check: Visual inspection for damage
- C-Check: Tap test, visual inspection
- D-Check: Ultrasonic inspection

### Repair
Minor damage (<25mm): Bonded patch repair
Major damage (>25mm): Panel replacement

### Disposal
- Method: Pyrolysis recycling
- Recovery rate: 85%
- Environmental classification: Non-hazardous (cured)
- Handling: Dust mask during cutting
