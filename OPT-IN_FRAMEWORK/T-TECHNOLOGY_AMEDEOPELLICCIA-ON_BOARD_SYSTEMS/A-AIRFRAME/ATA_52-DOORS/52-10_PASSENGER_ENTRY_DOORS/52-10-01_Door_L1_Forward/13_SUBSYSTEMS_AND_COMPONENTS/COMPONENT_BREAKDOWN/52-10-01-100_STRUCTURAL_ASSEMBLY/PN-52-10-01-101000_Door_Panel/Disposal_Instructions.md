# Door Panel - Disposal Instructions
## Component: PN-52-10-01-101000

### Overview
The door panel assembly consists primarily of CFRP face sheets with Nomex honeycomb core and aluminum edge bands. All materials are recyclable through specialized processes.

### Disassembly Steps

1. **Remove from Frame**
   - Disconnect from frame assembly
   - Remove all mechanical fasteners
   - Document serial number

2. **Separate Edge Bands**
   - Remove aluminum edge bands (PN-52-10-01-101004)
   - Send to aluminum recycling stream
   - Expected value: €6

3. **Prepare CFRP Panels**
   - Remove any remaining adhesive
   - Cut panels to 500mm × 500mm sections
   - Keep face sheets together with core

### Safety Precautions
- Wear dust mask when cutting CFRP
- Use diamond-coated cutting tools
- Avoid grinding (creates hazardous dust)
- Use proper ventilation
- Dispose of dust as hazardous waste

### Environmental Impact
- CO2 savings: 305 kg CO2e per panel
- Energy savings: 2,550 MJ
- Landfill diversion: 50 kg (85%)
