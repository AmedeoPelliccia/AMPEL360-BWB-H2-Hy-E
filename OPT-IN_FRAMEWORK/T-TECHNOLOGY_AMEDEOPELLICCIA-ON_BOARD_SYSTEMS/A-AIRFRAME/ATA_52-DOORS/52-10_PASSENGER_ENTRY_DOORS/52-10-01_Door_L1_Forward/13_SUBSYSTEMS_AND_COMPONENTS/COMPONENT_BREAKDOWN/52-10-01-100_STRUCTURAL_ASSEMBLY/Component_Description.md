# Component Description: Structural Assembly
## PN-52-10-01-100000

### Overview
The structural assembly forms the primary load-bearing framework of Door L1 Forward, consisting of advanced composite panels, aluminum frame members, and titanium reinforcements.

### Major Subassemblies

#### 1. Door Panel Assembly (PN-52-10-01-101000)
**Description:** Composite sandwich construction providing aerodynamic surface and structural integrity.

**Components:**
- Outer Face Sheet (PN-52-10-01-101001): CFRP, 25.8 kg
- Inner Face Sheet (PN-52-10-01-101002): CFRP, 25.8 kg
- Honeycomb Core (PN-52-10-01-101003): Nomex, 4.0 kg
- Edge Bands (PN-52-10-01-101004): Aluminum 7075-T6, 3.2 kg

**Key Features:**
- Lightning strike protection integrated
- Fire barrier certification FAR 25.856
- Impact resistance to 25 J
- Service temperature: -55°C to +85°C

#### 2. Frame Assembly (PN-52-10-01-102000)
**Description:** Aluminum perimeter frame providing mounting interface and load distribution.

**Components:**
- Upper Beam (PN-52-10-01-102001): 14.2 kg
- Lower Beam (PN-52-10-01-102002): 12.1 kg
- Side Rails (PN-52-10-01-102003): 8.9 kg (2x)

**Load Capacity:**
- Ultimate load: 12,000 N
- Limit load: 8,000 N
- Fatigue life: 90,000 cycles

#### 3. Reinforcements (PN-52-10-01-103000)
**Description:** Titanium doublers and fittings at high-stress locations.

**Components:**
- Hinge Doublers (PN-52-10-01-103001): 2.8 kg
- Latch Fittings (PN-52-10-01-103002): 3.5 kg

**Material:** Ti-6Al-4V per AMS4911
**Surface Treatment:** Anodize per AMS2488

### Design Standards
