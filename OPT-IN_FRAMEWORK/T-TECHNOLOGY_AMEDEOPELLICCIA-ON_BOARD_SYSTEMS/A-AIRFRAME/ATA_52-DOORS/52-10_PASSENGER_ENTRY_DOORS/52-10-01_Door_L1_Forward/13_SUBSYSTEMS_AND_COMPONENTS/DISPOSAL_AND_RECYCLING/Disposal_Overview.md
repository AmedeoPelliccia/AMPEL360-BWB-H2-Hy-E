# Disposal and Recycling Overview
## Door L1 Forward - End-of-Life Management

### 1. INTRODUCTION

This document outlines the comprehensive end-of-life management strategy for the Door L1 Forward assembly (PN-52-10-01-000000), ensuring maximum material recovery, environmental compliance, and value optimization.

### 2. DISPOSAL PHILOSOPHY

The AMPEL360 approach to end-of-life management is based on:

1. **Circular Economy Principles** - Design for disassembly and reuse
2. **Maximum Material Recovery** - Target 85%+ diversion from landfill
3. **Value Optimization** - Recover economic value from materials
4. **Environmental Responsibility** - Minimize carbon footprint
5. **Regulatory Compliance** - Meet all applicable waste directives
6. **Digital Traceability** - Complete lifecycle documentation

### 4. RECYCLING STREAMS

#### 4.1 High-Value Metals
- **Titanium (8.4 kg):** Specialty recycling → €420 recovery
- **Aluminum (42.8 kg):** Standard metal recycling → €85 recovery
- **Copper (2.1 kg):** Wire recycling → €19 recovery
- **Steel (6.2 kg):** Scrap metal → €12 recovery

#### 4.2 Advanced Composites
- **CFRP (51.6 kg):** Pyrolysis process
  - Fiber recovery: 85%
  - Resin thermal energy: 15%
  - Recovery value: €155

#### 4.3 Electronics
- **PCBs and Controllers (3.2 kg):** WEEE processing
  - Precious metal recovery (Au, Ag, Pd)
  - Copper recovery
  - Hazmat handling for lead solder
  - Recovery value: €25

#### 4.4 Low-Value Materials
- **Nomex Core (4.0 kg):** Chemical processing, 60% recovery
- **Rubber Seals (4.8 kg):** Energy recovery, 30% material recovery
