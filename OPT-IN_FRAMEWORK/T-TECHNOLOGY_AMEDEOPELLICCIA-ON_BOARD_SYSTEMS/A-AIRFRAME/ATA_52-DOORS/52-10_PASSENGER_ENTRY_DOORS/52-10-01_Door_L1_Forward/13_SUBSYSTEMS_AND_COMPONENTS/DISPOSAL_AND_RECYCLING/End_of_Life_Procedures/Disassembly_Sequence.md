### 2. DISASSEMBLY SEQUENCE

#### Phase 1: Electronics & Software
1. **Download software/data** (PN-52-10-01-401xxx)
   - Extract CAOS data
   - Archive configurations
   - License deactivation
   
2. **Remove sensors** (PN-52-10-01-303xxx)
   - Position sensors → WEEE bin
   - Pressure sensors → WEEE bin
   - Separate batteries → Battery recycling

3. **Remove control units** (PN-52-10-01-302xxx)
   - Door controller → WEEE special handling
   - Junction box → Aluminum recycling

#### Phase 2: Mechanical Systems
1. **Remove actuators** (PN-52-10-01-203xxx)
   - Drain fluids → Chemical disposal
   - Motor → Copper recovery
   - Gearbox → Steel recycling

2. **Remove latches** (PN-52-10-01-201xxx)
   - Stainless steel → Metal recycling
   - Springs → Steel recycling

3. **Remove hinges** (PN-52-10-01-202xxx)
   - Titanium → High-value metal recovery
   - Bushings → Bronze recycling

#### Phase 3: Seals & Consumables
1. **Remove seals** (PN-52-10-01-204xxx)
   - Primary seal → Rubber processing
   - Secondary seal → Energy recovery

2. **Collect fluids/lubricants**
   - Hydraulic fluid → Chemical treatment
   - Greases → Hazmat disposal

#### Phase 4: Structural Components
1. **Separate frame** (PN-52-10-01-102xxx)
   - Aluminum beams → Metal recycling
   - Fasteners → Sorting by material

2. **Process panel** (PN-52-10-01-101xxx)
   - Edge trim → Separate materials
   - Face sheets → CFRP pyrolysis
   - Core → Chemical processing
