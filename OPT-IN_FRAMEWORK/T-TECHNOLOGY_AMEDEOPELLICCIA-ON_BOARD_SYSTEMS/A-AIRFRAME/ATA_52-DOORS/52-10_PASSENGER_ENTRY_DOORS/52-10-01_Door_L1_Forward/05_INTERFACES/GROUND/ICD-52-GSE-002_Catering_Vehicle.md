## 1. INTERFACE OVERVIEW

This ICD defines clearance and safety requirements for catering vehicle operations near Door L1 Forward.

---

## 2. CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS

### 2.1 Vehicle Clearance Zone
- Minimum clearance: 2,000mm from door when open
- Approach path: Clear of door swing envelope
- Parking zone: Marked on ramp per airport requirements
- Safety margin: 500mm additional for operations

### 2.2 Height Clearance
- Door sill height: 2,500mm (empty) to 2,350mm (loaded)
- Vehicle maximum height: 3,500mm
- Clearance to wing: 500mm minimum vertical
- Clearance to fuselage: 300mm minimum horizontal

---

## 3. OPERATIONAL PROCEDURES

### 3.1 Catering Operations Sequence
1. Aircraft parked and chocked
2. Door L1 status verified (closed and locked)
3. Catering truck positioned at designated service doors
4. L1 door area kept clear during catering
5. Visual inspection before passenger boarding begins

### 3.2 Coordination
- Ramp coordination: Ground crew assigned
- Door status: Flight crew awareness
- Multiple GSE: Sequenced to avoid conflicts

---

## 4. SAFETY REQUIREMENTS

### 4.1 Clearance Monitoring
- Visual monitors: Ground crew present
- Proximity warnings: Vehicle alarm systems
- Collision avoidance: Standardized procedures
- Training: All ground crew certified

### 4.2 Emergency Procedures
- Evacuation priority: GSE clears immediately
- Communication: Radio contact maintained
- Clear zones: Marked on ramp diagrams
