# Ground Envelope Reference
## Door L1 Forward

**Document:** Ground_Envelope.md  
**Date:** 2025-11-03  
**Status:** Reference Document

---

## Overview

This document defines the ground clearance envelope for Door L1 Forward to ensure safe operation around ground support equipment and airport infrastructure.

---

## 1. DOOR GROUND ENVELOPE

### 1.1 Door Closed Position
- Sill height above ground: 2,350-2,500mm (loaded to empty)
- Door width: 1,120mm
- Door height: 1,880mm
- Protrusion from fuselage: 0mm (flush mounted plug door)

### 1.2 Door Open Position
- Swing radius: 650mm from hinge centerline
- Maximum protrusion: 1,200mm from fuselage at full open
- Vertical clearance: Same as closed (no vertical movement)
- Clearance required: 1,500mm safety zone

---

## 2. GROUND EQUIPMENT CLEARANCE

### 2.1 Jetway/Air Stairs
- Approach clearance: 100mm minimum to fuselage
- Docking clearance: 2,000mm side clearance when door open
- Height range: 1,800-3,500mm sill height capability
- Platform width: 1,200mm minimum

### 2.2 Catering Vehicles
- Minimum clearance: 2,000mm from door opening
- Operating height: Below 3,500mm
- Parking zone: Marked on ramp, clear of door swing
- Safety barrier: Recommended for operations

### 2.3 Ground Power Unit
- Connection point: Forward fuselage, 10m from door
- Cable routing: Below door sill height
- Clear zone: GPU positioned clear of emergency egress
- Disconnect: Before passenger boarding

### 2.4 Baggage Tugs and Loaders
- Forward clearance: 5,000mm minimum (emergency egress)
- Side clearance: 3,000mm from door
- Wing clearance: No operations under wing near door
- Traffic control: Designated routes on ramp

---

## 3. AIRPORT INFRASTRUCTURE

### 3.1 Gate Positions
- Gate width: 40m minimum for BWB span
- Door to jetway: Standard connection at gate
- Multiple jetway: Possible at L1 and R1 simultaneously
- Taxiway clearance: Per airport design standards

### 3.2 Ramp Markings
- Door zone marking: Yellow safety zone 1,500mm radius
- Equipment parking: Designated zones marked
- Safety lines: 3,000mm from aircraft
- Egress path: Marked and kept clear

## 5. EMERGENCY EGRESS ENVELOPE

### 5.1 Slide Deployment
- Deployment direction: Forward and down, 45° angle
- Slide length: 8,500mm
- Slide width: 1,400mm inflated
- Clear zone: 10m radius, 45° cone

### 5.2 Ground Evacuation Path
- Path width: 3,000mm minimum
- Path length: 15,000mm to safe area
- Obstructions: None in egress path
- Lighting: Adequate for night operations

---

## 6. SERVICING ACCESS

### 6.1 Door Maintenance Access
- Platform height: Adjustable 2,200-2,600mm
- Platform width: 1,500mm
- Side access: Preferred (door opens inward initially)
- Tools: Standard maintenance platform adequate

### 6.2 Ground Test Access
- Interior access: Through door opening
- Exterior access: Maintenance platform required
- Equipment: Portable test sets, laptop computers
- Power: GPU or APU required

---

## 7. SEASONAL CONSIDERATIONS

### 7.1 Winter Operations
- Ice buildup: Check door seal area
- Snow clearance: Door zone cleared before operation
- De-icing: Door area included in aircraft de-icing
- Heating: GPU climate control for prolonged ground time

### 7.2 Hot Weather Operations
- Temperature limits: Door operation -55°C to +85°C
- Sun exposure: Check seal condition
- Ground reflection: Consider for equipment positioning

---

## 8. SPECIAL OPERATIONS

### 8.1 Towing and Pushback
- Door status: Closed and locked verified
- Clearance check: Before aircraft movement
- Safety pins: Installed if required by procedure

### 8.2 Overnight Parking
- Door security: Locked and monitored
- Weather protection: Covers if extended parking
- Inspection: Pre-flight check before operations

---

## 9. DIMENSIONAL REFERENCES

### 9.1 Key Dimensions
- Door sill to ground (empty): 2,500mm
- Door sill to ground (loaded): 2,350mm
- Door width (opening): 810mm clear
- Door height: 1,820mm clear
- Fuselage width at door: 8,500mm (BWB)

### 9.2 Clearance Zones
- Safety zone (door operation): 1,500mm radius
- Equipment clearance: 2,000mm minimum
- Egress path clearance: 3,000mm width
- Slide deployment zone: 10,000mm forward
