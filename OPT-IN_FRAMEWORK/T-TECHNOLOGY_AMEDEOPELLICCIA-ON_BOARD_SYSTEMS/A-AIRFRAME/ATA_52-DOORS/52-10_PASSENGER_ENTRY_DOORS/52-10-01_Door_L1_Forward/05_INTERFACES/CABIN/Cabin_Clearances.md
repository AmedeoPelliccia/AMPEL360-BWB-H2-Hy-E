# Cabin Clearances Reference
## Door L1 Forward

**Document:** Cabin_Clearances.md  
**Date:** 2025-11-03  
**Status:** Reference Document

---

## Overview

This document defines clearance envelopes for Door L1 Forward interface with cabin interior.

---

## 1. DOOR SWING CLEARANCES

### 1.1 Interior Opening Arc
- Swing angle: 0° to 180° (fully open)
- Clearance radius: 650mm from hinge centerline
- Vertical clearance: Door height + 50mm
- No interference zone: 10mm safety margin

### 1.2 Adjacent Panels
- Seat back clearance: 100mm minimum (door closed)
- Bulkhead clearance: 50mm minimum
- Ceiling clearance: 20mm minimum
- Floor clearance: 10mm typical

---

## 2. OPERATIONAL CLEARANCES

### 2.1 Door Operation
- Handle swing: 90° rotation, 75mm handle length
- Handle clearance required: 150mm radius
- Latch mechanism: No protrusion beyond 25mm

### 2.2 Passenger Movement
- Door opening width: 810mm clear width
- Height: 1,820mm clear height
- Threshold: Flush to ±5mm
- Step height: 50mm down to door sill

---

## 3. SERVICE ACCESS

### 3.1 Maintenance Access
- Panel removal space: 300mm in front of door
- Tool access: 200mm clearance for standard tools
- Service panel location: Door frame upper left

### 3.2 Slide Bustle Access
- Cover removal clearance: 150mm above bustle
- Slide extraction path: Straight down, 500mm
- Rigging clearance: 1,000mm workspace required

---

## 4. EMERGENCY EGRESS

### 4.1 Clear Exit Path
- Width at threshold: 810mm (Type A requirement)
- Height: 1,820mm minimum
- Path to aisle: 1,200mm clear
- No obstructions: 45° cone from door center

### 4.2 Slide Deployment
- Deployment envelope: 45° cone, 10m radius
- Ground clearance: 2,500mm door sill height
- Slide length: 8,500mm deployed
- Width: 1,400mm inflated

---

## 5. LIGHTING CLEARANCES

### 5.1 Overhead Lights
- Fixture depth: 80mm maximum recess
- Clearance to door top: 100mm
- Lens to door edge: 150mm minimum

### 5.2 Exit Sign
- Mounting height: 2,100mm from floor
- Clearance to door: 200mm above door top
- Visibility: Unobstructed from 30m

---

## 6. EQUIPMENT CLEARANCES

### 6.1 Portable Equipment
- Fire extinguisher: 300mm clearance (adjacent)
- PBE (Protective Breathing Equipment): Adjacent stowage
- Life vest stowage: Under seats, clear of door path

### 6.2 Cabin Crew Equipment
- Jump seat: Folds clear, 150mm from door swing
- Crew panel: 100mm from door edge
- Storage compartments: Clear of door operation

---

## 7. DIMENSIONAL TOLERANCES

### 7.1 Installation Tolerances
- Position: ±10mm (not cumulative with door)
- Alignment: ±5mm across interfaces
- Gap control: 2-5mm typical, 10mm maximum

### 7.2 In-Service Tolerances
- Wear allowance: +5mm from new
- Adjustment range: ±10mm for rigging
- Replacement fit: Within new limits
