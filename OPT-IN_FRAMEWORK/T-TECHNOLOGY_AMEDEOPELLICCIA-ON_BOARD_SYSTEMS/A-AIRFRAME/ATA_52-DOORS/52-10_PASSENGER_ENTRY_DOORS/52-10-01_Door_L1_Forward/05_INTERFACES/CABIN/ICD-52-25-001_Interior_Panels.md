# Interface Control Document
## Door L1 Forward Interior Panels Interface

**ICD Number:** ICD-52-25-001  
**Issue:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-03  
**Systems:** ATA 52 (Doors) ↔ ATA 25 (Cabin Equipment)

---

## 1. INTERFACE OVERVIEW

This ICD defines the interface between Door L1 Forward structure and cabin interior panels, trim, and finishes.

---

## 2. INTERIOR PANEL SPECIFICATIONS

### 2.1 Door Interior Panel
- Material: Composite sandwich (Nomex core)
- Thickness: 12mm
- Finish: Class A surface, painted to cabin color scheme
- Fire rating: FAR 25.853 compliant
- Attachment: 24 fasteners to door frame

### 2.2 Surround Trim
- Material: Thermoplastic molded trim
- Color: Match cabin interior palette
- Mounting: Snap-fit with adhesive backup
- Gap tolerance: 2mm maximum at panel edges

---

## 3. MOUNTING PROVISIONS

### 3.1 Fastener Locations

| Zone | Fasteners | Type | Torque |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| Upper | 8 | NAS1097 | 2.2 N⋅m |
| Middle | 8 | NAS1097 | 2.2 N⋅m |
| Lower | 8 | NAS1097 | 2.2 N⋅m |

### 3.2 Accessibility
- Panel removal time: <15 minutes
- Tools required: Standard screwdriver
- Service access: From cabin side only

---

## 4. CLEARANCES

### 4.1 Dimensional Clearances
- Panel to structure: 5mm minimum
- Panel to seal: 10mm minimum
- Handle protrusion: 50mm maximum from panel surface

### 4.2 Operational Clearances
- Door swing: No interference with adjacent panels
- Emergency operation: Full function with panels installed
