## 4. COMPLIANCE METHODOLOGY

### Means of Compliance (MOC)

#### Test-Based Compliance (~40%)
**CS25.305** - Static ultimate test  
**CS25.307** - Proof test  
**CS25.571** - Fatigue and damage tolerance tests  
**CS25.783** - Door operation tests  
**CS25.810** - Emergency egress demonstration

#### Analysis-Based Compliance (~30%)
**CS25.301** - Loads (supported by test)  
**CS25.365** - Pressurization (supported by test)  
**CS25.571** - Damage tolerance (validated by test)  
**CS25.629** - Flutter (validated by GVT)

#### Inspection-Based Compliance (~10%)
**CS25.603** - Materials (data review)  
**CS25.807** - Emergency exit dimensions  
**CS25.809** - Exit arrangement

#### Demonstration-Based Compliance (~15%)
**CS25.783** - Door functionality  
**CS25.810** - Emergency operation  
**CS25.1301** - System operation

#### Design Feature Compliance (~5%)
**CS25.783(e)** - Plug door design (prevents opening in flight)

## 5. ISSUE PAPERS

### Issue Paper 01: Resonance with Propulsion System
**Topic:** Door Mode 1 at ~25-30 Hz may resonate with engine frequency

**Proposed Compliance:**
- Show by GVT that damping ζ ≥ 3%
- Demonstrate no flutter up to 1.2×VD
- Provide damping enhancement if needed

**Status:** Draft prepared, submit with pre-application

### Issue Paper 02: Composite Damage Tolerance
**Topic:** Composite door skin damage tolerance beyond current AMC

**Proposed Compliance:**
- Follow AMC 20-29 for composite structures
- Conservative analysis with test validation
- Enhanced inspection program

**Status:** Draft prepared, submit with pre-application

### Issue Paper 03: Emergency Egress from BWB
**Topic:** Novel BWB configuration affects egress paths

**Proposed Compliance:**
- Demonstrate compliance with CS25.810
- Full-scale evacuation demonstration
- Flight attendant training program

**Status:** Draft prepared, submit with pre-application

### Issue Paper 04: Lightning Strike Protection
**Topic:** Composite structure requires enhanced lightning protection

**Proposed Compliance:**
- Zone 1A direct effects testing
- Conductive layer or mesh
- Follow AMC 20-136 and SAE ARP 5414

**Status:** Draft prepared, submit with pre-application
