# Life Limits

**Document:** AMPEL360-52-10-01-LIFE-LIMITS  
**Status:** PRELIMINARY  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

## Overview

Component life limits for Door L1 components.

## Preliminary Life Limits

- **Door assembly:** 60,000 flights (design life)
- **Seals:** 3,000 flights or 3 years
- **Mechanisms:** 20,000 flights
- **Actuators:** 15,000 flights (if hydraulic/electric)

## Basis

Based on fatigue testing and damage tolerance analysis.

## Status

Life limits to be validated by test and finalized before TC issuance.
