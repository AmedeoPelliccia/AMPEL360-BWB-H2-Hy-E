**Regulation:** CS25.603  
**Title:** Materials  
**Status:** Partial - Material Datasheets Available

## Requirements

Materials must be suitable for application and environment, with validated properties.

- **Composite doublers:** CFRP IM7/8552
- **Fasteners:** Titanium Ti-6Al-4V
- **Seals:** Silicone elastomer
