# CS25.783 - Doors

**Regulation:** CS25.783  
**Title:** Doors  
**Status:** Not Started - Test and Demo Required  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

## Key Requirements

(a) Reasonable probability that door is properly secured
(b) Visual means to verify door closed and locked
(c) Reasonable opening force (<50 lbs normal, <100 lbs emergency)
(d) Reasonable closing force
(e) Plug doors: cannot open in flight (design feature)
(f) No inadvertent opening

## Door L1 Design

**Type:** Plug door (outward-opening emergency, inward then outward normal)
**Interlock:** Mechanical/electrical prevents opening when pressurized
**Indication:** Visual + EICAS

## Compliance

**MOC:** Test + Demonstration

- Operation tests (normal and emergency)
- Interlock function validation
- Force measurement
- Design feature (plug type)
