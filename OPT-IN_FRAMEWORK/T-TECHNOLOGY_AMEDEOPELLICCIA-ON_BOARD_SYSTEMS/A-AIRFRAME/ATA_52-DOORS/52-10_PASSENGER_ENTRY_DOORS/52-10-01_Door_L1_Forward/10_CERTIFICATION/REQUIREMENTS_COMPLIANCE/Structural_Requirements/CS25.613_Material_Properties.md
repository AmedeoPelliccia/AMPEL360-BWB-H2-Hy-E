# CS25.613 - Material Strength Properties and Design Values

**Regulation:** CS25.613  
**Status:** Not Started - Coupon Tests Required  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

## Requirements

Material strength properties must be validated by test for design values.

## Required Tests

- Tension, compression, shear
- Various temperatures (-65°F to 180°F)
- Environmental conditioning
- Statistical basis (A-basis or B-basis)

## Status

Literature values used for concept. Coupon tests required for certification.
