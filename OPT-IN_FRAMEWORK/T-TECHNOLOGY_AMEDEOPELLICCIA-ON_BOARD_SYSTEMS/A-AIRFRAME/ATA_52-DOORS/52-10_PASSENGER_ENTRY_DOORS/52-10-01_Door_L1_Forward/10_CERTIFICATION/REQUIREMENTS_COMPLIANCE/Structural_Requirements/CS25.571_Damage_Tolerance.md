# CS25.571 - Damage Tolerance and Fatigue

**Regulation:** CS25.571  
**Title:** Damage tolerance and fatigue evaluation of structure  
**Status:** Conceptual - Requires FEA and Test  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

## Requirements

### Fatigue
Demonstrate 2× service life (120,000 cycles for 60,000 flight design goal)

### Damage Tolerance  
Show residual strength ≥ ultimate with 50mm damage at critical location

## Compliance Approach

**MOC:** Analysis + Test

### Fatigue Test
- 120,000 pressurization cycles
- Ground-air-ground spectrum
- Door operations included
- Duration: ~6 months

### Damage Tolerance Test
- 50mm through-thickness damage
- Ultimate pressure (17.0 psi)
- Residual strength validation

### Analysis
- Crack growth analysis
- Inspection intervals
- Slow crack growth validation
