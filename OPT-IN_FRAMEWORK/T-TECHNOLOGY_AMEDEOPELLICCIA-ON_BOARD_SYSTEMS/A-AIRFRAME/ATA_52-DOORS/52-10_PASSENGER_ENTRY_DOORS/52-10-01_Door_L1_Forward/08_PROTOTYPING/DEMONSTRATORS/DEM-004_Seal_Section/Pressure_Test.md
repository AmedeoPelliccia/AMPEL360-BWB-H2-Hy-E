# Pressure Test Procedure
## DEM-004 Seal Performance Validation

**Document:** DEM-004-PRESSURE-TEST  
**Status:** Procedure  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

### Test Setup

**Equipment Required:**
- Pressure chamber (0-15 psi capability)
- Pressure regulator and gauges
- Flow meter (leak detection)
- Test fixture with seal installed
- Data acquisition system

### Test Procedure

#### 1. Preparation
1.1 Install seal in test fixture  
1.2 Verify compression: 6-8mm typical  
1.3 Connect pressure lines  
1.4 Calibrate instruments  

#### 2. Initial Leak Check
2.1 Pressurize to 2 psi  
2.2 Soap bubble test around seals  
2.3 Fix any gross leaks  

#### 3. Pressure Decay Test (8.6 psi)
3.1 Pressurize chamber to 8.6 psi (normal cabin pressure)  
3.2 Close supply valve  
3.3 Monitor pressure for 30 minutes  
3.4 Record pressure vs. time  
3.5 Calculate leak rate (CFM)  

**Acceptance:** Leak rate <0.1 CFM

#### 4. Ultimate Pressure Test (12.75 psi)
4.1 Pressurize to 12.75 psi (proof pressure)  
4.2 Hold for 5 minutes  
4.3 Inspect for seal failure  
4.4 Measure leak rate  

#### 5. Temperature Extremes
5.1 Cold soak to -40°C (2 hours)  
5.2 Repeat leak test at cold  
5.3 Heat to +70°C (2 hours)  
5.4 Repeat leak test at hot

### Acceptance Criteria

- [  ] All leak rates <0.1 CFM
- [ ] No seal damage at proof pressure
- [ ] Functions at temperature extremes
- [ ] No visible deterioration
