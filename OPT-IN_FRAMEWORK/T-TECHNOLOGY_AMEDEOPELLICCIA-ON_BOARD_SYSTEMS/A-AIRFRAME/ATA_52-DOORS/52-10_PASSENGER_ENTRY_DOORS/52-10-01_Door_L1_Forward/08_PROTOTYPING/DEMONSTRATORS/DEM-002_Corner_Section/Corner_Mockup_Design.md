# Corner Mockup Design
## DEM-002 Corner Section Manufacturing Trial

**Document:** DEM-002-CORNER-DESIGN  
**Status:** Conceptual  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

### 1. OBJECTIVE

Validate manufacturing process for complex corner geometry:
- Door corner radius forming (R150mm typical)
- Edge band integration
- Ply orientation transitions
- Tool pulling and demolding

### 2. MOCKUP SPECIFICATION

**Geometry:**
- Section size: 400mm × 400mm
- Corner radius: 150mm
- Edge band width: 25mm
- Total thickness: 48mm (8-ply faces + 38mm core)

**Materials:**
- Same as full door (M21E/Nomex/EA9396)
- Representative layup sequence
- Production cure cycle

### 3. MANUFACTURING CHALLENGES

#### 3.1 Corner Radius Forming
- Minimum bend radius for 8-ply laminate
- Bridging prevention
- Wrinkle formation risk
- Fiber waviness control

#### 3.2 Edge Band Integration
- Core potting compound
- Edge band adhesion
- Dimensional tolerance
- Surface finish

#### 3.3 Ply Transitions
- Staggered ply drops
- Interleaving requirements
- Overlap distances
- Resin-rich areas

### 4. TOOLING CONCEPT

**Mold Configuration:**
- Female corner mold
- Aluminum or steel construction
- Release agent: Frekote 700-NC
- Vacuum bag consumables

**Caul Plates:**
- Corner radius caul plate
- Pressure distribution
- Temperature monitoring

### 5. LAYUP PROCEDURE DEVELOPMENT

**Key Steps:**
1. Tool preparation
2. First face layup (flat area first)
3. Corner radius forming (critical)
4. Edge band placement
5. Core positioning
6. Second face layup
7. Vacuum bagging
8. Autoclave cure

**Photo Documentation:**
- Each ply placement
- Corner forming technique
- Edge band fit-up
- Bagging sequence

### 6. QUALITY ACCEPTANCE

**Visual Inspection:**
- No wrinkles in corner radius
- No bridging over core
- Smooth edge band transition
- Surface finish quality

**Dimensional Check:**
- Corner radius: 150±2mm
- Thickness: 48±1mm
- Width/length: ±2mm
- Flatness: <1mm deviation

**NDT:**
- Ultrasonic C-scan
- Void content <5%
- Bond line quality

### 7. LESSONS TO CAPTURE

- Corner forming techniques
- Debulking requirements
- Ply cutting templates
- Edge band procedures
- Tooling improvements

### 8. COST & SCHEDULE

**Budget:** $15,000
**Duration:** 6 weeks
