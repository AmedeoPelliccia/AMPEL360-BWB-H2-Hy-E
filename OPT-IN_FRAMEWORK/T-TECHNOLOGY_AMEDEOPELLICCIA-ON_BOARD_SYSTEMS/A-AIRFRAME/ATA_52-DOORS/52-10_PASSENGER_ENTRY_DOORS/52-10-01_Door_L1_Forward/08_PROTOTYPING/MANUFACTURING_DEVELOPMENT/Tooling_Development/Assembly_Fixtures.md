# Assembly Fixtures Specification
## Door Assembly Tooling

**Document:** TOOL-DEV-ASSY-001  
**Status:** Conceptual  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

### Required Fixtures

#### 1. Frame Alignment Jig
**Purpose:** Position frame to panel for bonding

**Features:**
- Locating pins (referenced to panel holes)
- Adjustable supports
- Access for bonding operations
- Curing capability (oven compatible)

**Cost:** $30,000

#### 2. Latch Drilling Template
**Purpose:** Precision hole drilling for latch mechanisms

**Features:**
- Hard anodized aluminum
- Drill bushings (6 latch locations)
- Reference to panel edges
- Clamp provisions

**Cost:** $15,000

#### 3. Hinge Installation Fixture
**Purpose:** Align and hold hinges during installation

**Features:**
- Hinge pin alignment
- Adjustable for tolerance
- Torque reaction
- Inspection features

**Cost:** $20,000

#### 4. Panel Handling Fixture
**Purpose:** Safe handling and transport

**Features:**
- Vacuum cups or soft jaws
- Rotation capability
- Transport cart
- Storage cradle

**Cost:** $10,000
