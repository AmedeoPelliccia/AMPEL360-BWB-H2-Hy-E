# Bonding Process Specification
## Secondary Bonding Operations

**Document:** MFG-DEV-BOND-001  
**Status:** Development  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

### Applications

1. **Frame-to-panel bonding**
2. **Insert installation**
3. **Edge band bonding**
4. **Repair patches**

### Adhesive System

**Primary:** EA9396 film adhesive
- Cure: 120°C for 60 minutes
- Pressure: 2-4 bar
- Service temp: -55°C to +120°C

### Surface Preparation

1. **Abrasion:** 180-grit paper or Scotch-Brite
2. **Cleaning:** MEK or IPA wipe (2x)
3. **Grit blast:** Optional for metals (120 grit aluminum oxide)
4. **Primer:** BR127 if required
5. **Time limit:** Bond within 24 hours of prep

### Bonding Procedure

1. Apply adhesive film
2. Position parts (within tolerance)
3. Apply vacuum bag or pressure
4. Cure per cycle (oven or autoclave)
5. Inspect bond line

### Quality Control

- **Bond line thickness:** 0.1-0.3mm
- **Inspection:** Tap test, ultrasonic
- **Witness coupons:** Pull test (>25 MPa)
