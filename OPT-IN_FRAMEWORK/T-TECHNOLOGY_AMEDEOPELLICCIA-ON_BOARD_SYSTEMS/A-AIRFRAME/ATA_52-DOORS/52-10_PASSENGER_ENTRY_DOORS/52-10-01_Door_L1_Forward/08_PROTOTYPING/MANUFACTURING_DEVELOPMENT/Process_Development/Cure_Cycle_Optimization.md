### Baseline Cure Cycle

**M21E Prepreg + EA9396 Adhesive**

```
Temperature Profile:
0-60 min:   23°C → 120°C (2°C/min ramp)
60-120 min: 120°C hold (adhesive gel)
120-180 min: 120°C → 180°C (2°C/min ramp)
180-300 min: 180°C hold (prepreg cure)
300-390 min: 180°C → 80°C (2°C/min cooldown)

Pressure Profile:
0-150 min: Vacuum only
150-300 min: 7 bar (100 psi)
300-390 min: Release at 80°C
```

### Optimization Parameters

**Variables to study:**
1. Ramp rates (1-3°C/min)
2. Hold times (90-150 min)
3. Pressure application point (140-160°C)
4. Vacuum vs. pressure transitions

**Objectives:**
- Minimize cycle time
- Minimize void content
- Maximize mechanical properties
- Reduce energy cost

### Instrumentation
