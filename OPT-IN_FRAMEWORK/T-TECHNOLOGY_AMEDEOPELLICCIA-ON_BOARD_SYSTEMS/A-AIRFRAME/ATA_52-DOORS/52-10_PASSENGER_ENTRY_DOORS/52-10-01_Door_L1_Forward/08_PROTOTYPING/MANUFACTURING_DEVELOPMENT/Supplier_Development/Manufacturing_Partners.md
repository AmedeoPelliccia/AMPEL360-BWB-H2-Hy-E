# Manufacturing Partners
## Supplier Development Strategy

**Document:** SUPP-DEV-PARTNERS-001  
**Status:** Planning  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

### Partner Categories

#### 1. Material Suppliers
**Primary need:** CFRP prepreg, honeycomb core, adhesives

**Candidates:**
- **Hexcel** - Prepreg (M21E system)
- **DuPont** - Nomex core
- **Henkel** - Structural adhesives

**Selection criteria:**
- Aerospace certification
- Technical support
- Delivery reliability
- Cost competitiveness

#### 2. Manufacturing Services
**Primary need:** Autoclave, machining, NDT

**Candidates:**
- Local composite shops (autoclave time)
- CNC machining shops
- NDT service providers

**Requirements:**
- AS9100 certified (preferred)
- Experience with aerospace composites
- Capacity availability
- Quality system

#### 3. Mechanism Suppliers
**Primary need:** Latches, hinges, seals

**Candidates:**
- Latch manufacturers (Hartwell, Adams Rite)
- Hinge suppliers (aerospace qualified)
- Seal manufacturers (Parker, Hutchinson)

**Requirements:**
- FAA/EASA approved
- Customization capability
- Certification support
- Spare parts availability

### Partnership Approach

**Phase 1: Prototype (Current)**
- Buy standard materials
- Use job shop services
- Minimal commitments
- Flexibility for changes

**Phase 2: Qualification**
- Supplier agreements
- Quality system audits
- Technical collaboration
- Long-term pricing

**Phase 3: Production**
- Supply agreements
- Dedicated capacity
- Joint development
- Cost reduction programs

### Supplier Qualification

**Process:**
1. Technical capability review
2. Quality system audit
3. Sample orders
4. Performance evaluation
5. Approved supplier list (ASL)

**Documentation:**
- Supplier questionnaire
- Audit reports
- Sample test results
- Approval certificate

### Risk Mitigation

**Single-source risks:**
- Identify alternate suppliers
- Qualify two sources where possible
- Maintain safety stock
- Escrow critical specifications
