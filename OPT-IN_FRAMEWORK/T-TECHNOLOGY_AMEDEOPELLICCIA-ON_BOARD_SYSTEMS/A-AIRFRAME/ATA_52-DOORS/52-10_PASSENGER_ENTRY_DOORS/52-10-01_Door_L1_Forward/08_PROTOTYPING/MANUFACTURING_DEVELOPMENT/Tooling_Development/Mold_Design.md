# Cure Mold Design Specification
## Primary Tooling Development

**Document:** TOOL-DEV-MOLD-001  
**Status:** Conceptual  
**Date:** 2025-11-03

### Mold Requirements

**Type:** Female mold (door outer surface)  
**Material:** Invar or tool steel  
**Surface area:** 400mm × 2000mm × 1200mm envelope

### Design Specifications

**Geometry:**
- CNC machined from solid block
- Tolerances: ±0.5mm on contoured surfaces
- Surface finish: 400 grit (smooth)
- Corner radii: 150mm typical

**Features:**
- Vacuum ports (grid pattern)
- Thermocouple penetrations (8 locations)
- Lifting points (4 corners)
- Alignment pins/references

**Material Properties:**
- CTE matched to part (Invar preferred)
- Autoclave compatible (250°C max)
- Corrosion resistant
- Thermal mass (for uniform heating)

- Finishing: $25,000
