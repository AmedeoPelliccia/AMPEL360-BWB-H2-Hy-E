### Scoring System

**Excellent (5):** Exceeds requirements  
**Good (4):** Meets all requirements  
**Acceptable (3):** Meets minimum requirements  
**Marginal (2):** Deficiencies noted  
**Unacceptable (1):** Does not meet requirements

**Minimum Score:** 3.5/5.0 weighted average
