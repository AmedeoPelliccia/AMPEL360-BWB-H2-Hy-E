## Recovery Process Flow

### Phase 1: Preparation (Week 1)
1. **Documentation Review**
   - Access Digital Passport (DP-CMP-52-10-01-001)
   - Review maintenance history
   - Check for hazardous materials
   - Verify material composition

2. **Disassembly Planning**
   - Generate AR disassembly guide
   - Assign certified technicians
   - Prepare tools and containers
   - Schedule recycling logistics

### Phase 2: Disassembly (Week 2)
1. **Electronics Removal** (4 hours)
   - Disconnect all electrical connections
   - Remove sensor network (12 units)
   - Extract control electronics
   - Package for urban mining facility

2. **Mechanical Components** (8 hours)
   - Remove latch system
   - Extract actuators
   - Detach hinges and brackets
   - Separate for remanufacturing assessment

3. **Panel Separation** (16 hours)
   - Debond face sheets from core
   - Separate CFRP layers
   - Extract titanium inserts
   - Remove seal materials

### Phase 3: Material Processing (Weeks 3-6)

#### CFRP Pyrolysis
- **Facility**: CompositeRecycle GmbH, Bremen
- **Process**: 
  1. Pre-shred to 50mm pieces
  2. Pyrolysis at 550°C for 4 hours
  3. Fiber recovery and cleaning
  4. Quality testing
- **Output**: 43.8 kg recovered carbon fiber
- **Quality**: 85-90% virgin strength
- **Revenue**: €1,533

#### Metal Recycling
- **Titanium**:
  - Facility: TitanWerk AG, Munich
  - Process: VAR remelting
  - Output: 8.4 kg ingots
  - Revenue: €378

- **Aluminum**:
  - Facility: Local aluminum recycler
  - Process: Remelt and casting
  - Output: 42.0 kg ingots
  - Revenue: €105

#### Nomex Chemical Recycling
- **Facility**: AramidRecovery SA, Lyon
- **Process**: Solvolysis
- **Output**: 2.4 kg aramid monomers
- **Revenue**: €43.20

#### Electronics Urban Mining
- **Facility**: TechRecycle Europe, Amsterdam
- **Process**: Specialized e-waste processing
- **Output**: Precious metals extraction
- **Revenue**: €1,440

### Phase 4: Quality Assurance (Week 7)
- Material testing and certification
- Digital passport updates
- Blockchain registration
- Customer notification

### Phase 5: Distribution (Week 8)
- Package recovered materials
- Ship to approved buyers
- Update asset registry
- Generate recovery certificates

## Risk Management

### Identified Risks
1. **Contamination**: CFRP contaminated with sealants
   - Mitigation: Careful manual cleaning
   
2. **Material Degradation**: UV or moisture damage
   - Mitigation: Pre-assessment, adjust recovery targets
   
3. **Transport Delays**: Logistics disruptions
   - Mitigation: Multiple approved carriers
   
4. **Market Volatility**: Recycled material prices
   - Mitigation: Long-term contracts with recyclers
