# Safety Analysis - Nose Cone (53-10-01)

## Overview
This directory contains safety analysis documents for the nose cone component, including failure mode analysis, fault tree analysis, and specific hazard assessments.

## Documents
- **FMEA.md** - Failure Mode and Effects Analysis
- **FTA.md** - Fault Tree Analysis
- **bird_strike_analysis.md** - CS-25.631 compliance analysis
- **lightning_zone_1a.md** - Lightning protection (Zone 1A)
- **radome_protection.md** - Weather radar radome design and protection

## Safety Summary
The nose cone has been designed to meet all applicable safety requirements:
- Bird strike: 4 lb at 250 knots (CS-25.631)
- Lightning: Zone 1A protection, 200 kA peak
- Hail impact: 25mm at 150 knots
- Fatigue life: 60,000 flight cycles

All identified hazards have been mitigated to acceptable risk levels.
