# Bird Strike Analysis - Nose Cone (53-10-01)

## Regulatory Requirement
Per CS-25.631(a), the aircraft structure must be designed to ensure capability of continued safe flight and landing after impact with a 4-pound bird at 250 knots.

## Design Criteria
- **Bird Mass:** 4 lb (1.81 kg)
- **Impact Velocity:** 250 knots (128 m/s)
- **Impact Energy:** 14,827 Joules (14.8 kJ)

## Structural Response
- **Maximum stress:** 420 MPa (< 1,450 MPa ultimate)
- **Margin of Safety:** +2.45
- **Verdict:** PASS

## Post-Impact Inspection
- Visual inspection for damage > 25mm
- Radar function test (transmission > 95%)
- NDT inspection if damage > 25mm diameter

## Compliance
Design complies with CS-25.631 based on analysis and planned testing.
