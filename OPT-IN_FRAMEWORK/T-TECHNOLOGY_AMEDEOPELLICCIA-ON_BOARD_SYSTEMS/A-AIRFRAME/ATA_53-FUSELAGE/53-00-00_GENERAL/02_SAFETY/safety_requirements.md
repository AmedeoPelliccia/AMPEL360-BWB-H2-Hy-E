# Safety Requirements - ATA 53 Fuselage

## Document Information
- **Document ID:** SR-53-00-00
- **Revision:** A
- **Date:** 2025-11-03

## General Safety Requirements

### SR-53-001: Structural Integrity
The fuselage structure shall maintain integrity under all design load conditions per CS-25.301-25.307.

**Verification:** Analysis + Static Test + Fatigue Test

### SR-53-002: Damage Tolerance
The fuselage structure shall comply with damage tolerance requirements per CS-25.571.

**Verification:** Analysis + Test

### SR-53-003: Cabin Pressure
The fuselage shall maintain cabin pressure of 8.5 psi differential with leak rate < 0.5 CFM total.

**Verification:** Pressure Test

### SR-53-004: Emergency Egress
The fuselage shall provide emergency exits per CS-25.807 enabling 300 passenger evacuation in < 90 seconds.

**Verification:** Evacuation Demonstration

### SR-53-005: Fire Protection
Fuselage materials and systems shall meet fire protection requirements per CS-25.853-25.869.

**Verification:** Material Testing + Analysis

### SR-53-006: Crashworthiness
The fuselage shall provide occupant protection per CS-25.561-25.562 in emergency landing.

**Verification:** Drop Test + Analysis

### SR-53-007: Lightning Protection
The fuselage shall provide lightning protection per CS-25.581, AC 20-136A.

**Verification:** Test + Analysis

### SR-53-008: H2 System Safety
The H2 tank integration shall prevent hydrogen leakage, accumulation, and ignition per SAE AIR7898.

**Verification:** Analysis + Test + Inspection

### SR-53-009: Fail-Safe Design
Critical structural elements shall incorporate fail-safe features preventing single-point catastrophic failure.

**Verification:** Analysis + Test

### SR-53-010: Inspection Access
The fuselage structure shall provide inspection access per AMM requirements.

**Verification:** Mock-up + Demonstration
