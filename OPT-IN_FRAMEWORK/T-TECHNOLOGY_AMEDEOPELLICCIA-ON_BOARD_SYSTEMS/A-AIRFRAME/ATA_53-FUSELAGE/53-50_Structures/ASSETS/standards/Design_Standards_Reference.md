# Design Standards Reference

## Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive reference to design standards, specifications, and guidelines applicable to AMPEL360 BWB fuselage structure.

## Regulatory Standards

### EASA Certification Specifications
- [CS-25](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27) - Large Aeroplanes
  - CS-25.301 - Loads
  - CS-25.303 - Factor of Safety
  - CS-25.305 - Strength and Deformation
  - CS-25.571 - Damage Tolerance and Fatigue
  - CS-25.603 - Materials
  - CS-25.605 - Fabrication Methods
  - CS-25.613 - Material Strength Properties

### FAA Regulations
- 14 CFR Part 25 - Airworthiness Standards: Transport Category Airplanes
