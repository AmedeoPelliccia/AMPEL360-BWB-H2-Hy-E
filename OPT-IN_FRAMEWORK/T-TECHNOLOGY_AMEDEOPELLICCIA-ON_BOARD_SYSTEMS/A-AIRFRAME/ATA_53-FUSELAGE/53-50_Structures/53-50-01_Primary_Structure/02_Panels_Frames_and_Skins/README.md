## Design Requirements

### Skin Panels
- Withstand aerodynamic pressure distribution
- Resist bending and shear loads
- Maintain aerodynamic surface tolerances
- Provide lightning strike protection
- Enable efficient manufacturing and assembly

### Frames
- Maintain fuselage cross-sectional shape
- Resist hoop tension from pressurization
- Distribute concentrated loads
- Provide attachment for systems and equipment
- Enable access for inspection and maintenance

### Stringers
- Stiffen skins against buckling
- Carry axial loads (bending and pressure)
- Efficient load path for distributed loads

### Pressure Bulkheads
- Contain cabin pressure at fuselage ends
- Efficient dome or hemispherical design
- Integration with floor structure and systems
