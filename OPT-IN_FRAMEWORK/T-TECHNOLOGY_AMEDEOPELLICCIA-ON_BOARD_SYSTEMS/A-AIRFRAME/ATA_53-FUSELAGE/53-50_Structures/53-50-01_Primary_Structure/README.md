# 53-50-01 Primary Structure

## Overview

Primary structure refers to the main load-bearing structural components of the AMPEL360 BWB fuselage that are essential for maintaining structural integrity and transferring flight loads. These components form the primary load paths and are critical for airworthiness.

## Purpose

This section documents the design, analysis, testing, and certification of primary structural elements including:

- Skin panels and shells
- Frames and stringers
- Pressure bulkheads
- Major structural joints and splices
- Wing, landing gear, and tail attachment fittings

## Scope

The primary structure documentation covers:

1. **Design Philosophy** - Load path concepts, material selection, design allowables
2. **Structural Components** - Detailed design of skins, frames, stringers, and bulkheads
3. **Joints and Connections** - Splice designs, cutout reinforcements, and penetrations
4. **Primary Attachments** - Wing-body, landing gear, and empennage attachment designs
5. **Margins and Analysis** - Stress and buckling margin summaries, critical parts tracking

## BWB-Specific Considerations

The Blended Wing Body configuration presents unique structural challenges:

- **Large uninterrupted pressure vessel** - Wider fuselage cross-section requiring efficient pressure containment
- **Distributed loading** - Wing and fuselage loads integrated across the blended structure
- **Unconventional load paths** - Load transfer through curved, blended surfaces
- **Hydrogen system integration** - Structural provisions for H₂ tanks and distribution

## Regulatory Framework

Primary structure design and certification comply with:

- [CS-25.301](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27) - Loads

- [CS-25.305](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27) - Strength and Deformation
- [CS-25.571](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27) - Damage Tolerance and Fatigue Evaluation
- [CS-25.603](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-amendment-27) - Materials
