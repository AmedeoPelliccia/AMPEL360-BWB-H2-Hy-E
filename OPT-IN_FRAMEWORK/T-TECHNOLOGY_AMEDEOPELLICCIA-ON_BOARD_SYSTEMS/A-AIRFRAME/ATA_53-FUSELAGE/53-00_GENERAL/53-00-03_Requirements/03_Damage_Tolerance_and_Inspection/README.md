# 03_Damage_Tolerance_and_Inspection

## Overview

This folder contains requirements related to damage tolerance, crack growth prediction, crack arrest features, and inspection requirements. These requirements ensure that the fuselage structure can sustain damage and remain safe until detection and repair.
