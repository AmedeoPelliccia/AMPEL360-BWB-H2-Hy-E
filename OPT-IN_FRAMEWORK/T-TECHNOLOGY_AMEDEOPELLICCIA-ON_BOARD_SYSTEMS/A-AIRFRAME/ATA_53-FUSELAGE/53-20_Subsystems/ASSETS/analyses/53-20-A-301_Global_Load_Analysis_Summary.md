# 53-20-A-301 Global Load Analysis Summary

## Purpose

Summarizes global structural loads applied to ATA 53-20 Subsystems for design and analysis.

## Load Categories

### Flight Loads
- **Maneuver Loads**: -1.0g to +2.5g per [CS-25.337](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes)
- **Gust Loads**: 50 ft/s vertical gust per [CS-25.341](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes)
- **Aerodynamic Loads**: Distributed pressure from CFD analysis

### Cabin Pressure Loads
- **Design Differential**: 8.6 psi (59.3 kPa)
- **Ultimate Differential**: 12.9 psi (88.9 kPa), 1.5Ã— safety factor

### Emergency Landing Loads
- **Forward**: 9.0g ultimate per [CS-25.561](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes)
- **Vertical**: 3.0g ultimate
- **Lateral**: 3.0g ultimate

### Landing Loads
- **Main Gear**: Limit load factor 3.0, ultimate 4.5
- **Nose Gear**: Limit load factor 2.0, ultimate 3.0
- **Reactions**: Per [CS-25.473](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes) through CS-25.479

## Critical Load Cases

1. **2.5g Maneuver + Pressure**: Upper shell maximum stress
2. **Landing Impact**: Lower shell and landing gear bay maximum stress
3. **Emergency Landing**: Floor beam and seat track maximum stress
4. **Gust + Pressure**: Wing-body blend maximum stress
