# 53-20-A-204 Composite Layup Standards

## Purpose

Defines composite layup methods, quality standards, and cure cycles for ATA 53-20 components.

## Layup Methods

### Automated Fiber Placement (AFP)
- **Speed**: 300-500 mm/s placement rate
- **Width**: 6.35mm (1/4 inch) tow width
- **Application**: Large flat/curved panels

### Hand Layup
- **Application**: Complex geometry, tight radii, repairs
- **Quality**: Visual inspection, ply orientation verification

## Quality Standards

### Void Content
- **Maximum**: 2% by volume (ultrasonic inspection)

### Fiber Volume Fraction
- **Target**: 60% ± 3%

### Cure Cycles
- **IM7/8552**: 177°C for 120 minutes at 6.9 bar
- **T800/M21**: 180°C for 120 minutes at 7.0 bar
