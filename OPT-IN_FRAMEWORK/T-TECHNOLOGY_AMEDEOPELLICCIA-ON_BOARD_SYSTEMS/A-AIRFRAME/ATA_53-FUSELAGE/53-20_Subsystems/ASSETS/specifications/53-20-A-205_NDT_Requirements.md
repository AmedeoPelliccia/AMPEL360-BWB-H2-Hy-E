# 53-20-A-205 NDT Requirements

## Purpose

Defines non-destructive testing (NDT) methods, frequencies, and acceptance criteria for ATA 53-20 components.

## NDT Methods

### Ultrasonic Inspection
- **Application**: Composite skin-to-core bond integrity, delamination detection
- **Frequency**: Every 4,000-8,000 flight hours
- **Acceptance**: No indications >25mm diameter

### Eddy Current Inspection
- **Application**: Metallic fastener holes, crack detection
- **Frequency**: Every 6,000 flight hours
- **Acceptance**: No crack indications

### Radiographic (X-Ray) Inspection
- **Application**: Impact damage assessment, void detection
- **Frequency**: On-condition (after suspected impact)
- **Acceptance**: Per engineering disposition

### Thermography
- **Application**: Bondline integrity, moisture detection
- **Frequency**: Every 8,000 flight hours
- **Acceptance**: No anomalies >50mm diameter

## Inspection Standards

All NDT must comply with:
- [CS-25.571](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/certification-specifications/cs-25-large-aeroplanes) - Damage tolerance requirements
- Approved NDT procedures manual
- Qualified NDT personnel (Level II minimum)
