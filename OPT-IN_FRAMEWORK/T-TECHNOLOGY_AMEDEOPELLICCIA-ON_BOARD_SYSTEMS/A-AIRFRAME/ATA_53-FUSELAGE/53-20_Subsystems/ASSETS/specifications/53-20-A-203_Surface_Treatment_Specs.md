# 53-20-A-203 Surface Treatment Specifications

## Purpose

Defines surface preparation and treatment requirements for bonding, sealing, and corrosion protection.

## Composite Surface Preparation

### Pre-Bond Surface Prep
1. Peel ply removal
2. Light abrasion (180-220 grit)
3. Solvent wipe (MEK or acetone)
4. Bond within 4 hours

### Lightning Strike Protection
- Expanded copper mesh outer ply
- Aluminum foil interleaving
- Conductive paint application

## Metallic Surface Treatment

### Aluminum Anodizing
- Type II anodizing per MIL-A-8625
- Chromate conversion coating (if anodizing not feasible)

### Titanium Surface Prep
- Grit blast + passivation
- Chemical milling where applicable
