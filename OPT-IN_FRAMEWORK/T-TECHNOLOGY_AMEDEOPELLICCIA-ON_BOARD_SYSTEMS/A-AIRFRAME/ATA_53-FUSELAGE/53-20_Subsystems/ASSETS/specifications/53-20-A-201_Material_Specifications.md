# 53-20-A-201 Material Specifications

## Purpose

Defines approved materials for ATA 53-20 Subsystems including composites, metals, fasteners, and sealants.

## Composite Materials

### Carbon Fiber Prepregs
- **IM7/8552**: Intermediate modulus carbon fiber, 350°F cure epoxy
  - Application: Primary structure, stringers, frames
  - Specification: AMS 3894
  
- **T800/M21**: High strength carbon fiber, toughened epoxy
  - Application: Impact-critical areas, lower shell
  - Specification: AMS 3905

### Core Materials
- **Aluminum Honeycomb**: 5052 alloy, 3.1 lb/ft³ density
  - Specification: AMS 4347
  
## Metallic Materials

### Aluminum Alloys
- **7075-T7351**: High strength, stress-relieved
  - Application: Frames, fittings, floor beams
  - Specification: AMS 4078

### Titanium Alloys
- **Ti-6Al-4V**: Alpha-beta titanium
  - Application: Landing gear fittings, critical joints
  - Specification: AMS 4911

## Fasteners

- **Hi-Lok**: Titanium collar, interference fit
  - Specification: NAS3354 through NAS3358 series
  
## Sealants

- **PR-1776**: Class B polysulfide pressure sealant
- **PR-1422**: Class B fay seal
