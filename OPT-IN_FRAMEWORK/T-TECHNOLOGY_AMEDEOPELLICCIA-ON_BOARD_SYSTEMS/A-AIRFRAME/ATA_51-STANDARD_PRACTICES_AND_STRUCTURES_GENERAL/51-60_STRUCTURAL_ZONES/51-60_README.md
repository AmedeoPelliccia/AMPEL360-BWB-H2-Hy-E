# 51-60: Structural Zones

## Overview
ATA 51-60 defines the structural zone breakdown for the AMPEL360 aircraft, following ATA zone conventions for inspection and maintenance management.

## Components
- **51-60-10:** Zone 100-Series (Forward Body)
- **51-60-20:** Zone 200-Series (Center Body)
- **51-60-30:** Zone 300-Series (Aft Body)
- **51-60-40:** Zone 400-Series (Left Wing)
- **51-60-50:** Zone 500-Series (Right Wing)
- **51-60-60:** Zone 600-Series (Vertical Stabilizers)
- **51-60-70:** Zone 700-Series (Landing Gear Attachments)
- **51-60-80:** Zone 800-Series (Access and Servicing)

## Zone 220 (Critical LH₂ Tank Zone)
- **Inspection Interval:** 600 FH (vs 1,200 FH standard)
- **SHM Sensor Density:** 2× (500mm vs 1,000mm spacing)
- **NDT:** Annual ultrasonic C-scan
- **Structural Margin:** 1.5× standard
