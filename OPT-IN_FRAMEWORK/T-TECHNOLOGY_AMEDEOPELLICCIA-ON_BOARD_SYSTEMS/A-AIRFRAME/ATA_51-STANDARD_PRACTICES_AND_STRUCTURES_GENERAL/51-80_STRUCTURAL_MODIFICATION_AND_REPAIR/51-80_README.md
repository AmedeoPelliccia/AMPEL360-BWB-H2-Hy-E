# 51-80: Structural Modification and Repair Approval

## Overview
ATA 51-80 establishes the engineering disposition process for structural modifications and repairs, including classification levels, approval requirements, and Service Twin validation.

## Components
- **51-80-10:** Modification Classification (Levels 1-4)
- **51-80-20:** Engineering Disposition Procedures
- **51-80-30:** Service Twin Simulation
- **51-80-40:** Structural Repair Manual (SRM)
- **51-80-50:** Non-Standard Repair Procedures
- **51-80-60:** Type Certificate Amendment Process

## Classification Levels
**Level 1:** Minor (no engineering) - rivet replacement, paint
**Level 2:** Standard (SRM procedure) - composite patch repair
**Level 3:** Major (engineering disposition) - non-standard repair
**Level 4:** Design change (TC amendment) - structural redesign

## Service Twin Role
- Simulate stress redistribution before approval
- 10,000 flight simulations with modified structure
- Verify safety margin >0% at ultimate load
