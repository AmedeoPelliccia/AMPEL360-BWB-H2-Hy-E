# 51-30: Composite Materials

## Overview
ATA 51-30 specifies all composite materials used in the AMPEL360 primary structure, including carbon fiber prepregs, honeycomb cores, structural adhesives, coatings, and sealants.

## Components
- **51-30-10:** Carbon Fiber Prepreg (Hexcel M21E/IM7, M21E/HM63)
- **51-30-20:** Honeycomb Core (Nomex HRH-10, HRH-78)
- **51-30-30:** Structural Adhesives (Cytec FM 300, Hysol EA 9396)
- **51-30-40:** Surface Coatings (Primers, Topcoats)
- **51-30-50:** Sealants (Fuel tank, aerodynamic)

## Primary Materials
**M21E/IM7:** Standard primary structure (moderate stiffness, high strength)
**M21E/HM63:** High-load areas (high stiffness)
**M21E Resin:** Toughened epoxy for enhanced impact resistance

## Laminate Design
- Thickness: 10-20mm primary structure (4-6mm typical)
- Layup: Quasi-isotropic [0/±45/90]s
- Cold-zone: Enhanced M21E with more ±45° plies
