This is ATA Chapter 46: INFORMATION SYSTEMS DATALINK, part of the T — TECHNOLOGY AMEDEOPELLICCIA — ON BOARD SYSTEMS axis.

This chapter follows the mandatory OPT-IN Framework structure:

7. **46-00-07_V_AND_V**: Verification and validation
8. **46-00-08_Prototyping**: Prototype development
9. **46-00-09_Production_Planning**: Manufacturing planning
10. **46-00-10_Certification**: Certification evidence
