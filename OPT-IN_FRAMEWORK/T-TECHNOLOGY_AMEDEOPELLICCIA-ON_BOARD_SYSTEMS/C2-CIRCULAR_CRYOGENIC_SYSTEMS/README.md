# C2 - CIRCULAR, CRYOGENIC SYSTEMS

**Framework**: OPT-IN AMEDEOPELLICCIA  
**Axis**: T-TECHNOLOGY  
**Domain**: C2-CIRCULAR_CRYOGENIC_SYSTEMS  
**Aircraft Platform**: AMPEL360 BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA

## Overview

The C2-CIRCULAR_CRYOGENIC_SYSTEMS domain encompasses fuel systems with emphasis on sustainable aviation fuel (SAF), cryogenic hydrogen storage, and circular economy systems including CO₂ capture.

## Scope

Circular and cryogenic systems include:
- **Cryogenic Hydrogen**: LH₂ storage and handling
- **Fuel Systems**: Fuel storage, distribution, and management
- **Sustainable Aviation Fuel**: SAF integration
- **CO₂ Capture**: Carbon capture and storage systems
- **Thermal Management**: Cryogenic temperature control

## ATA Chapters in C2-CIRCULAR_CRYOGENIC_SYSTEMS

  - Liquid hydrogen (LH₂) storage tanks

- **ATA 21-80**: CO₂ CAPTURE SYSTEM (Provisional)

## AMPEL360 Specific Considerations

The hydrogen-electric BWB configuration requires:
- Advanced cryogenic storage at -253°C
- Multi-wall vacuum-insulated tanks
- Boil-off gas management and utilization
- Safety systems for hydrogen handling
- Integration with fuel cell power generation
- Zero-emission propulsion architecture
- Potential CO₂ capture for hybrid operations

## Key Performance Indicators

- **Storage Efficiency**: >70% by volume
- **Boil-off Rate**: <0.3% per day
- **Fuel Cell Integration**: Seamless hydrogen delivery
- **Safety**: Triple-redundant monitoring
