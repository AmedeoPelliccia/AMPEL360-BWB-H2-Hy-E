# 51-90: Fatigue and Durability

## Overview
ATA 51-90 addresses composite fatigue characteristics and durability analysis for the AMPEL360 structure, with design philosophy targeting infinite fatigue life through low stress operation.

## Components
- **51-90-10:** Composite Fatigue Mechanisms
- **51-90-20:** S-N Curves (Stress vs Cycles to Failure)
- **51-90-30:** Rainflow Cycle Counting
- **51-90-40:** Environmental Degradation
- **51-90-50:** Full-Scale Fatigue Test Program
- **51-90-60:** Durability Test Results

## Fatigue Philosophy
- Design Stress: <40% UTS for all primary structure
- Result: Infinite fatigue life (no retirement for fatigue)
- Verification: 120,000 simulated flights (2× design life)

## Damage Mechanisms
1. Matrix Microcracking (~10,000 cycles at 50% UTS)
2. Delamination (~50,000 cycles)
3. Fiber Breakage (>1,000,000 cycles at 40% UTS)

## Environmental Degradation
- Moisture: 1.5% absorption (reduces strength 10%)
- Hot/Wet: -55°C to +82°C, 85% humidity
- Design Allowable: Reduced 15% for environmental knockdown
