# 51-20: Damage Tolerance

## Overview
ATA 51-20 establishes the damage tolerance philosophy and analysis methods for the AMPEL360 composite airframe, ensuring structural integrity throughout the service life through a three-tier approach: design for impact resistance, SHM monitoring, and scheduled inspections.

## Components
- **51-20-10:** Damage Tolerance Philosophy
- **51-20-20:** Impact Damage Scenarios
- **51-20-30:** Residual Strength Analysis
- **51-20-40:** Damage Growth Prediction Models
- **51-20-50:** Inspection Threshold Criteria

## Three-Tier Approach
**Tier 1 - Design:** Toughened matrix, thick laminates, sandwich construction
**Tier 2 - SHM:** Real-time impact detection and damage classification
**Tier 3 - Inspection:** NDT validation and repair approval

## Design Ultimate Load (DUL) Philosophy
- **NDD:** Structure sustains limit load with no detectable damage
- **BVID:** Structure sustains ultimate load with 25J impact (200J large aircraft)
- **DD:** Structure sustains limit load with visible damage until next inspection
