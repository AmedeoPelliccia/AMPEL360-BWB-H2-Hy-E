## Design Principles
1. **Weight Efficiency:** Minimize structural weight while meeting strength requirements
2. **Damage Tolerance:** Design for detectability and residual strength
3. **Maintainability:** Design for accessibility and repairability
4. **Manufacturability:** Design for efficient production
5. **Inspectability:** Design for SHM coverage and NDT access
6. **Redundancy:** Multiple load paths for fail-safe design
